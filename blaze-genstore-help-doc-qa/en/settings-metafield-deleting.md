# Delete metafields

If you no longer need a metafield, you can remove its definition from your Genstore admin. Once deleted, the field will no longer appear in any product, variant, or customer detail pages.

## Deletion options

When deleting a metafield definition, you can choose from two options:

- **Delete field only** – Removes the field definition, but keeps all previously entered values in your data (hidden but not deleted)
- **Delete field and its saved values** – Completely removes the field definition and permanently deletes all associated values from products, variants, and customers

::: tip
If you want to retain historical data for reporting or future use, we recommend choosing **Delete field only**.
:::

## Things to keep in mind

- Deletion is **permanent** and cannot be undone
- The field will be removed from all admin pages immediately
- Double-check whether the field is still being used before deleting
    
## Operation steps

1. In your Genstore admin, go to **Settings -> Metafields**.
2. Select the module where the metafield is defined (e.g., Products, Customers).
3. Click the metafield you want to delete
4. Click **Delete**, then choose one of the options:
    - **Delete field only** – Keep the data but remove the definition
    - **Delete field and its saved values** – Remove both the definition and all stored values
