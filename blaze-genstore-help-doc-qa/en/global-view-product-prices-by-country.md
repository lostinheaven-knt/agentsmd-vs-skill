# View product price details by country/region

For international e-commerce stores, being able to clearly view product price details across different markets is a key tool for ensuring pricing accuracy and consistency. Genstore enables merchants to see the price of each product in various markets, helping them better understand their pricing strategies and adjustments in different regions. This not only ensures localized pricing customization but also optimizes pricing on a global scale, improving merchants' profitability.

With this feature, merchants can easily access key information such as sales prices, exchange rate fluctuations, and price adjustments for each market. This provides valuable data to help further optimize pricing and create more competitive pricing strategies.

## Steps

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and click **Settings** -> **Markets**.
2. Click the desired market to open its settings page.
3. Click **Products and pricing**.
4. Select the product for which you want to view price details. You can search by product name or use filters to find the product.
5. If your product has multiple variants, click **View prices** to see the price for each variant.
