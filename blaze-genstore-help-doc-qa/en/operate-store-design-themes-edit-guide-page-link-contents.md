# Associate templates with content

In each theme set, the system includes a built-in default template for different page types (such as **products, collections, pages, blog collections, and blog posts**) to ensure the store can launch quickly and maintain a consistent display effect.

If you wish to distinguish different scenarios or styles based on the default style, you can assign them to corresponding content objects according to your business needs. When unassigned, the system will automatically use the default template for that type; however, once a custom template is assigned, the display effect will always be based on the selected template.

## Applicable scope

* Pages
* Products
* Collections
* Blog posts
* Blog collections

## Assign a template to a product

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Products**, and click on the target product to enter the product details page.
3. In the **Theme template** dropdown menu on the right, select the template to be applied.
4. Click **Save**.

## Assign a template to a collection

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Products** -> **Collections**, and click the **pencil** icon in the **Action** column of the target collection.
3. In the **Theme template** dropdown menu on the right, select the template to be applied.
4. Click **Save**.

## Assign a template to a blog post

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Blog posts**, and click the **pencil** icon in the **Action** column of the target blog post.
3. In the **Theme template** dropdown menu on the right, select the template to be applied.
4. Click **Save**.

## Assign a template to a blog collection

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Blog posts** -> **Manage blog collections**.
3. Click the **Edit** button in the **Action** column of the target blog collection.
4. In the **Theme template** dropdown menu on the right, select the template to be applied.
5. Click **Save**.

## Assign a template to a page

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Pages**.
3. Click the **pencil** icon in the **Action** column of the target page.
4. In the **Theme template** dropdown menu on the right, select the template to be applied.
5. Click **Save**.

## Bulk assign templates to products and collections

For products and collections, you can also apply templates in bulk through the bulk edit operation.

### Bulk assign templates to products

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Products**, and check the products you wish to apply a template to in bulk.
3. Click **Bulk edit**.
4. In the bulk editor, find the **Theme template** column.
5. In the dropdown list, select the template to be applied.
6. Click **Save**.

### Bulk assign templates to collections

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Products** -> **Collections**, and check the collections you wish to apply a template to in bulk.
3. Click **Bulk edit**.
4. In the bulk editor, find the **Theme template** column.
5. In the dropdown list, select the template to be applied.
6. Click **Save**.
