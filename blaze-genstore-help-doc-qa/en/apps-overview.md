# Apps

Genstore offers a variety of apps that make it easy to build your store and integrate with external services. Whether you're optimizing store management, improving user experience, or integrating payment, logistics, and marketing, Genstore apps provide comprehensive support to help automate and streamline your store's operations.

- **Genstore official apps**: Developed by Genstore, these apps are deeply integrated to ensure compatibility with the platform. They cover key areas like store setup, data analytics, and payment processing.
- **Third-party apps**: Developed by external creators to expand Genstore’s functionality. Whether you're looking for customized features or industry-specific solutions, you can find the right tools. If you run into issues, you can reach out to the app developer for support.
- **Custom apps:** An app specifically developed for a particular Genstore store. It will not be listed in the Genstore app store and is suitable for personalized functions that meet specific business needs.

You can also extend your store’s capabilities with custom apps. With [Genstore’s open API](https://partners.genstore.ai/docs/api-rest/), you can access store data, create deep customizations, and automate processes. Additionally, you can expand your store to other platforms to reach a wider audience and boost business growth.
