# **Genstore Lite plan**

The Genstore Lite plan includes all the essential tools to run your online store. No matter your store’s size, you can create and customize your store, showcase products, and manage orders and customer information. The Genstore admin helps you streamline operations. Plus, Genstore AI supports store design, product management, promotions, and data analysis, making daily management easier.

## Credits

- 50 monthly credits.
- 5 daily credits awarded for daily logins.

## **Plan features**

In addition to the features available on all plans, the Genstore Lite plan offers the following features:

### **Genstore AI**

Genstore AI is a suite of intelligent tools designed to help merchants automate and optimize store operations. With AI-powered automation, you can manage products, design your store, create promotions, and analyze data more efficiently—reducing manual work, cutting costs, and improving overall business  performance.

| Feature            | Description                                                                                                |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Launch Agent**   | Fast-track foundational settings, including domains, payments, and shipping, to ensure a compliant launch. |
| **Product Agent**  | Manage your catalog efficiently with smart product sourcing and creation tools.                            |
| **Campaign Agent** | Trigger AI-driven marketing campaigns and generate discount codes to boost conversions.                    |
| **Design Agent**   | Generate homepages and switch themes instantly to build high-converting brand visuals.                     |
| **Analytics agent**     | Analyze traffic, conversion rates, and sales performance in real-time for actionable business insights.    |
| **Support agent**  | Your 24/7 dedicated merchant expert for technical guidance and operational advice.                         |
