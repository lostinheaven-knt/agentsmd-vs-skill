# Checkout and ordering

The checkout page is the final step in completing a purchase. Genstore’s Checkout page is designed to be simple and intuitive, offering a smooth and efficient experience. The page includes the following key features:

## Order overview

- **Product list**: Displays all items in the checkout, including product name, image, specifications (like color and size), and quantity.

## Price details

- Unit price and total price for each product.
- Shipping or delivery fees.
- Discounts, coupons, or promotional offers applied.
- The final total amount due.

## Customer information

- **Shipping address**: The address entered or selected by the customer for delivery. Genstore typically offers address management, allowing users to switch or add new addresses.
- **Contact details**: Includes the recipient's name and contact information.

## Payment options

Multiple payment methods are supported:

- Online payment (credit/debit cards, third-party platforms, etc.). See [Get payments](./operate-payments.md) for more details.
- [Cash on delivery](./operate-payment-manual-payments.md).

## Delivery methods

Customers can choose from various delivery options (e.g., standard shipping, in-store pickup, local delivery). See [order fulfillment](./operate-fulfillment.md) for more details.

## Going to checkout

Once the user confirms their order, they must proceed to the checkout page to complete the process. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store.
2. In the online store, click **Check out** in either the product list or product details page, or go to the **[shopping cart](./operate-orders-add-to-cart.md)** and click **Check out**.

## Enter contact information

Once on the Checkout page, the customer can fill in or update their contact information. Please ensure the details are accurate. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store and go to the checkout page.
2. In the **Contact** section, enter or update your **email** or **phone number**.

## Select delivery method and shipping plan

On the Checkout page, customers can choose the delivery method and shipping plan. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store and go to the checkout page.
2. In the **Delivery** section, select from the available options (e.g., **standard shipping, in-store pickup, local delivery**).
3. Enter your **shipping address**.
4. After confirming the address, choose your preferred **shipping plan**.

## Choose payment method

On the checkout page, customers can choose their preferred payment method. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store and go to the checkout page.
2. In the **Payment** section, select your desired payment option.

## Enter billing address

After selecting your **payment method**, you can enter your billing address or use the same address as the shipping address. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store and go to the checkout page.
2. After choosing your payment method, fill in your billing address or select to use the shipping address as your billing address.

## Apply discount codes

On the checkout page, customers can enter **[manual discount codes](./expand-sales-discounts.md)**. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store and go to the checkout page.
2. Enter your **manual discount code**.
3. If you no longer want to use the code, click `x` to remove it.

## Use gift cards

Customers can also use **[gift cards](./operate-product-gift-cards.md)** on the checkout page. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store and go to the checkout page.
2. Select your gift card.
3. If you change your mind, click `x` to remove the selected gift card.

## Place order and pay

Once all checkout details are confirmed, customers can click **Pay now** to complete the payment and finalize the transaction. Here’s how:

1. Log in to the Genstore online store and go to the checkout page.
2. Click **Pay now** to complete the process.

## FAQ
### Q1. Can I display my store policies on the Checkout page?
> Yes. You can display key policies—such as Refund, Shipping, Privacy, Terms of Service, and Contact Information—at the bottom of the checkout page (typically below the "Pay Now" button). These are presented as clickable links, allowing customers to review your terms before finalizing their purchase. Providing this transparency helps build buyer trust and reduces cart abandonment.

### Q2. How do I set up store policies on the Checkout page?
> A: 
> - Navigate to **Settings** → **Policies** in Genstore admin. Enter or edit your Refund, Privacy, and Shipping terms, then click **Save**.
> Once saved, the system automatically generates the corresponding links in the checkout footer.
> Each policy title acts as a hyperlink. When clicked, it opens the full policy in a new window or a pop-up, depending on your settings.
> You can manually configure the destination URL for each policy link within the admin settings.
> For more detailed instructions, please refer to our guide: [Setting Up Store Policies](./settings-policy.md).
