# Drop Pay

Drop Pay is a lightweight, turnkey payment solution specifically tailored by Genstore for the **Dropshipping business model**. It enables merchants to start accepting payments and settling orders instantly, without the hurdles of a complex application process.

This solution is ideal for merchants looking to launch quickly, test new products, or those who haven't yet prepared full business registration documents for traditional payment gateways.

## Key features

* **No Onboarding, Active by Default:** Once you create and enable your Genstore Dropshipping store, Drop Pay is automatically activated. No separate application or waiting for KYC approval is required.
* **Built for Dropshipping:** Drop Pay is optimized exclusively for Dropshipping transactions, offering a much leaner operational flow.
* **Direct PayPal Payouts:** Once funds are settled, they are transferred directly to your linked PayPal account.

## Ideal for

* **New Entrepreneurs:** Merchants just starting their journey in the dropshipping space.
* **Agile Testers:** Sellers who want to rapidly validate products and market demand.
* **Pre-Launch Merchants:** Businesses that are still in the process of gathering formal payment application materials.

## Getting started

To begin accepting payments and ensure secure fund clearance, please complete the following steps:

1.  **Link Your Account:** Associate your preferred PayPal email address with your store.
2.  **Tax Compliance:** Follow the prompts to electronically sign and submit the required tax documents (such as **W-8** or **W-9** forms).
3.  **Manage Funds:** Use the [Balance page](./operate-dropshipping-drop-pay-balance.md) to track your transaction history, pending balances, and settlement status in real-time.
