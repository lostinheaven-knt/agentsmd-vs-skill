# General store settings

Before setting up your Genstore store, you need to create a Genstore account and add essential business information.

## Create a Genstore account

Visit the [Genstore account creation page](https://accounts.genstore.ai/account/signup) and enter a valid email and password to start setting up your store.

While you don’t need to complete all details immediately, make sure to add the following:

- Store name and contact information
- Legal business name and company address
- Store time zone
- Default store currency
- Default weight unit

## Domain name

When you create a store, the system automatically assigns a unique `genmystore.com` domain, such as `1a2b3c4-5.genmystore.com`. This domain is used for logging into the Genstore admin and verifying account identity. If you need to connect your Genstore account with third-party apps or services, you may be required to provide this domain as your account ID.

By default, this domain serves as your store’s primary domain. However, you can [connect a third-party domain to Genstore](./global-domains-custom-domain-third-party.md) to replace the `genmystore.com` domain for customer access.

Additionally, merchants can use custom domains. For more details, refer to [domains](./global-domains.md). **Note:** You only have one opportunity to set a new `genmystore.com` domain.

## Manage store name and contact information

Your store name will appear on every page of your website. By default, the store name is **My Store**.

:::tip

Changing your store name does not change your default `genmystore.com` domain. To modify your store’s domain, visit [domain](./global-domains.md).

:::

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Store details** section, click the pencil icon next to your store name.
3. In the **Edit profile** dialog, update the store name, phone number, and contact email.
4. Click **Save**.

## Manage legal business name and business address

Your legal business name and address will appear on your [billing information](./start-genstore-billing.md).

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Store details** section, click the pencil icon next to **Billing address**.
3. Enter your legal business name and business address.
4. Click **Save**.

## Select store currency

1. Log in to **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Store defaults** -> **Display currency** section, click **...** -> **Change store currency**.
3. Use the dropdown menu to select a new currency.
4. Click **Save**.

After updating your store currency, please review and update the following store settings:
* **Review and update product pricing**: Since the value of different currencies is usually not equivalent, using a new currency may cause product prices to change, which can impact your profit margins.
* **Review and adjust discounts**: Ensure that they are aligned with the new store currency.
* **Update shipping rates**: Delete existing shipping rates, then manually re-add shipping rates to display the correct shipping costs in the new currency.
* **Issue new gift cards for customers with remaining balances**: To ensure customers can continue making purchases with the new currency, it is recommended to issue new gift cards for those with remaining balances.

## Manage the default weight unit for your store

Genstore supports both imperial (e.g., pounds (lb), ounces (oz)) and metric (e.g., kilograms (kg), grams (g)) weight units as the store default. You can set different weight units for specific products later.

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Store defaults** section, select **Unit system** (imperial or metric), then use the **Default weight unit** dropdown menu to choose the unit.

## Manage store time zone

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Store defaults** section, select a new time zone from the **Time zone** dropdown.
3. Click **Save**.

## Manage store brand assets

You can set up brand assets such as your store’s logo, colors, and social media links. These brand elements are shared across sales channels, themes, and apps.

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Brand assets** section, click **Manage** to go to the **Brand** page for setup.
   For more details on each setting, see [Manage store brand assets](./start-genstore-account-brand-assets.md).
