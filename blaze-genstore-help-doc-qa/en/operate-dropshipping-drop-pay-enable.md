# Activate Drop Pay

Drop Pay is a turnkey payment solution designed by Genstore specifically for dropshipping businesses. To ensure your store can accept payments and settle funds smoothly, please review the following activation process and guidelines.

## Prerequisites

- **Valid PayPal Account:** You need a PayPal email address capable of receiving USD. Genstore will issue payouts to this address.
- **Tax Documentation:** Depending on your tax residency, you must complete and submit the appropriate tax form for compliance:
    - **U.S. Residents/Entities:** Form W-9
    - **Non-U.S. Residents/Entities:** Form W-8

## How to activate

- **Automatic Activation:** Drop Pay does **not require a manual application**.
- **Instant Enablement:** The feature is automatically activated once you successfully create and enable your Genstore Dropshipping store.
- **Zero Wait Time:** You can start accepting orders immediately without waiting for manual KYC approval.

## Linking your Payout account & tax status

Currently, Drop Pay funds are settled to your PayPal account. Follow these steps to complete the setup:

1. Log in to your **Genstore Admin**.
2. Navigate to **Dropshipping** -> **Balance** in the left sidebar.
3. Under the **Drop Pay** view, click the **Settings** button.
4. Enter your **PayPal account email** in the pop-up and click confirm.
5. Configure your **Tax residency**. For more details, please refer to [Tax Residency Configuration](./operate-dropshipping-drop-pay-tax-document.md).
6. Click **Confirm** to save.

## Scope and limitations

Please note the following operational restrictions:

- **Product Restriction:** Drop Pay **only supports Dropshipping products**. If an order contains non-dropshipping items, the system will trigger your other configured third-party payment gateways.
- **Feature Restriction:** Accelerated Checkout buttons are currently unavailable on product detail pages and side-carts for dropshipping products using Drop Pay.
- **Solution Mutuality:** Drop Pay and Genstore Payments cannot be used simultaneously. Activating Genstore Payments will automatically disable Drop Pay.
- **Settlement Requirement:** You must submit a valid **Tax form** in addition to your PayPal account before payouts can be issued.
