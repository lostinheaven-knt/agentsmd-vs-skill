# Sales by channel

The **Sales by Channel report** provides comprehensive insights into performance metrics (e.g., sales, orders) across all sales channels, enabling strategic resource allocation and reduced dependency on single channels to mitigate risks and enhance market competitiveness.

## Use this report to

- **Channel Ranking**: Identify top 20 revenue-generating channels.
- **Performance Benchmarking**: Compare channels using metrics like **Sales**, **Orders**, and **Conversion Rate**.
- **Granular Analysis**: Review channel-specific sales details including units sold, returns, and regional penetration.

## Report data fields

For standard definitions, refer to [Sales Reports - Common Terms](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-sales.md#common-terms).

**Additional terms specific to this report**:

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Sales channel name** | Platform or marketplace where orders/returns originated (e.g., "Amazon US"). |

## How custom columns affect this report

Adding custom columns to the **Sales by Channel report** may alter data grouping:

- Each product in an order generates a new row when product-level custom columns (e.g., Product title) are added.
- Channel attribution remains anchored to the original order context.
