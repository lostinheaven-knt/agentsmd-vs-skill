# Manage pending shipments

On the **Pending shipment** (**Store** -> **Orders** -> **Shipping**) page, you can centrally manage all orders that have not yet been fulfilled. From here, you can modify package details, select shipping methods, and purchase electronic shipping labels.

## Page overview

Each order entry displays the order number, order time, product details, customer name, sales channel, and shipping/receiving addresses. You should also pay close attention to these core fields:
* **Package information**: Displays the dimensions and weight of the parcel.
* **Shipping**: After a logistics provider is authorized, the system will display available shipping routes and the lowest estimated shipping costs.

## Preparation and adjustments before shipping

Before officially purchasing a label, you can fine-tune the order details:
* **Modify package information**: Click **Edit** under **Package information** to update the weight and dimensions. Click **Confirm** once finished.
* **Change shipping address**: Click the **Pencil icon** next to the **Ship to** address. Please ensure the address is accurate to avoid delivery errors.
* **Add notes**: Click **... -> Add note**. These notes are only visible in the admin backend for internal follow-up and are not visible to customers.

## Buy shipping labels

* **Logistics provider**: This section automatically displays supported routes and shipping costs based on your selected provider. You can choose the most suitable route based on your specific needs.
* **Complete purchase**: Click the **Buy shipping label** button. The system will generate a shipping label through your authorized provider, such as Genstore Shipping or Shippo. 
* **Note for Shippo users**: If purchasing labels via Shippo, please ensure your account has a sufficient balance and a valid payment method linked.

## Other fulfillment options

* **Merchant self-shipment**: If you prefer not to purchase a label through the system, click **... -> Self-delivery**.
* **Print documents**: Under the **...** menu, you can print **packing list`** (for verifying items) and **Pick list** (to guide warehouse staff during picking).

> **Tip**: Genstore Dropshipping orders are fulfilled by suppliers. These orders currently do not support merchant self-shipment or label purchasing directly on the platform.
