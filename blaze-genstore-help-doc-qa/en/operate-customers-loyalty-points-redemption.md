# Point redemption

Genstore Loyalty program allows store owners to transform customer activities—like registration, shopping, and social media interactions—into various tangible rewards.

## Access point redemption settings

Navigate to the **Redeem points** section within your dashboard to view and adjust your store's redemption rules. Here, you can:

- **Edit**: Modify existing redemption strategies by clicking the **Edit** button.
- **Toggle**: Activate or deactivate rules using the on/off switch.

## FAQs

### Q1. How are points selected for redemption?
> A: Points are utilized starting with the **earliest accumulated** to prevent expiration.

### Q2. Can discount coupons be used if the order doesn't meet the minimum requirement?
> A: Depending on whether you have set **Minimum order amount requirement**, if yes, the order must meet the minimum threshold set for using a discount coupon, if no, there will be no requirement.

### Q3: Can discount coupons be combined with other offers?
> A: Yes, enable this in the **Combines with** settings to allow coupons to be used with other discounts.

### Q4. Do redeemed discount coupons expire?
> A: Depending on your settings, coupons can have an expiration date, with notifications sent before expiry.

### Q5. Are points refunded if an order with a redeemed coupon is canceled or refunded?
> A: **Used coupons are not refundable**, but points spent are returned with recalculated validity.  for instance, if 100 points were used to redeem a $5 coupon that was applied to an order that was later refunded, the coupon is not refunded, but the 100 points are returned with recalculated validity.

### Q6. How are points recalculated if a discount coupon redeemed with points is used and the order is partially refunded?
> A: Points will be refunded based on the refunded item's proportion of the total order amount. For example, if a customer uses 100 points to redeem a $5 coupon for an order containing two items costing $50 each and one item is returned, the coupon is not refunded, however, 50 points would be returned (50% of the points used).

### Q7. Are discount coupons redeemed with points tied to an individual? Can they be transferred to someone else?
> A: Coupons redeemed with points **do not need to be linked to an individual account**, meaning they can be gifted to others. Customers are free to share their redeemed coupon codes with friends or family.

### Q8. If a reward coupon is set to expire, are customers notified before it expires?**
> A: If reward coupons have set expiration dates, the system will send an expiration alert email 30 days and then 7 days before the coupon expires.
