# Genstore Translate app

**Genstore Translate** app helps you create multilingual content for your store to deliver a seamless global shopping experience. A multilingual storefront can drive more traffic, improve SEO performance, and build trust with international customers—helping your business grow worldwide. The app offers one-click AI translation and manual editing options. A built-in side-by-side editor makes it easy to review translations efficiently and ensure accuracy.

## Key features

- Fully integrated with the Genstore admin to manage multilingual content in one place
- One-click AI-powered translation
- Side-by-side editor for manual edits and review

## Use cases

Use Genstore Translate app to localize your store content across languages, including:

- **Product descriptions**: Attract global shoppers with translated product details to improve conversion rates.
- **Website pages**: Translate templates and navigation to provide a localized user experience across markets.
- **Blog posts**: Expand your brand’s reach by translating blog content for global audiences.
- **Email notifications**: Send order and shipping updates in your customer’s preferred language to build trust.
- **Store policies**: Translate your privacy policy and terms to ensure legal compliance and customer clarity.

## How to install

Genstore Translate installs automatically when you add a new language to your store.

If you've already added a language and want to install it manually, go to **Settings -> Languages** and click **Install Genstore Translate**.

## How to manually translate content

You can manually translate your content within the app or directly from the content pages in your store admin.

### Steps:

1. In your Genstore admin, go to **Apps** or **Settings -> Languages**.
2. Click **Genstore Translate**.
3. Select the language you want to translate.
4. Choose the content you want to translate.
5. Enter your translation into the corresponding fields.
6. Click **Save.**

## How to automatically translate content

Use Genstore Translate app to automatically generate translations. We recommend reviewing auto-translated content before publishing.

### Auto-translate all content in a language

You need to activate auto-translation separately for each language. Translated content usually appears on the storefront within a few minutes.

#### Steps:

1. In your Genstore admin, go to **Apps** or **Settings -> Languages**.
2. Click **Genstore Translate**.
3. Select the language.
4. Click **Auto-translate**.
	- If the selected language already has translations, choose:
        - **Translate only empty fields** — fill in untranslated fields only.
        - **Overwrite translations for all fields** — regenerate translations for all fields.
    - Click **Confirm**.
5. Once the translation is complete, a notification email will be sent to your account email address.

::: tip

- When new content is added (e.g., a new product), go back to the app and click **Auto translate** to generate translations for the new content.
- When existing content is updated (e.g., a product description):
    - If translations already exist, you can manually edit them, choose **Overwrite translations for all fields**, or clear the previous translations and then run **Translate only empty fields**.
    - If the content is new or was not translated before, simply click **Auto Translate** and select **Translate only empty fields**.

:::

### Auto-translate a specific item

You can also auto-translate individual items such as products or blog posts.

#### Steps:

1. In your Genstore admin, go to **Apps** or **Settings -> Languages**
2. Click **Genstore Translate**
3. Select the item
4. Click **Auto-translate**.
	- If the selected language already has translations, choose:
        - **Translate only empty fields** — fill in untranslated fields only.
        - **Overwrite translations for all fields** — regenerate translations for all fields.
    - Click **Confirm**.
5. Click **Save**

::: tip

- For content with images or videos, you can choose different versions for each language.
- URL handles are not automatically translated—you’ll need to edit them manually for each language.

:::
