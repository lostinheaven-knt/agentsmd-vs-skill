# Manage store brand assets

By setting up your store’s brand assets, you can add a logo, brand colors, social media links, and other elements to ensure a consistent brand image across all sales channels, themes, and apps.

## Logo

Add a unique logo to represent your brand. Genstore allows you to upload both a default logo and a square logo to ensure optimal display across different platforms and applications. Keep in mind that some platforms, such as social media, may crop logos into a circular shape.

### Upload requirements

Before uploading, ensure that your logo meets the following specifications for the best display:

| Brand asset  | Description                                                  | Requirements                                                 |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Default logo | Used in most common logo placements, such as the store homepage and cart. A high-quality logo should complement your store name and match your brand’s primary color. | - Formats: HEIC, WEBP, SVG, PNG, JPG <BR/> - Recommended size: At least 512 x 512 pixels |
| Square logo  | Used for social media profiles and platform icons. If the platform uses a circular image format, the logo will be cropped accordingly. | - Formats: HEIC, WEBP, SVG, PNG, JPG <BR/> - Recommended size: At least 512 x 512 pixels |

### Upload a logo

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Brand assets** section, click **Manage**.
3. In the **Logos** section, upload your brand logo or select one from the media library. You can also search for an image by name or upload a new image from your device.
4. Repeat step 3 to select or upload a square logo.
5. Click **Save**.

:::tip

- If your logo does not meet the format or resolution requirements, the system will prompt you to adjust it. Ensure the file meets the guidelines before proceeding.
- Each logo type supports only one file at a time. You can update or delete the existing logo whenever needed.
- Once the logo is uploaded for the first time, it will be automatically synced to all display locations across the site. If you update the logo later, you'll need to manually update it in the [theme editor](./operate-store-design-images#upload-logo-and-site-icon).
:::

## Colors

Setting brand colors is essential for maintaining consistency. Choose primary, secondary, and contrast colors to ensure a unified visual identity across different platforms.

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Brand assets** section, click **Manage**.
3. In the **Colors** section, click to add a primary color.
4. *(Optional)* The system will automatically generate a contrast color, but you can customize it to better match your brand identity.
5. Repeat the process to select secondary and its contrast colors.
6. Click **Save**.

## Social media links

Adding social media links makes it easier to engage with customers. These links typically appear in the store’s footer or other designated areas.

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Settings** -> **General**.
2. In the **Brand assets** section, click **Manage**.
3. In the **Social media links** section, click **+ Add social media link** and select a social media platform from the dropdown menu, such as Instagram, Twitter, or Facebook.
4. Enter the full URL in the provided field, such as `https://instagram.com/genstore` or `https://twitter.com/genstore`.
5. Click **Save**.
