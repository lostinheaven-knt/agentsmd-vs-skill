# Sales by shipping method

The **Sales by shipping method report** reveals which shipping options customers choose for their purchases, helping you evaluate the popularity and performance of different **delivery channels**.

## Use this report to

- **Efficiency Analysis**: Identify the **top shipping methods** with the highest order fulfillment efficiency to optimize logistics and enhance customer satisfaction.
- **Performance Metrics**: Analyze **detailed sales data per shipping method**, including Products Sold, Shipping, Taxes, and Discounts, to assess market competitiveness.

## Report data fields

The sales reports use several [common terms](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-sales.md#common-terms).

**Additional terms specific to this report**:

| Terms | Definition |
|----------|----------|
| **Shipping method** | The shipping method selected for the order, indicating how the items will be delivered. |
