# Find and filter products

In addition to powerful product management tools, **Genstore** also offers smart sorting and filtering features, helping you quickly find products, services, or data that meet your needs, improving both efficiency and decision-making.

## Search for products

You can quickly search for products by entering the product name or keywords.

### Steps:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to the **Store** -> **Products** page.
2. Enter the keywords or product name in the search box.
3. Press Enter, and the search results will display automatically.

## Sort the product list

The **Genstore** product list includes all the products you've created and saved. Once you create a product, it’s automatically added to the list, and you can access or manage it anytime. The product list shows basic information for each product and allows you to sort, filter, or search:

- **Create time**: Sort by date (ascending or descending).
- **Price**: Sort by price (ascending or descending).

### Steps:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to the **Store** -> **Products** page.
2. Click the sort button at the top of the product list.
3. Choose a sorting option, and the product list will update automatically.

## Filter the product list

You can easily find specific products to edit, view, or update by applying filters. Available filter options include:

- **Title**: Show products that match the search text.
- **Price**: Filter by price range (set a maximum and minimum price).
- **Inventory**: Filter by stock status (in stock, out of stock, or partially out of stock).
- **Product vendor**: Filter by a specific vendor.
- **Tags**: Filter by products with specific tags.
- **Status**: Filter by product status (archived, active, draft, or unlisted).
- **Sales channel**: Filter by products in specific sales channels.
- **Market**: Filter products sold in specific markets.
- **Category**: Filter by product category, with the option to select multiple categories.
- **Collections**: Filter by product series.
- **Gift cards**: Filter for gift card products for your store.
- **Metafields**: Filter by products with specific metafield values.

### Steps:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to the **Store** -> **Products** page.
2. Click the filter icon.
3. Set the filter criteria and values, and the product list will update automatically.

:::tip

When you set multiple filter criteria, the system will only display products that match all conditions.

:::
