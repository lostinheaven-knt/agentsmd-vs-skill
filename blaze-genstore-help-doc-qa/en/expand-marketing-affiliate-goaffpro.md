# Genstore GOAFFPRO

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based growth strategy where partners—bloggers, influencers, or loyal customers—promote your products to their audience. You only pay a commission when they drive a successful sale. This "pay-per-result" model is one of the most cost-effective ways for SMBs to scale brand awareness and revenue without upfront ad spend.

To help you launch this strategy quickly, **Genstore is deeply integrated with GOAFFPRO.** This integration automates the heavy lifting: partner recruitment, referral link generation, and real-time order tracking. With Genstore’s high-performance infrastructure and GOAFFPRO’s tracking precision, you can scale your influencer network without touching a single line of code.

## Prerequisites
* **An active GOAFFPRO account:** If you haven’t signed up yet, create your account at [GOAFFPRO.com](https://www.goaffpro.com/).

## Install the GOAFFPRO app
Get started by connecting the app to your Genstore admin.
1. Visit the [Genstore App Store](https://apps.genstore.ai) and log in.
2. Select the store you wish to integrate.
3. Search for **Genstore GOAFFPRO**.
4. Click **Install**. You will be redirected to your Genstore admin panel.
5. **Pro tip:** Click the **pin icon** next to GOAFFPRO in your app list to keep it in your sidebar for easy access to your analytics.

## Collect your Genstore store details
You will need two specific pieces of information to link your accounts:
* **Store name:** Found in **Settings** -> **General** under the **Store details** section.
* **Store domain:** Found in **Settings** -> **Domains**. Copy your primary "live" domain.

## Generate your SDK link in GOAFFPRO
1. Log in to your **GOAFFPRO Admin Dashboard**.
2. Click the **"+ Add app to Store"** button.
3. Select **Custom SDK** as your connection method.
4. Enter the **Store Name** and **Store URL** you collected from Genstore.
5. The system will generate a unique **SDK Link**. Click **Copy**.

## Activate tracking in Genstore
1. Go back to your **Genstore Merchant Admin** and open the **GOAFFPRO** app.
2. In the **Install Integration Code** field, paste the SDK Link you just copied.
3. Click **Save**. Your store is now live and tracking referral traffic.

## How the data sync works
Once the setup is complete, the system runs on autopilot:

* **Partner onboarding:** Affiliates join through your branded portal and receive unique links or custom discount codes.
* **Conversion:** A customer clicks an affiliate's link and completes a purchase on your Genstore site.
* **Real-time attribution:** The system identifies the specific affiliate responsible for the sale and syncs the order data to GOAFFPRO instantly.
* **Performance tracking:** You can monitor conversion rates, top-performing affiliates, and owed commissions directly from your dashboard.
