# Manage AI Agent usage and costs

AI Agent replies in Inbox are typically triggered by your customers. Without limits in place, customers could interact with the AI Agent freely, which may lead to unexpected charges. To help you manage costs effectively, Genstore offers flexible usage control features.

## Set usage limits for your Inbox AI Agent

In your Genstore admin panel, go to the **Inbox** section in the left-hand menu. Under the **Usage** panel, click **Set usage limits** to open the limit settings panel.

You can set usage caps across the following three dimensions to help manage your AI Agent's usage:

|Control Type|Description|Max limit (for subscribed Genstore plans)|
|---|---|---|
|Monthly reply limit|The maximum number of AI replies allowed per billing cycle. Once reached, service will pause until the next cycle.|Up to 10,000 replies/month|
|Daily reply limit|The maximum number of AI replies allowed per day. Helps balance usage and prevent rapid overuse. Resets every 24 hours, based on your store's timezone.|Up to 10,000 replies/day|
|Per-customer daily limit|The maximum number of replies a single customer can receive from the AI per day. Helps ensure fair use and prevents overuse by individual users.|Up to 10,000 replies per customer/day|

::: tip
Your actual charges are based on the **Monthly reply limit**.  
Daily and per-customer limits are for usage control only and do not affect your total cost.  
The system calculates your usage fees based on monthly caps and actual reply count—no double counting.
:::
## What happens when limits are reached?

- **If a per-customer limit is reached**: That customer’s further messages will be routed to a live agent. The limit resets the next day.
- **If the daily limit is reached**: All AI replies are paused for the rest of the day, and new inquiries will be routed to a live agent. Resets the next day.
- **If the monthly limit is reached**: All AI replies are paused until the next billing cycle begins. Messages are routed to a live agent.

## Other reasons AI Agent replies may be paused

Even if your Inbox AI Agent hasn’t hit the usage limits above, it may still stop responding in the following situations:

- **Your monthly AI usage charges have reached $1,000**  
    To protect against excessive billing, AI Agent usage is temporarily paused until the next billing cycle.
- **There is an unpaid invoice on your store account**  
    Please go to the **Billing** section and complete the payment to resume AI Agent service.
