# Sales by product variant SKU

Compared to the [Sales by product report](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-sales-product.md), the **Sales by product variant SKU report** provides granular sales insights for top-performing **product specifications (e.g., color, size)**, empowering data-driven inventory and merchandising decisions.

## Shipping & tax handling

**Shipping costs are excluded** from this report because a single order may contain multiple products but only one shipping fee. Since shipping cannot be allocated to individual products:

- **Tax calculations** also exclude taxes related to shipping.

## Use this report to

- **Identify Top SKUs**: Discover the top 20 SKUs by sales performance to prioritize high-demand variants.
- **Analyze Variant Performance**: Compare sales differences across SKUs to identify popular specifications.
- **Optimize Inventory**: Review detailed metrics per SKU, including units sold, net sales, and returns.

## Report data fields

For standard definitions, refer to [Sales reports - common terms](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-sales#common-terms).

**Additional terms**:

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Product title** | Customer-facing display name of the product. |
| **Variant title** | Name of the product variant (e.g., "Blue / Large"). |
| **Variant SKU** | Unique identifier for the product variant. |
| **Net quantity** | Calculated as **Units sold** minus **Returned units**. |
