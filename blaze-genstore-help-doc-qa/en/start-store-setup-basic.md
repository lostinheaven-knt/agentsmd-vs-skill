# Set up your store

This guide walks you through the essential steps to get your Genstore store up and running—from creating your store to uploading products and customizing your storefront. By the end, you’ll have a solid foundation for a successful launch.

## Create your account and store

Start by signing up for a Genstore account.

- Go to the [Genstore sign-up page](https://accounts.genstore.ai/account/signup) and register with a valid email and password.
- After signing up, you’ll be guided through an AI-powered store setup. You can follow the quick AI process or choose **Skip** or **Skip and create store** to set things up manually.
- In Genstore admin, click **your account name** in the lower-left corner and click **Manage account** to manage settings like external login services and admin language.

## Complete your store information

Navigate to **Settings -> General** to fill in your store details.
 For detailed steps, refer to [Store information settings](./start-genstore-account-create-manage.md):

- Store name and contact info (default name is `mystore`)
- Billing address and legal business name (for billing and compliance)
- Default currency and weight unit
- Store time zone (for orders and analytic reports)
- Store logo, brand colors, and social media links

> **Pro tip**: Filling in accurate information ensures smooth billing and helps unlock store features.

## Add staff and set permissions

If you have a team, you can create separate logins for each member and control their access. 

**Go to**: **Settings -> Users and permissions**. For details, see [Roles and permissions](./start-genstore-account-roles-permissions.md)

- Create individual staff accounts
- Assign roles like customer support, operations, or finance
- Limit access to sensitive data based on role

> Clear roles = better security and smoother collaboration.

## Set up your domain

Your domain is your store’s address online. 

**Go to**: **Settings -> Domains**.  For details, see [Domains](./global-domains.md)

- Use your Genstore default domain (e.g., `yourstore.genmystore.com`). You can [customize it once](./global-domains-change-default-domain-name.md).
- Prefer to use your own domain (e.g., `yourbrand.com`)? [Connect it through DNS](./global-domains-custom-domain.md). Set up [URL redirects](./operate-store-design-navigation.md) if needed.

> A custom domain helps you build brand credibility and trust.

## Add your products

Start populating your store with products.

**Go to**: **Store** -> **Products**

- [Manually add items](./operate-product-create.md) with images, descriptions, prices, and inventory
- [Import large product catalogs via CSV](./operate-product-export-import.md)
- [Organize items into collections](./operate-product-collections.md) for easier browsing

> Use quality photos and rich descriptions to boost engagement and sales.

### Speed up with AI-generated content

Upload product images and let Genstore’s AI assistant, Sara, generate titles, descriptions, and more. 

> You can always fine-tune the output to match your brand tone. For details, see [Collaborate with AI agents](./start-genstore-account-homepage.md)

## Design your storefront

Make your store look and feel just right.

**Go to**: **Store -> Online Store -> Themes**

- [Choose a theme](./operate-store-design-themes-template-select.md) from the template library
- [AI-powered design tool](./operate-store-design-themes-ai-decorate.md): Quickly generate a complete homepage using AI, with key sections like banner carousel, product displays, bestsellers, and brand story automatically created, streamlining your design process.
- Use the [visual editor](./operate-store-design-themes-edit-guide.md) to customize your homepage, product pages, and more
- Advanced users can [edit with code](./operate-store-design-themes-code-editor.md) for deeper customization
- Preview everything before going live

> A smooth, modern storefront builds trust and drives conversions.
