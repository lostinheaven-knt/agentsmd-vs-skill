# New vs returning visitors

The **New vs returning visitors** report provides traffic data segmented by **visitor type (new or returning)**, revealing differences in session patterns and engagement levels.

This report helps you understand how first-time visitors and loyal customers interact with your website, enabling optimization of user experiences and loyalty strategies.

## Use this report to

- **Analyze traffic trends**: Observe changes in visitor counts, sessions, and page views for new/returning visitors across daily, weekly, or monthly cycles to identify seasonal patterns and growth opportunities.
- **Customer composition analysis**: Use visualizations to evaluate the proportion of new vs. returning visitors in total traffic and sessions, assessing the effectiveness of acquisition and retention efforts.
- **Behavior comparison**: Compare engagement metrics like session duration (indicates interest level), bounce rate (reveals page relevance), and purchase conversion rate (reflects loyalty and spending intent) to tailor experiences for each segment.

## Report data fields

For general terms, refer to [Acquisition reports - common terms](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-acquisition.md#common-terms).

This report includes:

| Term | Definition |
| ---- | ---------- |
| **Visitor type** | Categorizes visitors as **New** (first-time) or **Returning** (repeat). |

:::tip

Visitor classification relies on browser cookies and may exclude users who clear cookies or use privacy modes.

:::
