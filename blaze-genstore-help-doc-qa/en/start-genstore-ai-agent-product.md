# Product agent

Product agent Sara is your inventory expert. Whether you’re building a private label brand, selling digital content, or running a dropshipping business, Sara automates the entire process from sourcing to listing.

## Scenario 1: Dropshipping

*Best for: Merchants looking to connect with global suppliers and quickly sell high-profit, trending products.*

Forget jumping between multiple platforms to find inventory. You can use AI as your "head of sourcing" to find the perfect products from a massive database of millions.

### Smart sourcing workflow
* **Prompt**: Enter your requirements in the chat, such as *"Recommend 5 high-quality yoga mats."*
* **Process**: AI analyzes your intent, focusing on product categories, cost margins, and holiday trends to find the best matches.
* **Proposal**: AI searches millions of products in real-time and presents a **proposal** with high-quality, cost-effective options for your approval.
* **One-click sync**: Once you select your favorites, AI automatically syncs the data to your admin and lists them in your store.

## Scenario 2: Your own products

*Best for: Boutique owners, makers, manufacturers, or traditional retailers with their own inventory.*

Stop filling out tedious forms. Just provide a photo or a quick description, and AI will act as your "listing specialist" to get your new arrivals online.

### 1. Image-to-product
* **How it works**: Simply upload a product photo and say, *"Create a listing for this."*
* **Visual recognition**: The AI vision engine scans the photo to identify color, style, material, and brand attributes.
* **Proposal**: Based on the image, AI generates SEO-friendly titles, persuasive marketing copy, and optimized main images.
* **Review & go**: AI presents a **proposal** for your review. Once confirmed, the product is saved as a "draft" or listed live.

### 2. Text-to-product
* **How it works**: Describe your item, such as *"Help me create a listing for a genuine leather men's wallet."*
* **Semantic analysis**: AI extracts the category, key selling points, and pricing, expanding your notes into a professional product page.
* **Proposal**: AI extracts your product’s top selling points, writes high-converting descriptions, and generates professional, studio-quality imagery in seconds.
* **Review & go**: AI presents a **proposal** for your review. Once confirmed, the product is saved as a "draft" or listed live.

## Scenario 3: Digital products

*Best for: Creators selling e-books, online courses, photography, or music.*

AI acts as your "digital publisher," helping you turn raw files into attractive commercial listings.

* **Collaboration logic**: Upload your file (PDF, video, etc.) and describe what it is.
* **Proposal**: AI analyzes the content, highlights the core value, writes the description, and generates high-quality cover art.
* **Review & go**: AI presents a **proposal** for your review. Once confirmed, the product is saved as a "draft" or listed live.
