# Product collections

**Genstore** offers a powerful product collection feature that helps merchants categorize and manage products based on different conditions. With flexible automation and manual options, merchants can easily create and manage product collections, improving product management efficiency, optimizing store displays, and enhancing the shopping experience.

::: tip
You can add products in any status (archived, active, draft, or unlisted) to collections. However, only products with the status "active" will be visible on the online store front.
:::

## Automatically create product collections

The [automatically create product collections](./operate-product-collections-auto.md) feature lets merchants automatically add products that meet specified conditions to a particular collection. By setting criteria such as product type, price, tags, and inventory, merchants can precisely match products. This feature is especially helpful for managing large quantities of products, reducing manual work, and improving efficiency.

### Automatic filtering conditions

When creating an automatic product collection, merchants can control which products are included by using various [filter conditions](./operate-product-collections-condition.md). Configurable filter options include product type, price range, inventory status, title keywords, and more. Merchants can choose to set **all conditions must be met** or **any condition met** to determine the collection's contents. You can add up to 60 filter conditions.

## Manually create product collections

[Manually create product collections](./operate-product-collections-manual.md) is ideal for merchants who want full control over the collection content. Merchants can freely select products and add them to the collection based on their specific needs. This method provides flexibility for managing product displays, special promotions, or curated selections.

## Preview product collections

After saving a product collection, you can preview how it will appear in your online store in real time.

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin** and go to the **Store** -> **Products** -> **Collections** page.
2. In the product collection list, click the **Preview** icon in the action column to open a new window and view the product collection’s display in your **online store**.
3. If the product collection is assigned to multiple markets, you can switch between them in the preview:
    - **Active markets**: See the actual storefront display.
    - **Inactive markets**: Preview how the product will look in that country/region before publishing.
4. At the bottom status bar, you can view the current theme name, and either **exit preview** or click **Customize theme** to open the theme editor for customization.

## Use case: Quickly guide customers to new product collections

To make it easy for customers to quickly find your store's new product collections, you can create a drop-down menu in your online store's navigation menu, integrating the links to the new collections. This allows customers to easily browse all new products without multiple clicks.

**Steps:**

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and click **Store** -> **Products** -> **Collections**.
2. Create a new product collection, for example, "Spring/Summer 2026." For detailed instructions, refer to [Manually Create Product Collection](./operate-product-collections-manual.md).
3. In the navigation menu, link the current product collection:
   * Click **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Menus**.
   * Click the **pencil** icon next to **Main menu**.
   * Enter the navigation title (e.g., "Spring/Summer 2026") and handle (system-generated or custom), then click the **Add menu item** button.
   * In the pop-up window, enter the menu item name (e.g., "Spring/Summer 2026"), click the search box, select **Collections**, and choose the target product collection.
   * Click **Save**.
   * Repeat the above steps to add other product collections.
4. In the navigation list, you can drag and drop to adjust the display order of the menu items.
