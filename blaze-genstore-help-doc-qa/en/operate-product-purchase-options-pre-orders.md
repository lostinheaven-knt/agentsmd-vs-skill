# Genstore Preorder

Genstore Preorder is a stock and sales optimization tool designed for e-commerce merchants. It transforms traditional "Out of Stock" notices into "Pre-order" opportunities, ensuring your store keeps selling and capturing every potential order even when inventory is low.

## Core value

* **Automated switching**: Seamlessly toggle between "In-stock" and "Pre-order" modes based on real-time inventory—no manual effort required.
* **Boost conversions**: Use custom pre-order text and clear shipping promises to eliminate customer hesitation at checkout.
* **Precision forecasting**: Gauge market demand through pre-order volume to optimize restocking strategies and reduce overstock risks.

## Activation requirements

To ensure pre-order features work correctly, please verify the following product settings in your admin panel:

* **Enable inventory tracking**: The **Track inventory** option must be checked for all product variants.
* **Allow overselling**: The **Continue selling when out of stock** option must be enabled.
* **Trigger mechanism**: The pre-order mode activates automatically only when inventory is **0 or less**.

## Installation and authorization

1.  Visit the [Genstore App Store](https://apps.genstore.ai/).
2.  Search for **Genstore Preorder** and click the app card to view details.
3.  Click **Install**. You will be redirected back to your Genstore Merchant Admin.
4.  Follow the prompts to complete authorization and enable the app.
5.  **[Optional]** Click the "Pin" icon to keep the app in your navigation bar for quick access.

## Enable Genstore Preorder

1.  Log in to your admin and go to **Apps** -> **Genstore Preorder**.
    * *If not pinned, go to **Apps** -> **Installed**, then click **Launch app**.*
2.  On the app home page, click **Enable now**.
3.  You will be directed to the **App management** page. Toggle the switch to enable Genstore Preorder.
4.  Click **Save**.

## Create a pre-order campaign

::: tip
A product variant cannot be part of multiple active campaigns at the same time. If a variant is already in another campaign, please remove it first to avoid conflicts.
:::

Navigate to the Genstore Preorder app and click **Create campaign** to configure the following:

* **Campaign name**: Enter a name for your campaign. This is for **internal identification only** and will not be shown to customers.
* **Applicable products**: Choose between **All out-of-stock products** or **Specific out-of-stock products**.
* **Campaign duration**: Set a specific start and end date, or select **Always available**.
* **Shipping promise**: Set the estimated shipping time displayed on your storefront. Options include **Immediate delivery**, **Available on a specific date**, or **Rolling timeframes** (e.g., ships 10 days after order).

### Summary and save

Review your setup in the **Campaign summary** section. Once verified, click **Save**. Your campaign will go live at the scheduled time and begin accepting pre-orders.

## Customize display style

Click the **Customize style** button on the home page to tailor how the app appears on your storefront:

* **Visibility**: Choose to display on the product page, featured product sections, or collection pages.
* **Shipping info**: Toggle the display of the estimated shipping date.
* **Button text**: Customize your CTA (e.g., "Pre-order Now" or "Reserve Today").

## Manage campaigns

* **Monitor status**: View all active campaigns, delivery types, and payment modes in your **Campaign list**.
* **Quick adjustments**: Edit active campaigns or delete expired ones at any time.
* **Style preview**: We recommend viewing your storefront on both mobile and desktop after saving to ensure a seamless customer experience.

## FAQ

### Q1: If I restock and inventory becomes positive, will the pre-order button disappear?**
> **A**: Yes. When the system detects inventory > 0, it automatically switches the "Pre-order" button back to the standard "Buy Now" button.

### Q2: Why does my product still say "Sold Out" after setting up a campaign?**
> **A**: Please double-check your product settings in the admin panel to ensure "Continue selling when out of stock" is enabled.
