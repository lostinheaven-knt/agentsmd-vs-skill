# Earn points

**Genstore Loyalty** offers diverse ways to accumulate points through direct interactions in our online store and active participation on social media platforms. This system is categorized into:

- **Online store activities**: Everyday interactions like making purchases, registering an account, writing reviews, and collecting birthday rewards.
- **Social media engagements**: Activities such as liking our Facebook page, following our social media account, and sharing our products.

## **Ways to earn points**

In the **Earn points** section, you can view the set of point-earning rules that have been configured for your store. You can easily modify these rules by clicking the **Edit** button or toggle them on and off as needed.
