# Products

**Genstore** offers a robust product management system that makes it easy to sell a variety of products. Simply input product details and upload images to quickly create and showcase your products. Whether you’re selling items with multiple variants (like size and color) or products with a single variant, **Genstore** provides flexible multi-variant management for each combination, helping you maintain precise control over inventory and sales.

## Get started with product management

When you first visit the **Products** page, a welcome guide will appear to help you quickly build your product catalog. You can choose from the following options:

- **Add digital product**: Create a digital product, such as downloadable content like eBooks, music, or software.
- **Add physical product**: Add a physical product, ideal for items with inventory, sizes, colors, or other variants.
- **Import product / Import from Shopify**: Import products using an Excel template or sync your existing products directly from Shopify to speed up your setup.

Click any of the options to start creating your products. The system will guide you through entering key product details and uploading images.

## Key Benefits

- **Multi-channel sales support**: Publish products to multiple sales channels with just one click to expand market reach and boost brand visibility.
- **Flexible variant management**: Set up multiple variants for each product (such as size, color, etc.) and link them to inventory for accurate tracking.
- **Real-time inventory tracking**: The system updates inventory levels in real time, supports multi-warehouse management, and helps you stay on top of stock to prevent shortages or excess inventory.
- **Digital gift card feature**: Offer digital gift cards that not only increase sales conversion but also provide a more flexible shopping experience for your customers.
- **Automatic and manual product collections**: Easily manage different product collections with either automatic or manual collection features, improving operational efficiency.
