# Inventory

Inventory management is a crucial aspect of e-commerce operations, covering inventory tracking, purchasing, adjustments, and transfers. Genstore provides a comprehensive set of inventory management tools to help merchants efficiently monitor stock levels and maintain a smooth supply chain.

## Key benefits

- **Track inventory in real-time** – Monitor stock levels at any time to prevent overselling or stockouts.
- **Manage inventory across multiple locations** – Set up and track stock levels separately for warehouses, retail stores, and distribution points.
- **Inventory alerts** – Receive notifications when stock falls below a specified threshold.
- **Bulk inventory management** – Update multiple product inventories at once to improve efficiency.
- **Import and export inventory data** – Use CSV files to batch manage inventory for greater convenience.
- **Track inventory changes** – Maintain a record of all inventory adjustments for traceability.
- **Transfer inventory** – Move stock between different locations to optimize supply chain management.
