# **Genstore Growth plan**

The Genstore Growth plan provides full AI-powered support for store management and optimization. It includes all features from the Genstore Lite plan, plus lower transaction fees and a higher limit of dropshipping products to help you run your business more efficiently.

## Credits

- 200 monthly credits.
- 5 daily credits for daily logins.
- Extra credits available by purchasing a separate Credit Pack.

## **Plan features**

In addition to the  features available on all plans, and the features available on the Genstore Lite plan, the Genstore Growth plan offers the following features: 

### **Genstore AI**

| Feature            | Description                                                                                                |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Launch Agent**   | Fast-track foundational settings, including domains, payments, and shipping, to ensure a compliant launch. |
| **Product Agent**  | Manage your catalog efficiently with smart product sourcing and creation tools.                            |
| **Campaign Agent** | Trigger AI-driven marketing campaigns and generate discount codes to boost conversions.                    |
| **Design Agent**   | Generate homepages and switch themes instantly to build high-converting brand visuals.                     |
| **Analytics agent**     | Analyze traffic, conversion rates, and sales performance in real-time for actionable business insights.    |
| **Support agent**  | Your 24/7 dedicated merchant expert for technical guidance and operational advice.                         |

### **Additional features**

| Feature                | Description                                                                                   |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Lower Transaction Fees | Get reduced transaction fees. See the Genstore pricing page for details based on your region. |
| Dropshipping Products  | Push up to 100 dropshipping products to your store.                                           |
| Staff Accounts         | Support up to 10 staff accounts for better team collaboration and management.                 |
| Locations              | Manage your inventory across 15 locations effectively.                                        |
| Markets                | Include 3 markets and manage them from a single site.                                         |
