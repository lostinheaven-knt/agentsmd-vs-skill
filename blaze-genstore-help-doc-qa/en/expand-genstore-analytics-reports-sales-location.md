# Sales by billing location

The **Sales by billing location report** displays sales distribution based on the **country or region** specified in **order billing addresses**, providing insights into global market penetration and regional revenue contributions.

This report helps identify core markets and optimize international expansion strategies by analyzing purchasing patterns across geographical boundaries.

## Use this report to

- **Geographical sales analysis**: Evaluate sales distribution across countries, regions, and cities using billing location data.
- **Regional performance metrics**: Compare key metrics like **Total Sales**, **Orders**, **Shipping**, and **Taxes** to assess profitability and operational efficiency per region.
- **Drill-down capability**: Review detailed sales records filtered by billing location, including order-specific revenue and associated costs.

## Report data fields

The sales reports use several [common terms](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-sales.md#common-terms).

**Additional terms specific to this report**:

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Billing location** | Country or region derived from the customer’s billing address. |
