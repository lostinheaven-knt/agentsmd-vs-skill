# Customers over time

The **Customers over time** report shows how many customers made purchases during specific dates or months. Track who orders  products, services, courses, or subscriptions on your website.

Use the **Group By** dropdown to select a time unit (e.g., **daily, weekly, monthly**) and control how data is aggregated.

## Use this report to

- **Transaction customer growth trend analysis**: Identify increases or decreases in the number of transacting customers over time to evaluate marketing effectiveness and customer retention.
- **Multi-dimensional customer analysis**: Analyze changes in customer count across dimensions like market, region, channel, and payment method to uncover growth drivers and opportunities.
- **Customer transaction details**: Access granular data for each customer, including transaction frequency and amounts, to support targeted marketing and CRM strategies.
