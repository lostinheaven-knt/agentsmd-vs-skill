# Apply for an SMS sending number

To help you send SMS messages successfully, Genstore offers **free sending numbers** based on your target regions (multi-country support available):

- **United States & Canada**: toll-free numbers (TFN)
- **Europe**: branded sender IDs

You only need to complete a one-time registration to receive the appropriate number(s).

## How to enable SMS

You can enable SMS during the initial setup guide, or follow these steps:

1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Marketing -> SMS marketing**.
2. Click **Register SMS number for free**, or go to the top-right **Settings** -> **Register SMS number for free** to start registration.
3. Select the countries or regions where you plan to send messages (multi-select supported).
4. Click **Next**.
5. Provide a **branded sender ID** (for supported countries only).
6. Enter your business information, including company name and contact details.
7. Click **Submit** to complete your registration.

## Verification and approval

In some countries or regions, your SMS number must be approved by telecom carriers. They’ll review your business and sender ID details to ensure authenticity and compliance.

- Approval typically takes **15–30 business days**
- **No cost for registration or approval**
