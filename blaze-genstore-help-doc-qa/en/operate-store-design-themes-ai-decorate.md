# Design agent

Genstore integrates advanced AI-powered design features, offering two efficient ways to build your store:

* **AI homepage generation**: Quickly create a personalized homepage.
* **AI theme switching**: Instantly deploy a full theme template with one click, reducing design complexity and time.

## AI homepage generation

### Getting started and gathering requirements

* In Genstore merchant admin, click **Home** on the left navigation bar, then in the section above the chatbox, choose **AI Customization** -> **Design Store** to start the process. Then choose **AI Homepage Design**. Or simply type `AI homepage design` to begin.
* AI will first have a conversation with you to gather key information such as store type, brand style, and main content to showcase.

### Requirements analysis and style confirmation

* AI will intelligently analyze your input and generate a draft for you to review.
* You can adjust your preferred colors, layout, and content sections (e.g., banner, product displays, bestsellers).

### Homepage creation and optimization

* Once the style is confirmed, AI will automatically generate the complete homepage.
* At any time, you can enter new requests in the **AI chat box** (e.g., "Add a brand story section" or "Adjust banner colors"), and AI will optimize and regenerate the page until you're satisfied.

### Save and publish

* Once the generation is complete, click **Preview & Edit** to make updates.
* Under **More**, you can choose to **Save Draft** to save it for later or **Publish** to set it as your new homepage. If you'd like to start over, click **Restart** to clear the current design and begin the process again.

## AI theme switching

### Theme recommendations and selection

* Go to the quick-access menu **AI Customization** -> **Design Store** above the chatbox to begin. Then select **Change Theme**, or type `Change theme` to start the process.
* Based on your store’s category and business needs, AI will recommend a variety of popular theme templates and show you previews.

### One-click installation and configuration

* After selecting your preferred theme, click **Select**, and AI will automatically install and configure the full theme template for you.

### Publish and go live

* Once the installation is complete, AI will guide you to confirm the publication. You can either click **Publish** directly or use the chat to instruct AI (e.g., "Publish to my store now"), and AI will handle the publishing process.
* After the new theme goes live, you can continue to fine-tune the details using the template editor.
