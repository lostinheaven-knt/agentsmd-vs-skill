# Point expiration

In the **Point expiration** section of the **Genstore Loyalty** program, adjust the following options to encourage timely point usage:

- **Point expiration toggle**:
	- This function is off by default. When enabled, it causes points to expire automatically after the designated period.
- **Point validity period**:
	 - Points typically last 12 months, but you can customize this duration from 1 to 60 months based on your operational needs.
Make sure to click **Save** after making changes.

## Example of point expiration

Consider a customer who earns 300 points on January 1, 2025. The points begin to expire from March 1, 2025, with a one-year validity period:

- If no points are redeemed, all 300 points will expire by March 1, 2026.
- If 200 points are redeemed on May 1, any remaining points (100) will expire on the same date, March 1, 2026.

## Handle expirations

Once the point expiration is activated:

- **Points expiration**: The system checks daily for expired points and resets them to zero.
- **Email notification**: Customers receive timely reminders to redeem points before they expire, enhancing customer engagement and satisfaction. **Note**: The system sends a warning email **30 days** before the points expire and a final reminder **7 days** prior if the points remain unused.

## FAQs

### Q1. How is the validity period of customer points calculated?
> A: After activation, the expiration timer starts, and points will expire at the end of the set period. Daily checks reset expired points and notify customers nearing point expiration.

### Q2. What happens if I turn off the point expiration feature?
> A: Turning off the feature stops the calculation of point expiration, and all points previously issued remain valid indefinitely.
