# Checkout page

The **checkout page** is the final step before customers complete their order and payment. It includes key information such as order confirmation, address details, and payment options.

In **Genstore admin -> Settings -> Checkout**, you can customize the **login requirement** and **customer address fields** to collect necessary information while keeping the checkout process smooth.

## Login settings

- **Default**: Customers can check out as guests without logging in.
- **Login required**: Select **Login required before checkout** to require customers to log in before they can complete their order.
    > Tip: Requiring login may reduce order volume.
## Customer address

You can control which address fields appear on the checkout page and whether they are optional or required. This allows you to meet your business needs while maintaining a smooth checkout experience.

### Full name

- **Require first name only** (**Recommended**): Customers only need to enter their first name.
- **Require first and last name**: Customers must enter both first and last names.
### Address line 2 (apartment, unit, etc)

- **Hide**: The Address line 2 field will not be shown.
- **Visible, optional** (**Recommended**): Customers can choose whether to provide address line 2.
- **Visible, required**: Customers must enter this field to submit their order.
### Shipping address phone number

- **Hide**: The Phone number field will not be shown.
- **Visible, optional** (**Recommended**): Customers can choose whether to provide a phone number.
- **Visible, required**: Customers must provide a phone number to complete checkout.
### Company name

- **Hide** (**Recommended**): The Company name field will not be shown.
- **Visible, optional**: Customers can choose whether to provide their company name.
- **Visible, required**: Customers must enter their company name to submit their order.
