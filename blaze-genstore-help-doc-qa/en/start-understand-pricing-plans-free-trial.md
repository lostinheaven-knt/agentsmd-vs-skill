# Genstore Free plan

Genstore offers a **Free Plan** so you can **smoothly experience the full AI store-building workflow** before you’re ready to operate a live store. When you’re ready to launch, you can upgrade at any time.

The Free Plan has no time limit and is great for:

- Exploring how AI builds your store
- Trying different store styles, page layouts, and product presentation approaches
- Creating and previewing a store prototype you can share internally

## AI capabilities included

With Genstore Free Plan, you can use **all Genstore AI capabilities**:

| Feature            | Description                                                                                                |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Launch Agent**   | Fast-track foundational settings, including domains, payments, and shipping, to ensure a compliant launch. |
| **Product Agent**  | Manage your catalog efficiently with smart product sourcing and creation tools.                            |
| **Campaign Agent** | Trigger AI-driven marketing campaigns and generate discount codes to boost conversions.                    |
| **Design Agent**   | Generate homepages and switch themes instantly to build high-converting brand visuals.                     |
| **Analytics agent**     | Analyze traffic, conversion rates, and sales performance in real-time for actionable business insights.    |
| **Support agent**  | Your 24/7 dedicated merchant expert for technical guidance and operational advice.                         |

## Credits

- 5 free credits (one-time)
- 5 daily credits for daily logins

## Create a store

1. Go to the [Genstore signup page](https://accounts.genstore.ai/account/signup/), enter your email and password, then click **Sign up**.
2. In the chat, describe what you want to build (e.g., product category, store style, store language).
3. Based on your request, AI will ask you to select dropshipping products or upload your own products.
4. After products are selected or uploaded, AI will generate a fully previewable store.

## Store limit

- Each account can create up to **2 Free Plan stores**.
- You can use different stores to compare:
  - different store styles and positioning approaches
  - different layouts and product presentation approaches
- Once you reach the Free Plan store limit, you can upgrade to create additional stores.

## Usage scope & limits

### Products & resources

- **Products**: Up to **20** products
- **Dropshipping products**: Up to **10**
- **Asset storage**: **500MB** (for basic use; deletion not supported)
- **Email sending**: Up to **20 emails** total (including marketing emails and test emails)
- **Marketing Automation (MA)**: You can create workflows, but **cannot activate** them
- **Additional staff accounts**: Not supported
- **Inventory locations**: Single inventory location
- **Localized Markets**: Single localized market
- **Apps**: You can install apps for free; to subscribe to paid apps, please upgrade to a paid plan

### Payments & transactions

- The Free Plan **does not support live online transactions**
- You can’t enable **Genstore Payments** or **third-party online payments**
- Customers can only place **$0 orders**, or use a **manual payment method** you add

If you want to start selling and collecting payments, please upgrade to a paid plan.

### Branding & access control

- **Genstore branding**: Free Plan stores display the “Made with Genstore” badge; it can be removed after upgrading
- **Store access**: Free Plan stores are password-protected by default. If you want to share access, you can share the **store password** (the first **20 visitor IPs per day** can access without a password), or upgrade to a paid plan
- **Auto domain connection**: The Free Plan does not support automatic custom domain connection

## Upgrade from the Free Plan

Consider upgrading when you want to:

- Create more stores
- Expand product and dropshipping scale
- Enable payments, domains, and multiple markets
- Remove Genstore branding and start live operations

You can upgrade anytime to **Lite / Growth / Scale**. After upgrading, everything you created in the Free Plan will be **fully preserved**.
