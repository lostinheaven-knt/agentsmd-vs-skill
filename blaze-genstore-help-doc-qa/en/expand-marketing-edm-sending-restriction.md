# Sending restrictions

To keep Genstore’s email system healthy and to protect deliverability for all merchants, we may temporarily **pause your ability to send emails** if we detect unusual or risky sending behavior.
This guide explains what typically triggers a restriction and how you can get it resolved quickly.

## What can trigger a sending restriction?

Your email sending may be paused if we see any of the following:

### **1. High-risk or inappropriate content**

The email includes content that is unsafe or not allowed.

### **2. Deliverability issues**

If your email performance shows unusual spikes or poor engagement, it may signal that recipients didn’t expect your messages. Common indicators include:

* High **hard bounce** rate
* High **soft bounce** rate
* High **spam complaint** rate
* High **unsubscribe** rate
* Sudden jumps in your sending volume

These issues often mean your list needs cleaning or your content needs adjustment.

## How to get your sending access restored

Sending access does **not** automatically resume.
If you believe the restriction was applied in error, email **[support@genstore.ai](mailto:support@genstore.ai)** with the following:

1. **What type of emails you were sending**
   For example: promotions, newsletters, customer updates, order-related messages, etc.
2. **Where your contact list came from**
   Examples: customer signups, past orders, event registrations, or other opt-in sources.
3. **What improvements you’ll make going forward**
   This helps us confirm you’re taking steps to protect deliverability. You can include actions such as:
   * Filter your recipients
   * Reducing sending frequency
   * Optimize your email content
   * Remove invalid email addresses
   * Reviewing your opt-in process, only emailing contacts who have clearly opted in

Once we receive your information, our team will review your request and restore sending if everything meets our policies.

## What we look at during review

Our review focuses on:
* Whether your email content aligns with Genstore’s acceptable-use guidelines
* Whether your sending domain appears on major blocklists (like Spamhaus)
* Whether your account shows signs of high-risk behavior (phishing, impersonation, bulk spam)

If serious violations are found, sending cannot be restored.

## When sending cannot be restored

We’re unable to lift restrictions if:
* Your sending domain is listed on a global blocklist
* Your emails involve impersonation, fraud, or deceptive content
* Your sending activity harms Genstore’s system reputation or stability
* Your messages clearly resemble spam or misleading promotions
