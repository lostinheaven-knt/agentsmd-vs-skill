# Email templates

To help you create and send emails more efficiently, we offer a variety of pre-built templates covering common marketing scenarios. Simply choose a template, make minor edits, and quickly generate professional emails.

## Template categories

Our email templates are divided into the following categories:

### Basic templates

- **Letter**: A simple template for daily communication, notifications, and other general messages.
- **Newsletter**: A text-and-image mixed layout for regular newsletters, event announcements, and updates.
- **Blank**: A fully customizable template for creating your own design from scratch.

### Designed templates

- **Notification**: Ideal for important announcements and event invitations.
- **Holiday**: Festive-themed templates for holiday marketing and greetings.
- **Product highlight**: Designed to showcase new products and key promotions.
- **Discount**: Marketing templates for sending promotional offers and discounts.

### Custom templates

Save and reuse your own designed templates for future campaigns.

## How to find a designed template

1. Log in to your Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Marketing** -> **Email marketing** -> **Create email campaign**.
3. Choose a template.

## FAQs

### Q1. How do I choose the right template?
> A: Select a template based on your email’s purpose and audience. For example, use a discount template for promotions or a holiday template for seasonal greetings.

### Q2. Can I customize the templates?
> A: Yes, all templates are fully editable. You can modify the content, colors, and layout as needed.

### Q3. How do I save a custom template?
> A: After editing an email, go to the email marketing campaign list, select **...** -> click **Save as template** to store your design for future use.

### Q4. Are the templates mobile-friendly?
> A: Yes, all templates are optimized for a seamless display across different devices.
