# Quick launch checklist

Ensure your online store is quickly launched and running smoothly. Please refer to the following checklist.

## AI inquiry

After you finish initializing your store, [AI will automatically start an inquiry](./start-genstore-account-homepage.md) to learn about your business and recommend more targeted, efficient setup tasks.

::: tip  
AI inquiries will start in your account language. With advanced LLM technology, Genstore can automatically detect and switch to the language you’re using, ensuring smooth and natural communication.  
:::

### Inquiry topics

- Your business foundation and preparation
- Your traffic channels and promotional goals
- Your business experience
- Your reasons for choosing Genstore

## Basic setup

Before you begin configuring your store and launching promotions, complete the following basic setup to ensure your store information is accurate and your team has the right access:

- [Set up store information](./start-genstore-account-create-manage.md): Add your store name, logo, contact details, and business address
- Set up [time zone](./start-genstore-account-create-manage#manage-store-time-zone) and [currency](./start-genstore-account-create-manage#select-store-currency): Ensure order and financial data are displayed accurately
- [Add staff accounts](./start-genstore-account-roles-permissions.md): Create user accounts and assign permissions to team members
- [Set store language](./settings-languages.md) and [translations](./global-market-language-translate.md): Configure your default language and enable other language versions (for multilingual stores)
- [Choose a pricing plan](./start-understand-pricing-plans.md): Review and select the plan that fits your business to activate your store

## Website building tasks

After the inquiry, the AI will generate customized tasks to help you prepare your store for operations, including:

### Online sales

- [Physical products](./operate-product-physical.md): Add or import at least one product
- [Digital products](./operate-product-digital.md): Add or import at least one digital product
- [Store migration](./start-migrate-guide.md): Import products, orders, and customers using the Genstore migration tool

### Website basic configuration

- Choose a [theme template](./operate-store-design-themes.md) or [let AI generate a homepage](./start-genstore-account-homepage.md)
- [Link to social media](./start-genstore-account-brand-assets#social-media-links) (e.g., Instagram, Facebook, X, TikTok)
- Link to [e-commerce platforms](./expand-sales-channels.md) (e.g., Amazon, TikTok)
- Set up a [shipping profile](./operate-fulfillment-shipping-profile-shipping-rate.md)
- Set up [payment methods](./operate-payments.md)

### Launch your store

- [Connect your domain](./global-domains.md)

### Promotion and marketing

- Promote your store via SEO and advertising
- Set up [marketing automation](./expand-marketing-automated-email-overview.md)

After completing the steps above, your store will be ready to go live, attract traffic, and start converting visitors into customers.
