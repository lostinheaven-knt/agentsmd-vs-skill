# Deactivate your Genstore store

As the **store owner**, if your store meets the required conditions, you can cancel your subscription and deactivate your store at any time via the Genstore admin.

Before deactivating your store, please make sure to read and understand the [Terms of Service](https://www.genstore.ai/legal/terms) to ensure you're aware of the impact.

::: tip

Stores on a free trial cannot be deactivated manually.


:::

## Cancel your subscription and deactivate your store

::: tip

Deactivating your store will immediately cancel your subscription. You will lose access to the admin. To reactivate the store later, you’ll need to re-subscribe to a paid plan.

:::

### Steps to deactivate your store:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Settings -> Billing.**
3. In the **Subscription** section, click **Deactivate store.**
4. Select a reason for closing, and optionally provide additional comments.
5. Click **Continue** to confirm the deactivation.

After the store is deactivated, you can still access and download past invoices.
 Your store data — including sales, product, and customer information — will be retained for **two years**, allowing you to reactivate your store at any time.

## Reactivate your store

If you decide to reopen your store, you can log in at any time and choose a plan to reactivate.

### Steps to reactivate:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and select your deactivated store.
2. Click **Continue subscription** or **Change plan.**
3. Complete the payment to reactivate your store.
