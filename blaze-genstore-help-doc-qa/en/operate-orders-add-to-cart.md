# Shopping cart

The **shopping cart** is a temporary space where consumers store items they plan to purchase. Consumers can add one or multiple items at once (up to 99 items). When they’re ready to checkout, they can submit the entire cart for purchase.

## Add items to the cart

To add items to the cart, the following conditions must be met:

- The item must be listed and available for sale.
- The item must have sufficient stock.

**How to add items**:

1. Select the items you want to purchase in the your online store.
2. In the **product list** or **product details page**, click **Add to cart**, and the item will be automatically added to the cart.

## View items in your cart

Once added to the cart, you can view the items at any time. If an item is no longer available, it will be automatically removed from the cart.

**How to view your cart**:

1. Go to your Genstore online store.
2. Click the `cart` icon to view all the items in your cart.

## Modify item quantities

You can adjust the quantity of items in your cart. If you change the quantity to zero, the item will automatically be removed.

**How to modify quantities**:

1. Go to your Genstore online store.
2. Click the `cart` icon to view your cart.
3. Click the `+ / -` button next to an item to adjust the quantity. If the quantity is set to zero, the item will be removed from the cart.

## Remove items from the cart

You can delete items you no longer need from your cart.

**How to remove items**:

1. Go to your Genstore online store.
2. Click the cart icon to view your cart.
3. Click the **Delete** button next to the item you want to remove.
