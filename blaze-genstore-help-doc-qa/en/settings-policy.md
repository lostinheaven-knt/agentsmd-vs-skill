# Set up store policies

You can add or create the following policies for your Genstore store:

- Return and refund policy
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- Shipping policy
- Contact information

## Add a policy

### Steps:

1. In the Genstore admin, go to **Settings** -> **Policies**.
2. Click the policy you want to add or create.
3. Enter your store policy, or click **Insert template** to generate the default template. Use the rich text editor to style your policy, add links, and insert images.
4. Review the policy and make any changes if needed.
5. Click **Confirm** to publish the policy.

::: tip

- While Genstore can generate templates, make sure to comply with all relevant laws and regulations when adding any policy. Be sure to carefully review each policy before posting.
- After adding your store policies, the policy links will automatically appear in the footer of the checkout page. For easier access, it’s a good idea to add these links to your store menu before checkout.

:::
