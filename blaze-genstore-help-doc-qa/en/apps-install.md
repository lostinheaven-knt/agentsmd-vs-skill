# Install an app

Once you’ve found the app you want, you can easily add it to your store through the Genstore app store. Follow these steps to install and get started.

## Install an app from the Genstore app store

### Steps:

1. **Log in**: Sign in to your Genstore merchant account.
2. **Find the app**: Browse or search for the app you want, then click on its details page.
3. **Review details**: Check the app’s features and user reviews to ensure it meets your needs.
4. **Click install**: On the app page, click the **Install** button.
5. **Authorize the app**: You’ll be redirected to your Genstore admin. Click **Install** to grant the app access to your store data.
6. **Complete installation**: After installation, the app will appear in your **Apps** section of the Genstore admin.

## Next steps

1. **View installed apps**: Go to the **Apps** page in your Genstore admin to see the list of apps you’ve installed.
2. **Start using the app**: Click on the app icon and follow the instructions to start using it and boost your store’s performance.
