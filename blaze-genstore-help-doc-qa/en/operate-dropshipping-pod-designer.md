# Express design

The express design mode is the central creative hub of Genstore POD. Powered by "real-time rendering" technology, it enables merchants to complete bulk designs and previews across multiple product categories in record time.

## Flexible design workflows

The system offers two creative paths to suit different design habits:

* **A: Start from the "Design studio" (Recommended)**: Create directly within the **Design studio** on the left side of the **Print On Demand** page. As you upload images or edit text, **all products in the list on the right will sync and render** your design effects in real-time.
* **B: Start from a "specific product"**: Select a specific item from the product list on the right and click **Start design**. The system will open the express designer by default, using that specific product as your canvas.

## Core design and interactions

Operations within the express designer provide instant feedback across the entire product line:

| Functional module | Operation description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Upload image** | Select image from the asset library or your local device. Supports free **movement, scaling (drag-to-resize), and deletion** within the canvas. |
| **Edit text** | Input custom text. Supports selection of **fonts, colors, sizing, and rotation angles**. |
| **Templates** | **Custom templates**: Save your current design for easy reuse on similar products later.<br>**System templates**: Use built-in design assets with support for resizing and rotation. |
| **Canvas settings** | **Canvas dimension**: 3 dimension options to fit different product design requirements.<br>**Canvas fill**: Toggle between black, white, grey, and transparent backgrounds. |
| **Reset** | **One-click clear**: Resets only the current editing area without affecting designs already rendered on product display images. |
| **Preview** | Once the design is complete, click **Preview** to see the actual appearance in the **default print position** of the selected product. |

### Fine-tuning
After completing a design through the creative center, if you are unsatisfied with the rendering on a specific item, you can **click the design icon on that product card** to enter a fine-tuning mode for that single product.

## Publishing and management

Once your design is finalized, click **Push products** to sync them to your store.

* **Default attribute sync**: The system automatically pushes all available color and size options for the product.
* **Printing method**: If a product supports multiple methods (e.g., DTG or embroidery), the system will **default to the first printing method** and place it in a fixed print position.

## Pricing logic

The final product price is automatically calculated based on your **[Dropshipping pricing settings](./operate-dropshipping-product-management.md)**, including:
* Your preset fixed markup amount or markup percentage;
* Whether estimated shipping costs are included.
