# Manage your Genstore plan

During store operations, you may need to adjust your subscription based on business needs, such as:

- Preparing to launch and unlocking more sales or payment features
- Handling high traffic during marketing campaigns
- Downgrading to a basic plan to reduce costs

You can manage your plan anytime from **Settings -> Plan and credits** in the Genstore admin, including upgrading/downgrading your subscription or canceling it to close your store.
