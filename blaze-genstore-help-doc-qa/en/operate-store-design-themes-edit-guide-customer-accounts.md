# Customer accounts

The customer accounts pages allow shoppers to register, log in, view, and update personal information. They are essential for building a customer system and delivering personalized services. Genstore provides standard customer account pages with basic style customization, suitable for B2C merchants.

## Page types

The system includes the following customer account pages (each supports separate template settings):

- **Register**: Customers can create an account by filling in details or using a social login.
- **Login**: Supports email/password login or third-party accounts.
- **Reset password**: Customers can reset passwords after email verification.
- **Address management**: View, add, edit, and delete shipping addresses.
- **Account homepage**: View profile details, order history, and manage addresses.

## How to access

1. Log in to **Genstore admin**.
2. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Themes**.
3. Find your theme and click **Customize** to open the theme editor.
4. In the top navigation bar, open the page selector and choose **Customer accounts**.
5. Expand **Customer accounts** and select the page template you want to edit.
6. Click the template to edit sections and blocks in the left panel, and adjust styles in the right panel.

## Style and layout settings

Some customer account pages support basic style adjustments to optimize visual presentation. Common options include:

- **Title style**: Adjust heading size.
- **Layout style**: Choose horizontal or vertical layout.
- **Card mode**: Enable to add borders and shadows.
- **Section padding**: Adjust top and bottom spacing.

**Tip**: Available options vary by page. Check the right panel in the editor to confirm supported settings, and preview changes before publishing.
