# Understanding your Genstore bill

Your Genstore bill is a record of charges based on your store’s activities, subscription plan, and services used. It includes details on what you need to pay, such as subscription fees, transaction fees, and taxes. Genstore emails each bill to store owners so they can stay informed and pay on time. You can check your billing history and upcoming charges anytime in the Genstore admin.

## What’s included in your bill

Your bill may include:

- Your store’s subscription fees
- Third-party transaction fees (e.g., [external payment providers](./operate-payments-third-party.md))
- Taxes (if applicable)
- Other fees, like [app](./apps-overview.md) charges

## How to view your bill

To access your billing history, go to **Settings** -> **Billing** in the Genstore admin.

- **Billing history**: Review past bills.
- **Current billing cycle**: See upcoming charges and update billing details. You can edit payment methods, currency preferences, and tax settings (if applicable) to keep your billing info accurate.
