# Your store setup

Setting up and launching your store on Genstore involves two main phases:

- [Set up your store](#set-up-your-store)
- [Finalize store compliance and go-live readiness](#finalize-store-compliance-and-go-live-readiness)

## Set up your store

Lay the groundwork for your store by preparing content and setting up page structure:

- Register and create your store
- Complete basic store information
- Add staff accounts
- Set up your domain
- Add products
- Customize and publish your storefront

For a full walkthrough, see [Set up your store](./start-store-setup-basic.md).

## Finalize store compliance and go-live readiness

Complete store policies, shipping, and payment setup, run tests, and prepare for launch:

- Create policy pages (e.g., privacy, returns)
- Configure shipping and tax rules
- Set up payment and checkout
- Preview and test your store
- Place test orders
- Launch your store

For detailed steps, see [Finalize store compliance and go-live readiness](./start-store-setup-launch-prep.md).

By completing these two setup phases, your Genstore store will be fully equipped for sales, operations, and customer support—ready to begin your journey to success!
