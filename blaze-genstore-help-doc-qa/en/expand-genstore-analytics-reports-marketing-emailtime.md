# Email campaign engagement over time

The **Email campaign engagement over time** report allows you to **aggregate results from all email** marketing campaigns and observe how specific metrics change over time. Select a time period and choose the metrics you wish to track.

## Use this report to

- **Analyze overall email marketing trends**
	Monitor key metrics like open rates and click-through rates across different time periods to assess the effectiveness of your campaigns over time. For example, a spike in open rates during a specific period might be linked to a holiday promotion or a particularly engaging subject line.
- **Identify the impact of content and send timing**
	If you notice unusual changes in the number of clicks or unsubscribes during a specific period, review the content, theme, or timing of the emails sent at that time. These insights can help you optimize future campaign schedules and messaging strategies.

## Report data fields

- Unique opens
- Unique clicks
- Number of Bounced Emails
- Number of Unsubscribes

For detailed definitions, refer to [EDM campaign performance report - common terminology](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-marketing-email#report-data-fields).
