# Choose the right app

Genstore’s app store offers a wide range of apps to help grow your business. To choose the best app, consider these key questions:

### 1. Does it fill a functionality gap?

Are there any areas where your store needs more features beyond Genstore’s core functions? For example:

- Do you need to improve efficiency in certain tasks?
- Are there features you’re missing that could help your store?

### 2. Can it help drive sales growth?

To boost your sales, think about:

- **Traffic generation**: Do you need help with SEO or targeted ads to bring in more customers?
- **Promotions**: Do you need regular discounts or campaigns to encourage purchases?

### 3. Will it simplify store management?

Are there time-consuming tasks you’d like to automate? For example:

- **Order management**: Do you need tools for bulk shipping labels or automatic inventory updates?
- **Data analysis**: Do you need tools to track sales and trends in real time?

Answering these questions will help you choose apps that can improve your store’s efficiency.

## App reviews

When choosing an app, pay attention to reviews and ratings from other merchants. Reviews give you a better understanding of an app’s strengths, weaknesses, and whether it suits your business needs.

### Star ratings: A quick look at satisfaction

- Star ratings range from one to five, showing overall satisfaction. This helps you quickly gauge the app’s quality.

### Review system: Ensuring real feedback

- **Who can leave a review**: Only active Genstore merchants (not trial users) who have installed or recently uninstalled the app can review it. This ensures the feedback is genuine.
- **Review limits & edits**: Merchants can leave one review per app and edit it if needed to keep the information current.

Merchant reviews are a key factor in choosing the right app for your business. By reading these reviews, you’ll get a clearer picture of the app’s real-world performance.
