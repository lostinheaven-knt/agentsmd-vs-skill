# TikTok sales channel

Genstore’s TikTok sales channel lets you promote and sell products by syncing them to your TikTok storefront. Customers can browse, place orders, and complete payments directly within TikTok. With support for live shopping, short-form video promotions, and product showcase features, you can reach potential buyers through multiple touchpoints and boost product visibility and conversion.

:::tip
**The TikTok channel is a built-in system app** that is automatically installed after completing the initial store setup, requiring no manual installation. You can find this channel in the **Sales channels** page of your merchant admin.
:::

## Key benefits

- Sync your products to TikTok for direct discovery and shopping.
- Use TikTok’s ad ecosystem to maximize reach and performance.
- Gain insight with TikTok Pixel to optimize your ad campaigns.

## TikTok account requirements

To use the TikTok sales channel, you’ll need to create or connect the following accounts:

- **TikTok for Business account**: Offers access to ad creation, audience engagement tools, and creative asset management. You can:
	- Create and manage ads in TikTok Ads Manager.
	- Manage your brand in TikTok Business Center.
	- Use organic TikTok videos for Spark Ads.
	  > Note: You can convert or connect your personal TikTok account when setting up a Business account.
- **TikTok Business Center account**: Centralized platform for managing organizational roles, permissions, and assets. A Business account is required to access it.
- **TikTok Ads Manager account**: Used to create ad campaigns, manage creatives, view reports, and optimize performance.

## Set up TikTok sales channel and complete product sync

1. Log in to your Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Sales channels** -> **TikTok**.
3. Click **Connect TikTok account** to start the setup process.
4. Follow the prompts to connect the required accounts:
	- Business Center
	- Ads Manager
5. Set up data sharing:
	- Select or create a TikTok Pixel to track performance and conversions.
6. Click **Complete setup** to finish connecting the TikTok sales channel.
7. Once the review is complete, products in your store that have the TikTok sales channel configured will be automatically synced to the TikTok product catalog. 
   > For instructions on how to configure sales channels for products, please refer to [Configuring Sales Channels for Product](./operate-product-create#sales-channels).

## Disconnect TikTok account

If you no longer want to use the TikTok sales channel:

1. Log in to Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Sales channels** -> **TikTok**.
3. Click **Settings** (top right).
4. Expand the TikTok account settings and click **Disconnect**.

## Unlink TikTok Pixel

1. Log in to Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Sales channels** -> **TikTok**.
3. Click **Settings** (top right).
4. Expand the **Data sharing** section.
5. Click **Unlink** next to the TikTok Pixel to remove or replace it.
