# Shipping

The **Shipping report** provides a detailed overview of shipping fees for orders within a selected time period, including **shipping discounts and taxes** applied to shipping fees.

## Report data fields

| Term | Definition |
| ---- | ---------- |
| **Date/time** | The timestamp when the customer placed the order, added/updated items, or modified shipping fees. |
| **Sales channel** | The platform or sales channel associated with the order or return. |
| **Shipping fee** | The total shipping cost for the order, including discounts and taxes. |
| **Shipping discounts** | The discount amount applied to the shipping fee for the order. |
| **Shipping taxes** | The tax amount applied to the shipping fee for the order. |
| **Net shipping** | The net revenue from shipping fees, calculated as `Shipping fee - Discounts - Taxes`. |
