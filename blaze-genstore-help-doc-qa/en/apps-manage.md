# Manage installed apps

Genstore apps provide various features to enhance your store. You can manage them easily from your Genstore admin, including opening apps, pinning them for quick access, and reviewing privacy policies.

## Launch an app in Genstore

You can access installed apps from the **Apps** page in your Genstore admin.

### Steps

1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Apps**.
2. Find the app you want to open and click its name.
3. On the app page, click **Launch app** at the top.

## Pin an app to the Genstore admin

Pinning an app to the navigation menu makes it easier to access frequently used apps.

### Steps

1. Log in to your Genstore admin and open the app details page.
2. Click the pin icon in the top-right corner to add the app to your navigation menu.
3. To unpin the app, click the pin icon in the navigation menu to remove it.

## View an app’s privacy policy

You can check an app’s permissions and privacy policy from the **About** page.

### Steps

1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Apps**.
2. Click the app’s name from the list and click **View details**.
3. In the app details page, scroll to the **Permission details** section.
4. Click **View privacy policy** to see details about the app's permissions and data usage.
