# "Made with Genstore" branding badge

By default, the **“Made with Genstore”** brand attribution appears in the footer (bottom-right corner) of your store theme. This attribution helps build platform brand awareness and shows visitors which platform your store is built on.  
If you are on a paid subscription plan, this attribution is automatically hidden so that only your own brand is displayed.

## Why it matters

- **Professional appearance**: Removing the attribution ensures that only your brand elements are shown, making the storefront look more polished.
- **Stronger independence**: Customers see only your brand, without confusion from the platform label.
- **Exclusive benefit**: As an exclusive benefit for paid subscribers, this feature is automatically activated. Available to paid subscribers as part of advanced customization features.

## Brand attribution: Automatic hiding and restoring display

::: tip
Trial merchants must first upgrade to a paid subscription plan to hide the brand attribution. It will then be hidden automatically.
::: 

- **Automatic Hiding**: Upon subscribing to a paid plan, the Genstore brand attribution is automatically hidden, and no further action is required from you.
- **Restoring the Display (If needed)**:
    If you have a specific need to show the "Made with Genstore" attribution, please follow these steps:
    - In your Genstore admin, go to **Online Store** -> **Preferences**.
    - Turn the toggle switch for **Remove branding** off. 
    - Save your changes.

::: tip

- Removing the attribution only affects the storefront display. It has no impact on your admin settings or SEO.
- After saving your changes, the setting will take effect on the next store visit. If you don’t see the update, try refreshing the page.

:::
