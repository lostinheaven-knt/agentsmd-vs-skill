# Store notifications

You can send notifications to both customers and staff through your store. All notifications and their details are available for viewing and management in the **Settings -> Notifications** section of the Genstore admin.

## Manage sender

To ensure your store’s email notifications are sent properly, you need to verify a valid sender email address.

### Steps:

1. In Genstore admin, go to **Settings** -> **Notifications**.
2. Click **Manage sender**.
3. Enter the sender name, email address, reply email address, and verify the email address.
4. Click **Save**.

::: tip
By default, the sender name is set to your store name, and the sender email will appear as store+123@genstoremail.ai. We recommend using a branded email address as your sender email to present a professional image and increase brand recognition. [Learn how to update your sender email.](./settings-notifications-email#update-your-sender-email)
:::


## Set up notification channels

- Customer-facing store notifications support email, with some also supporting SMS.
- Staff-facing store notifications support email, with some also supporting store admin notifications.

### Steps:

1. In Genstore admin, go to **Settings** -> **Notifications**.
2. Find the notification you want to set up.
3. Use the **toggle** to turn the corresponding channel on or off.
