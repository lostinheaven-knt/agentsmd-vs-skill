# SMS marketing

Genstore SMS is a visual marketing tool that helps you connect with customers through targeted text messages, driving engagement, conversions, and repeat sales. With support for custom languages and regions, built-in tracking, and flexible controls, it’s ideal for everything from new customer activation to loyalty-driven re-engagement.

## Key features

- **Quick setup, instant results**
	Get started in minutes—no coding required. New users receive a **$1 free credit** to test SMS campaigns before switching to paid billing.
- **Real-time performance dashboard**
	Track key metrics at a glance:
	- Conversations: traffic brought by SMS
	- Conversion rate: how many messages lead to orders
	- Orders & revenue: data about converted orders
	- Filter by date range for deeper analysis
- **Powerful campaign management**
	- Create multiple SMS campaigns
	- Filter and manage campaign status in one place

## Sending rules & compliance settings

### Sending rules

- **Brand name display**: Show your store name in messages
- **Quiet hours**: Avoid sending texts at night based on customer time zones
- **Send limits**: Control how many texts go to the same customer each day

### Opt-out compliance

Include unsubscribe instructions to meet U.S. and international standards, such as:

- `Reply STOP to opt-out`
- `Unsubscribe {{unsubscribe_link}}`

Genstore adds unsubscribe links automatically to help you stay compliant with TCPA, CTIA, and GDPR where applicable.

### Tracking & consent

- **Click tracking**: Monitor engagement to measure campaign impact
- **Double opt-in**: Optional extra consent layer before sending messages
