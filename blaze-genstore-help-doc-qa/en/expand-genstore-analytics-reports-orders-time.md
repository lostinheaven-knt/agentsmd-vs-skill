# Orders over time

The **Orders over time report** clearly displays the total order volume across customizable time ranges.

This report enables multidimensional analysis **by product category, sales channel, payment method, region, and multi-market configurations** to reveal order trends. Detailed exportable lists support granular analysis and data-driven decision making.

Additionally, you can filter orders by **payment status to track status-specific trends.

## Use this report to

- **Identify order trends**: Detect order volume fluctuations through time series analysis, including seasonal patterns and campaign impacts.
- **Analyze across dimensions**: Examine order distribution patterns by business dimensions (product categories, sales channels, payment methods, regions, markets).
- **Compare market performance**: Benchmark order metrics across regions/markets to identify growth opportunities.
