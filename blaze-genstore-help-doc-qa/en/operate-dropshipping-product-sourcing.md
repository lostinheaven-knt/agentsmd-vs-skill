# Genstore marketplace

Genstore offers two Dropshipping models. If you wish to sell pre-existing, standardized products, navigate to **Dropshipping** -> **Marketplace** in the Genstore admin.

> If you are looking to build a brand IP or offer customized products, please use our [Print on Demand (POD) solution](./operate-dropshipping-pod.md).

In the Standard Dropshipping model, product selection determines the competitiveness of your store. The **Genstore Dropshipping Marketplace** helps you efficiently discover and import high-quality products through systematic filtering and AI-driven recommendations — enabling low-inventory, high-efficiency operations.

## Key benefits

* **Discover high-potential products** – Access a large and continuously updated product catalog, enhanced by market data and algorithmic analysis to highlight trending and high-performance products.
* **Multi-dimensional filters** – Narrow down results by cost, warehouse, shipping country, or product category to match your business strategy.
* **Smart AI recommendations** – The system uses AI models to analyze product popularity, trends, and similarity, continuously optimizing recommendations to support better sourcing decisions.

## Page overview

### Filter panel

On the left side of the **Dropshipping** -> **Marketplace** page, you can refine your search using multiple filter conditions:

* **Best sellers** – Filter top-selling and trending items based on your store category, market performance, and AI recommendations, making it easier to spot the best-selling opportunities.
* **Cost range** – Set a minimum and maximum product cost.
* **Ship from** – Filter products by warehouse or shipping origin.
* **All categories** – Filter by main or secondary product category.

You can also search by keyword. As you type, the system provides **real-time keyword suggestions** to help you quickly locate relevant items.

### Product list

The system displays products that best match your selected filters and search terms.
By default, results are sorted by overall relevance and overall rating, but you can also sort by:

* **Product cost** (low to high / high to low)
* **Newest arrivals** (latest first)

Each page shows 40 products by default, with infinite scrolling to load more results.

### Product cards

Each product card includes key product details:

* Product image and title
* Product cost and shipping country
* Actions for favorite, push, or view details

#### Available actions

| Action | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Push product** | Add selected products to your product list. By default, they will be set to **Active** and ready for sale. You can also adjust the status to **Draft** in [Settings](./operate-dropshipping-product-management.md) to optimize the product page before making it live. |
| **Add to favorites** | Save potential products for later review and management. |
| **View details** | Open the product details page to view detailed product information, logistics data, and related recommendations. |

> Different subscription plans support different limits on the number of products you can push. Please refer to [Pricing plans](./start-understand-pricing-plans.md) for details.

### Product details include

* **Basic information** – Variant cost, estimated price (based on your [pricing rules](./operate-dropshipping-pricing.md)), product descriptions, and inventory.
* **Logistics and delivery** – Shipping profiles, estimated shipping costs, and delivery times by region.
* **Recommended products** – AI-recommended products that are similar or complementary to your selection, helping you expand your sourcing strategy.

## FAQs

### Q1. How can I find trending products?

> A: Check the **Best sellers** section under **Featured filters**. The system recommends trending products based on sales data and algorithmic scoring.

### Q2. What happens after I push a product to my store?

> A: Pushed products appear in the **Products** module, where you can edit product details, pricing, and sales channels before publishing.
> For detailed guidance, see [Product management guide](./operate-dropshipping-product-management.md).

### Q3. Any tips for using the price filter?

> A: Start with a wide range to explore broadly, then narrow it down to identify products with the best value-to-cost ratio.
