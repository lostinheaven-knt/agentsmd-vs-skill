# Domain

A domain name is the URL or web address that customers use to find your online store. Choosing a domain name that matches your brand helps build trust with your customers. You can find your current store URL on the **Domains** page.

By default, when you register a Genstore store, your online store will be assigned the `.genmystore.com` URL. If you want to change the URL displayed to customers visiting your store, you can **change your `.genmystore.com` domain name** (note: you can only make this change once); add a custom domain name (like `www.example.com`), for example, a domain purchased from any **third-party domain provider**. Once you add a domain name to Genstore, you can manage your store’s domain settings on the **Domains** page in the Genstore admin.
