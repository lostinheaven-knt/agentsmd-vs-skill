# Search conversions over time

If you use Genstore’s storefront search feature, the **Search conversions over time** report shows the conversion performance of storefront searches within the selected timeframe, helping you understand how your store converts searches into sales.

## Data refresh & distribution

- **Refresh frequency**: Data in this report is **updated daily**, but there may be a delay of 12 to 72 hours.
- **Data structure**: The report uses a **funnel model**, where:
  - Sessions `≥` Sessions with clicks
  - Sessions with clicks `≥` Sessions with add-to-carts
  - Sessions with add-to-carts `≥` Sessions with purchases

You can group data by day, week, or month, and compare performance across different date ranges.

## Use this report to

- **Search conversion trends**: Analyze how effectively search traffic converts into sales over time.
- **Funnel analysis**: Identify bottlenecks in the conversion journey (e.g., high drop-off between clicks and add-to-cart).
- **Optimize search relevance**: Use insights to refine search algorithms or product tagging to improve click-through and purchase rates.

## Report data fields

The report includes the following columns:

| Term | Definition |
| ---- | ---------- |
| **Date** | The date when customer interactions occurred. |
| **Sessions with search** | Total visits where customers performed a search. |
| **Sessions with clicks** | Visits where customers clicked a product from search results and viewed its page. |
| **Sessions with add-to-cart** | Visits where customers clicked a product from search results and added it to their cart. |
| **Sessions with purchase** | Visits where customers clicked a product from search results, added it to the cart, and completed a purchase. |
| **Click rate (CTR)** | Percentage of search sessions that led to product page views. |
| **Add-to-cart rate** | Percentage of search sessions that resulted in adding a product to the cart. |
| **Purchase rate** | Percentage of search sessions that converted into purchases. |

**Example**:

- If 100 sessions performed a search, 40 clicked a product, 20 added it to their cart, and 10 purchased:
  - **CTR**: 40%
  - **Add-to-cart rate**: 20% (20/100)
  - **Purchase rate**: 10% (10/100)
