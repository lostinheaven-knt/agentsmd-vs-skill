# Pricing plans

Welcome to Genstore’s pricing plans! We offer flexible options to meet different business needs. Each plan includes a range of features to help you build and optimize your online store with ease.

## Genstore pricing plans

### [Genstore Free plan](./start-understand-pricing-plans-free-trial.md)

- Kickstart your journey with 20 one-time free credits
- Earn 5 daily credits for daily logins
- Each account can create up to **2 free plan stores**
- Full access to our AI suite: Including Store Builder, Product Creation, Store Design, and Dropshipping Selection.

### **[Genstore Lite plan](./start-understand-pricing-plans-basic.md)**

- 50 monthly credits.
- 5 daily credits awarded for daily logins.
- You can build and customize your store, upload products, handle dropshipping, and manage customers or orders, with Genstore AI to boost efficiency across your daily operations and data analysis. 

### **[Genstore Growth plan](./start-understand-pricing-plans-genstore.md)**

- 200 monthly credits.
- 5 daily credits for daily logins.
- Extra credits available by purchasing a separate Credit Pack.
- Unlock a larger total product push volume, expanded resource quotas (such as staff accounts, inventory locations, and markets), and more favorable fee rates to help you efficiently manage and optimize your store operations.

### **[Genstore Scale plan](./start-understand-pricing-plans-advanced.md)**

- 700 monthly credits.
- 5 daily credits for daily logins.
- Extra credits available by purchasing a separate Credit Pack.
- Unlock an even greater total product push volume, our highest resource quotas (such as staff accounts, inventory locations, and markets), and our best fee rates, designed to support the operational demands of larger-scale businesses.
