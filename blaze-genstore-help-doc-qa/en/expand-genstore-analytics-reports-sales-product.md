# Sales by product

The **Sales by product report** provides detailed insights into your sold products and services, **excluding shipping fees**.

## Data handling for shipping fees

**Shipping fees are excluded** from this report because a single order may contain multiple products but only one shipping charge. Since shipping costs cannot be allocated to individual products, both shipping fees and associated taxes are omitted.

## Use this report to

- **Top product analysis**: Identify the top 20 product categories and product titles by sales performance to prioritize high-demand items.
- **Metric customization**: Select key metrics like Gross Sales, Units Sold, or Profit Margins for tailored analysis.
- **Granular sales review**: Access detailed product-level data including units sold, revenue, and regional performance.

## Report data fields

For standard definitions, refer to [Sales reports - common terms](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-sales.md#common-terms).

**Additional terms specific to this report**:

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Product vendor** | The vendor of your product if you purchase it from a third party. |
| **Product type**   | The type of product you are selling. For example, hats, shirts, or shoes. |
| **Product title**  | The name for your product. |
| **Net quantity**   | Equates to number of sold items - number of returned items. |
