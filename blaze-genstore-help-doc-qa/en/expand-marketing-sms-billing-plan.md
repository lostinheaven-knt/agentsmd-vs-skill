# Genstore SMS pricing plan

## Free trial

When you enable SMS for the first time, you’ll receive a **$1 free credit** to try it out. This credit is issued once and only applies to your first-time activation.
- After the free credit is used, billing automatically switches to standard rates
- Charges are based on the actual number of messages sent and the destination country rate

## How billing works

SMS costs are calculated based on:

```
Number of messages × Per-message rate by country (USD)
```

- All pricing is billed in **USD**
- If a message exceeds the character limit, it will be split into multiple segments and billed accordingly
