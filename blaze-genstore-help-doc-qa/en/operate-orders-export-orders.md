# Export orders

You can export order information from the **Orders** page in the Genstore admin. This generates a CSV file, making it easy to view a detailed overview of the orders. Each export can include up to 10,000 order item rows.

## Steps to export

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin** and go to the **Store** -> **Orders** page.
2. On the **Orders list** page, filter the orders you want to export.
3. Click the **Export order** button to begin exporting the order data.
4. Go to the **Task center** to find your exported content, then click **Download** to save the data to your local device.
