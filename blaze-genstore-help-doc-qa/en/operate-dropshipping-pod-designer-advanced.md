# Professional design

The professional design mode is a professional-grade creative tool. Whether you are uploading your own artwork or creating from scratch using built-in features, this tool helps you generate print-ready files and high-quality product mockups.

::: tip
For products supports multiple print technique, select your preferred printing technology before proceeding with design and mockup configuration.
:::

## Design workflow 

1. Log in to the Genstore merchant admin, select a specific product, and click **Start design** on the product card.
2. Click the **Professional design** button located in the top-left corner of the page.
3. Once you are in the design took, click to choose the desired print technique (e.g., DTG, embroidery, full print).
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the design process.

### Assets and creative tools
* **Custom uploads**: Support for uploading local design files. All uploaded assets are saved in your **asset library** for easy future reuse.
* **Text tools**: Customize fonts and colors, with support for applying **pattern fill effects** to your text.
* **Extensive asset library**: Choose from multi-themed **Clipart** and gain access to **premium curated assets**. All selected assets are automatically stored in your library for streamlined access.
* **Quick templates**: Access a collection of fully editable pre-set layouts, allowing for rapid on-demand customization.

### Layout and editing

- Place your designs on different print areas (front, back, sleeves, tags, etc.).
- Adjust the size, position, and rotation of design elements, or configure layer blending effects.
- Monitor print quality alerts to ensure your image resolution meets professional printing standards.
- Apply background colors or patterns to print areas, or utilize premium curated assets.

### All-over print (AOP) tools

* **Seamless pattern generation**: Within the **Fill** tab, you can use the **Apply background to all placements** feature to instantly transform clip art, custom uploads, or sample files into a seamless repeating pattern.
* **Precision layout control**: Use the dedicated sliders to adjust pattern spacing, rotation angles, and alignment for a perfect visual arrangement.
* **Cross-placement synchronization**: In the **Layer** panel, select a design element and click **⋮** -> **Duplicate to** to copy the design to other print areas with a single click, ensuring a consistent brand look across the entire product.

## Choose mockup images

Product mockups demonstrate how your design will look on the physical product, providing you with high-quality imagery for your store. Once your design is complete, you can:

* **Select multiple angles**: Choose from various mockup perspectives to showcase the product comprehensively.
* **Set primary image**: Freely designate any specific mockup as the main product image.
* **Sync to store**: Push your selected mockup images directly to your [product list](./operate-product-manage.md) alongside the item details.
