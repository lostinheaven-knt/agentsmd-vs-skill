# Customer reports

**Customer Reports** provide in-depth analytics to help you comprehensively understand the composition, behavior, and value of your customer base. These reports offer insights into sales contributions from new vs. returning customers, behavioral patterns, retention rates, geographic distribution, and more, enabling you to refine marketing strategies and enhance customer satisfaction.

## How to access

1. Log in to your Genstore admin.
2. Navigate to **Analytics** -> **Reports** in the left menu.
3. Locate financial reports under the **Customers** section on the Reports Summary page.
4. Click any report title to view comprehensive metrics and granular data.

## Data update frequency

- Customer reports may not display all activities from the past 12 hours due to data processing delays.
- However, when you open the **[Customers over time](./expand-genstore-analytics-reports-customers-timeline.md)** report, it displays near-real-time data (updated within a few seconds). **Refresh the report or reopen** it to view the latest updates.

## Use this report to

The customer reports can help you analyze customer behavior and distribution from a regional perspective, enabling you to create more targeted marketing strategies and customer engagement plans.

- **New vs. returning customer sales**: Analyze revenue contribution from new and returning customers to understand the value and growth potential of each customer segment.
- **Customer activity over time**: Track the number of transacting customers across different time periods to identify peaks in customer activity and buying trends.
- **Customer segmentation**: Understand the characteristics and needs of different customer groups to support market segmentation and personalized marketing efforts.
- **Geographic distribution**: Analyze where your customers are located to fine-tune regional marketing strategies and optimize shipping or logistics planning.
- **Customer profiles**: Build rich customer profiles based on demographics, purchase preferences, and lifestyle indicators to support targeted marketing.
- **Customer behavior**: Examine purchasing behavior and on-site activity—including page views, searches, and conversion paths—to enhance the user experience and drive conversions.
- **Customer retention**: Measure customer loyalty and retention over time, and identify strategies to increase repeat engagement.
- **One-time customers**: Analyze the characteristics of one-time purchasers, identify reasons for churn, and optimize retention strategies to enhance customer value.
- **Returning customers**: Identify patterns in repeat customer behavior and purchase frequency to develop strategies that increase retention and repeat sales.
