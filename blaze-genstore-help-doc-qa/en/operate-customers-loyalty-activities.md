# Track customer interactions

The **Activity** tab in **Genstore Loyalty** enables real-time monitoring of all user activities related to points, VIP status, and referrals. This guide helps you effectively use this page to manage customer activities and optimize your customer relationship management strategy.

## Points management

The **Points** tab displays all point-related activity records:

- **Member**: Shows user's email.
- **Action**: Records points earned, used, or revoked.
- **Status**: Shows the result of each points record, indicating whether points are earned, redeemed, expired, refunded, canceled, or adjusted.
- **Points**: Shows increase or decrease in points.
- **Note**: Provides detailed explanation for point changes.
- **Time**: Records exact time of point changes.

## VIP

The **VIP** tab shows all VIP level change activities:

- **Member**: Shows user email.
- **VIP tier**: Records VIP tier upgrades or downgrades
- **Note**: Specific details about level changes
- **Date**: Date when a level change occurred

## Referrals

The **Referrals** tab displays all referral marketing activities and related level changes:

- **Member**: Includes user email
- **Referrals**: Shows all key information related to referral activities
- **Status**: Shows the result of each points record, indicating whether points are earned, redeemed, expired, refunded, canceled, or adjusted
- **Note**: Provides detailed description of referral activities
- **Date**: Records exact time of activities

## Export activities

To export activity records, go to the Points, VIP, or Referral activity page and click **Download**. You can export records based on the current filters or all activity records.

**Note:** Large data exports may take time. You can close the page and continue other tasks, then check the **Export history** section later to view or download the exported files.

## Additional information

If a customer is **deleted** or **merged**, their related Points, VIP, or Referral activity records will no longer be visible or searchable on the Loyalty **Activity** page.
