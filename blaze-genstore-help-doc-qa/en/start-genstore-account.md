# Store and account

This section guides you through creating and managing your Genstore store—from setting up your account and store details to using the AI-native Genstore workspace for daily operations.

## Topics in this section

- [Manage your Genstore account](./start-genstore-account-manage.md): Learn how to update your personal information, connect third-party login services, view linked stores, and change the Genstore admin language.
- [General store settings](./start-genstore-account-create-manage.md): Set up key business details for your store, including name, contact info, time zone, currency, and weight unit.
    - [Manage brand assets](./start-genstore-account-brand-assets.md): Upload your store logo, brand colors, and social media links to ensure a consistent brand presence across all channels.
- [Manage your subscription plan](./start-genstore-account-plan.md): View your current plan, upgrade or downgrade your plan, or deactivate your store when needed.
- [Roles and permissions](./start-genstore-account-roles-permissions.md): Assign user roles and access levels to your team for secure and efficient store management.
- [Collaborate with AI Agent](./start-genstore-account-homepage.md): Work with Genstore's AI Agent to perform tasks like product uploads, order processing, and customer service. The agent can respond to your prompts or automatically detect your intent to assist you more efficiently.
- [Store migration](./start-migrate-guide.md): Use the Genstore Migration Assistant to quickly import data from other e-commerce platforms to Genstore.
