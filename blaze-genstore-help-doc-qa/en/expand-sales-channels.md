# Sales channels

Genstore offers multiple channels to help you expand your reach:

- **[Google & YouTube sales channel](./expand-sales-channels-google.md)**: Sync your products to Google Merchant Center to optimize ad performance and increase visibility in search results.    
- **[Facebook & Instagram sales channel](./expand-sales-channels-facebook.md)**: Sync products to Facebook and Instagram Shops to boost brand awareness and engagement.
- **[TikTok sales channel](./expand-sales-channels-tiktok.md)**: Sync products to your TikTok storefront to boost product visibility and conversion.
- [**Pinterest sales channel**](./expand-sales-channels-pinterest.md): Supports syncing products to Pinterest and enables direct product sales on Pinterest.  
- **[Genstore Market](./expand-sales-channels-genstore-market.md)**: Connect your Genstore store to marketplaces like Amazon to manage products and orders across multiple platforms in one place.
- **[Buy Button](./expand-sales-channels-buy-button.md)**: Embed a purchase button in your blog, official website, or social media platform. Customers can complete purchases without being redirected. Ideal for content marketing, influencer promotion, and external campaign scenarios.
- **[Genstore Inbox](./expand-inbox.md)**: Communicate with customers in real time, send promotional messages and product recommendations, and improve customer service.

## Manage sales channels

- **For Google & YouTube, Facebook & Instagram, TikTok, and Inbox (built-in channels):**
    1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Sales channels** in the left-hand navigation.
    2. Select the desired channel to access the setup page.
    3. Follow the on-screen instructions to connect your account and sync products.
- **For Pinterest (requires installation):**
    1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Apps** in the left-hand navigation.
    2. Use the search bar to find **Pinterest**.
    3. Click **Install**, then **Launch app** to begin setup.
- **For Genstore Market (requires installation):**
    1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Apps** in the left-hand navigation.
    2. Use the search bar to find **Genstore Market**.
    3. Click **Install**, then **Launch app** to begin setup.
- **For Buy Button (requires installation):**
    1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Apps** in the left-hand navigation.
    2. Use the search bar to find **Genstore Buy Button**.
    3. Click **Install**, then **Launch app** to begin setup.
