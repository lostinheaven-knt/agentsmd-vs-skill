# Authorize logistic carrier

To improve shipping efficiency, Genstore partners with Shippo to provide a seamless way to purchase shipping labels. By authorizing your Shippo account, you can buy shipping labels directly from your Genstore admin. This guide will walk you through the process of authorizing your Shippo account on the Genstore platform.

## Step 1: Authorize your Shippo account

1. Log in to your Genstore admin.
2. In the left-hand navigation menu, go to **Settings -> Shipping and delivery -> Carrier management**.
3. On the page, find and select **Shippo** as your shipping service provider.
4. Click **Authorize**.

## Step 2: Sign up or log in to Shippo

1. **If you already have a Shippo account**, log in with your existing credentials to complete the authorization. The system will return you to the Genstore shipping settings page and display an **Authorization successful** message.
2. **If you don’t have a Shippo account yet**, click **Sign up for a new account** on the authorization page. Follow the instructions to register, then proceed with the login and authorization process.

Once the authorization is successful, you can start purchasing shipping labels through Shippo in your Genstore admin.

::: tip

Make sure your Shippo account has valid payment information set up to avoid issues when purchasing labels.

:::

## FAQs

### Q1. What should I do if the authorization fails?
> A: Ensure that you have entered the correct Shippo account details. If the issue persists, contact [Shippo support](https://goshippo.com/contact) for assistance.

### Q2. Why is the shipping label price different from what I expected?
> A: Shipping label prices are based on real-time rates from the carrier. The cost may vary depending on factors such as package weight, dimensions, and destination.
