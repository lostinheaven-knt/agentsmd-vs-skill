# Points plan

Points are central to **Genstore Loyalty**, encouraging customer interaction and purchases through activities like ordering, registering, and sharing on social media. Customers can redeem accumulated points for various rewards, enhancing engagement and return visits.

## Activate the points program

1. Log in to your Genstore store management page.
2. Navigate to **Store** -> **Customers** -> **Loyalty**.
3. On the **Points** tab, use the toggle switch to activate or deactivate the program.
4. To customize your points display name, go to **Loyalty** -> **Settings**, then modify the points symbol in the **Points label** of the **General** section.

Once activated, you can set how users earn and redeem points and their validity. Learn more here:

- [Set ways for points earning](./operate-customers-loyalty-points-earnings.md)
- [Set point redemption](./operate-customers-loyalty-points-redemption.md)
- [Set point expiration](./operate-customers-loyalty-points-expiration.md)
