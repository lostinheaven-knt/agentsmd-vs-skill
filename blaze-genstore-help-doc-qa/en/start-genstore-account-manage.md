# Manage your Genstore account

You can access and manage your Genstore account by logging in to [Genstore admin](https://admin.genstore.ai/admin).

## Overview

Genstore allows you to manage the following account settings:

- Update or delete your personal information such as name, email, and profile photo.
- Connect third-party login services like Apple, Facebook, or Google.
- View all stores linked to your account.
- Change the display language of the Genstore Admin Console.

## Update account details

You can upload or edit your profile photo, update your first and last name, change your email address, or reset your account password.

### Steps:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and click your account in the lower-left corner.
2. Click **Manage account**.
3. In the **Details** section, update any of the following:
    - To add or change your profile photo, click **Upload photo**.
    - To update your name, enter new values in the corresponding fields.
4. Click **Save**.

## Verify or update your login email

If your email is marked as "not verified", click **Resend verification email** at the top of the page and follow the instructions in your inbox.

### To update your login email:

1. Click the pencil icon next to your current email.
2. Enter your new email and current password in the pop-up window.
3. Click **Send verification code**, then complete the verification process via email.
4. Once verified, click **Save**.

### To reset your password:

1. Click **Reset password** next to your email.
2. You will receive a password reset email.
3. Open the email and click **Reset password**.
4. Set a new password and use it to log in again.

## Connect external login services

For improved account security and easier access, you can link your Genstore account to external login services such as Apple, Facebook, or Google.

::: tip

Please verify your email before linking any external service.

:::

### Steps:

1. In the Genstore admin, click your account in the lower-left corner.
2. Click **Manage account**.
3. Under **Login service**, click **Connect** next to the desired service.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete authorization.

## View linked stores

In the **Manage account** section, you can view and access all stores associated with your account.

### Steps:

1. In the Genstore admin, click your account in the lower-left corner.
2. Click **Manage account**.
3. In the **Stores** section, click **View all stores**.
4. On the **Store list** page, click any store to access its admin.

## Change Genstore admin language

Changing your account language only affects the Genstore admin. It does not impact the language of your online storefront.

To change your storefront language, see the [Language settings guide](./settings-languages.md).

### Steps:

1. In the Genstore admin, click your account in the lower-left corner.
2. Click **Manage account**.
3. In the **Language** section, open the dropdown menu and select your preferred language.
4. Click **Save**.

## Delete account and data

If you wish to delete your account and related data from Genstore, please ensure that all the Genstore store bills associated with your account are settled and fully closed. Once we receive your account and data deletion request, it may take up to 30 days for Genstore to process it. You can follow the steps below to initiate the deletion process.

Steps:

1. In the Genstore admin, click on the logged-in account in the bottom left corner.
2. Click **Manage account**.
3. In the **Account and data** section, click **Delete account and data** to begin the deletion process.

::: warning

Once the account and data are deleted, they cannot be recovered. Please proceed with caution!

:::
