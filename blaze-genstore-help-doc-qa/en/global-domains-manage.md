# Manage domains

## Change domain type

There are three types of domains:

- **Primary domain**: Every store must have one primary domain. You can also have multiple alias or redirect domains. When you first register your Genstore store, the system creates a `.genmystore.com` domain as your primary domain. You can add a custom domain and set it as your primary domain.
- **Redirect domain**: A redirect domain points to your store, but when visitors enter the redirect domain in their browser, they will be redirected to the primary domain.
- **Alias domain**: An alias domain points to your store but doesn’t redirect to the primary domain. The alias domain shows up in the browser’s address bar.

### Steps:

1. In the Genstore admin, go to **Settings** -> **Domains**.
2. Find the domain you want to change.
3. Click the **Change domain type** icon.
4. Select the new domain type and click **Save**.

## Remove a domain

If you no longer want to associate a third-party custom domain with your Genstore store, you can remove it from the Genstore admin. You can also remove subdomains.

:::tip

- You cannot delete your `.genmystore.com` domain.
- To delete the primary domain, you must first change the primary domain and then remove the old one.

:::

### Steps:

1. In the Genstore admin, go to **Settings** -> **Domains**.
2. Find the domain you want to remove.
3. Click **Delete** to confirm.
