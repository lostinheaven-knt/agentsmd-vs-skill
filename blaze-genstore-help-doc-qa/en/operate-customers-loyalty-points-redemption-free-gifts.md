# Free gift reward

**Genstore Loyalty** enriches customer interaction by offering versatile point redemption options for free gifts. Businesses can leverage fixed or tiered redemption models to increase customer spending and loyalty.

## Set discount redemption

**Navigation**: 

1. Log in to Genstore admin and go to **Store** -> **Customers** -> **Loyalty**. 
2. Under the **Points** tab, find the **Redeem points** section.
### Setup steps

Go to **Add** -> **Free gift** -> **Add**, and you can set as follows:

- **Rule name**: Create a recognizable name for ease of future management.
- **Redemption rules**: Set the number of points required to redeem a free gift coupon, such as 1000 points.
- **Free gifts**
  - **Free product**: Customers can redeem points for a voucher that covers the full value of a chosen product. **Note**: these vouchers do not apply to shipping costs. For instance, 1,000 points could be exchanged for a voucher that allows a customer to pick up a product free of charge.
  - **Partial discount on product**: You can also offer vouchers that provide a dollar discount on purchases. Customers can redeem their points for a set discount, which will be deducted from the total cost at checkout. For example, redeeming 500 points might grant a $25 discount, which is automatically subtracted from the total purchase price.
- **Limitations on use**
  - **Expiration**: Establish an expiration period for discounts, e.g., 6 months, with an automated reminder email before expiry.
  - **Combines with**: Decide if the discount can be combined with other offers and under what conditions.

	:::tip

	Carefully consider discount combinations to avoid excessive reductions.

	:::

## Preview and Publish

Check your settings in the **Summary** section to the right. The system provides a real-time summary here. Once you confirm the details, click **Save** to activate the rule.

You can manage this rule—view, edit, enable, or disable it—via the **Earn points** list.

:::tip

Use the status switch to easily toggle this rule on or off.

:::

## FAQs

### Q1. Do free gifts redeemed by customers include shipping costs?
> A: No, vouchers for free gifts cover only the cost of the item itself; customers remain responsible for any applicable shipping fees. However, you can enable options that allow free shipping discounts to be combined with vouchers for free products.

### Q2. When multiple SKUs for a free product are available, which SKU's price determines the coupon's value?
> A: If you select the **Entirely free product** option and there are multiple SKUs available at different prices, the voucher will be based on the highest-priced SKU. For example, if there are three baseball caps priced at $5, $10, and $20, the voucher issued will be valued at $20, applicable to any of the available caps.
