# Add policy pages to your store

To ensure your store operates smoothly—especially if you sell through sales channels like Facebook Ads and Google Ads—you must add the following information to your store:

- Return and refund policy
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- Shipping policy
- Contact information

These details are usually displayed in your website footer. To add them, you’ll first create the policy pages, then link them to your footer menu, and finally display the footer menu on your storefront.

## Step 1 — Create policy pages (choose one method)

You can create policy pages in Genstore using one of the following methods:

### Option 1: Use Genstore templates (recommended for standard policies)

1. Go to **Settings** -> **Policies**.
2. Click on each policy type, review and edit the content to fit your store needs.
3. Click **Confirm** to save.

### Option 2: Create custom pages

1. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Pages** -> **Create page**.
2. Add a page title and paste your policy content into the **Body content** section.
3. Apply any basic formatting as needed.
4. Set the page as visible by clicking **Visible** under **Visibility status**.
5. Click **Save**.

### Option 3: Create directly in Theme Editor (for fully customized design)

1. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Themes**. Click **Customize** next to your current theme.
2. In the Theme Editor, click Page management (first icon on the left navigation bar) , locate Pages, you can select one of the exiting page template or click **Create template** to create a new page template. 
3. If you click **Create template**, enter the template name and select a theme base. Click **Confirm**. 
4. Customize the page visually. You can preview changes live.
5. When finished, click **Save**.

For more details on working with page templates, see [Customize with Theme Editor – Page templates](./operate-store-design-themes-edit-guide#page-templates).

## Step 2 — Link policy pages to the footer menu

After you create the pages, link them to your store’s footer menu:

1. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Menus**.
2. Click the edit icon next to the **Footer menu**.
3. Click **Add menu item**.
4. Enter the menu item name (this will appear in your storefront).
5. Click the **URL** input box and select your policy page:
    - If you used Genstore templates, select the page under **Policies**.
    - If you created a custom or Theme Editor page, select it under **Pages**.
6. Click **Save**.

## Step 3 — Display the footer menu in your storefront

To show the footer menu on your live store:

1. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Themes**. Click **Customize** next to your current theme.
2. Under the **Footer** section, click **Add block** -> **Menu**.
3. Select the **Footer** menu you just updated.
4. Click **Save**.

The policy pages will now appear in your storefront footer.
