# Additional payment methods

You can offer alternative payment options, such as **Alipay**, allowing customers to pay with digital wallets. By enabling additional payment methods in your **Genstore** store, you provide more flexibility for your customers.

:::tip

- Alternative payment methods typically redirect customers to a hosted checkout page.
- Third-party transaction fees may apply. Contact the payment provider for details.

:::

## Activate other payment methods

Unlike credit card payments, you can add multiple alternative payment methods to your store.

1. Log in to your **Genstore admin account** and go to **Settings** -> **Payments**.
2. In the **Additional payment methods** section, click **Add payment method**.
3. Search for a payment method or provider and select one.
4. Choose a provider from the search results and click **Activate**.
5. Enter your account credentials or follow the on-screen setup instructions.
6. Click **Activate** to complete the setup.

## Deactivate other payment methods

If you no longer need a specific payment method, you can disable it:

1. Log in to your **Genstore admin account** and go to **Settings** -> **Payments**.
2. In the **Additional payment methods** section, click **Manage** next to the payment method.
3. Click the **More** menu in the top-right corner and select **Deactivate**.
