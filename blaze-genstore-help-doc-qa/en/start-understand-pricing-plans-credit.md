# Credits usage guide

## What are credits?

Credits are the standard unit used to measure consumption of certain features within **Genstore**.

## How can I earn credits?

You can obtain credits in the following ways:

* **Free users** can get additional credits by subscribing to a paid plan.
* **Subscribed users** can earn more credits by upgrading to a higher-tier plan or by purchasing a separate [Credit Pack](#how-to-purchase-a-credit-pack).
* Credits may also be rewarded through **special campaigns**, such as following our social media accounts or completing onboarding tasks.

## How are credits consumed?

### Credit consumption priority

Credits are consumed in the following order:
1. Daily Credits
2. Bonus Credits
3. Add-on Credits
4. Monthly Credits

### Credit rules

* All subscribed users automatically receive **5 daily credits** upon logging in each day.
* **Monthly Credits** are included with your subscription and reset monthly based on your subscription start date.
  * For example, if you subscribed to the **$75 Standard plan** on January 1, you receive **200 monthly credits**. These credits must be used before February 1. Any unused monthly credits will reset back to 200 on February 1.
* **Add-on Credits** are obtained by purchasing a separate credit pack (available for **Standard plan and above only**)
  * Unused credits from a credit pack (i.e., credits exceeding your monthly plan allowance) will roll over to the next billing cycle **once only**.
* **Bonus Credits** earned through campaigns follow the same rollover rules as add-on credits.
* Unused credits remain valid **only with an active paid subscription**
  * If your subscription expires for more than **30 days**, any remaining credits will be permanently forfeited and **will not be restored**, even if you resubscribe later.
  * To avoid losing credits, we strongly recommend using them within your active subscription period.

## What features consume credits?

Currently, credits are used in the following scenarios:

1. Running AI-powered tasks, such as generating products or creating videos.
2. Using Genstore's **automated domain connection service**.

## Where can I view my credit balance and history?

### Steps

1. Log in to your **Genstore**  admin.
2. Go to **Settings** -> **Plan and credits**.
3. Under **My credits**, click **Detail**.
4. You will see:
   * Available balance for each credit type
   * A full history of credit earnings and usage

## How to purchase a credit pack

### Steps

1. Log in to your **Genstore**  admin.
2. Go to **Settings** -> **Plan and credits**.
3. Under **My credits**, click **Manage pack**.
4. Select the credit pack amount you want and click **Upgrade**.
5. Complete checkout to subscribe to the credit pack.
