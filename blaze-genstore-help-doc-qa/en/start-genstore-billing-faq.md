# Billing FAQs
::: faq

### Q1. Where can I find my Genstore bill?
> A: You can view your bill on the **Billing** page in your Genstore admin.

### Q2. What currency is used for Genstore billing?
> A: All Genstore bills are charged in **USD** for accounting purposes.

### Q3. What fees are included in my Genstore bill?
> A: Your bill may include:
> 	- Subscription fees
> 	- App charges
> 	- Taxes
> 	- Transaction fees (if you don’t use Genstore Payments)

### Q4. How do I get a detailed breakdown of my bill?
> A: You can [export your bill as a CSV or PDF](./start-genstore-billing-invoice#export-your-bill) to see a detailed list of charges.

### Q5. What is the minimum billing threshold?
> A: The [minimum billing threshold](./start-genstore-billing-invoice#minimum-billing-threshold) is a set USD amount. If your account’s usage fees exceed this amount, a bill is generated immediately. This usually includes third-party transaction fees and other outstanding charges. **Subscription fees are not included in threshold-based billing.**

### Q6. Why did I receive a bill before my regular billing cycle?
> A: If you have transaction fees from a third-party payment provider or recurring app charges, you may receive a bill before your regular monthly billing cycle. This happens when your total charges exceed your account’s minimum billing threshold.

### Q7. What payment methods are accepted for Genstore bills?
> A: Genstore accepts the following credit cards:
> 	- Mastercard
> 	- Visa
> 	- American Express
> 	- Discover

### Q8. What should I do if I can't pay my bill?
> - Check that your payment method is valid and has enough funds.
> - Ensure your credit card supports recurring payments.
> - Contact your bank or financial institution for assistance.
> - Reach out to Genstore support for further help.

:::
