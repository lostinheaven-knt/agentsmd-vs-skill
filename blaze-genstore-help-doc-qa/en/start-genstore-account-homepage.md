# Merchant admin: Home page overview

The merchant admin home page is your central command center for store management. Here, you can collaborate directly with AI through natural language to execute a wide range of operational tasks—from store design and product selection to marketing and promotion.

This guide outlines the three primary functional zones of your homepage: the **top navigation bar**, the **central chat workspace**, and the **bottom recommendations area**.

## Top navigation bar

The top toolbar integrates account management, community engagement, and system notifications.

* **Chat list**: Access your full history of AI conversations. Click any record to resume a previous task; chats are automatically sorted by the latest activity.
* **Discord community**: Join the official Genstore Discord server with one click. Connect with developers and global merchants to get product updates and growth hacks.
* **Activity center**: Participate in platform events to earn **credit** rewards.
* **Credit balance**: Monitor your available credit in real-time. Click to view detailed usage logs for full transparency on AI-driven task expenditures.
* **Plan management**: View your current subscription details. This section allows for easy upgrades or plan comparisons (available for standard and lower-tier users).

## Central chat workspace

This is your primary workspace for collaborating with AI agents. You can execute complex business logic using simple, natural language commands.

### Main input field
The central entry point for starting any task:
* **Natural language interaction**: Simply type your request (e.g., *"Redesign my storefront with a vintage aesthetic"*) and press enter or click **Send**." For more tips, see [Collaborating with AI agents](./start-genstore-ai-agent-collaboration.md).

### Quick task menu
Access specialized AI agents tailored for specific business domains to streamline your workflow:
* **[Launch agent - Malik](./start-genstore-ai-agent-store-setup.md)**: Fast-track foundational settings, including domains, payments, and shipping, to ensure a compliant launch.
* **[Product agent - Sara](./start-genstore-ai-agent-product.md)**: Manage your catalog efficiently with smart product sourcing and creation tools.
* **[Campaign agent - Olivia](./start-genstore-ai-agent-discount.md)**: Trigger AI-driven marketing campaigns and generate discount codes to boost conversions.
* **[Design agent - Dean](./operate-store-design-themes-ai-decorate.md)**: Generate homepages and switch themes instantly to build high-converting brand visuals.
* **[Visual agent](./start-genstore-ai-agent-visual.md)**: Transform static images into dynamic videos, virtual try-ons, or global adaptations with ease.
* **Analytics agent - Harvey**: Analyze traffic, conversion rates, and sales performance in real-time for actionable business insights.

> **Tip**: Selecting a specific agent will display relevant sub-tasks, helping you complete complex operations with guided precision.

## Bottom recommendations area

This section provides personalized, high-frequency action shortcuts based on your current operational stage.

* **Trending tasks**: Automatically surfaces the business cards you use most often based on your habits.
* **One-click launch**: Click a card to auto-populate the chat with expert-tuned prompts and pre-configured parameters, saving you the time of manual typing.
