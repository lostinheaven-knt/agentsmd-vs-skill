# Search for apps

The Genstore app store offers a variety of apps to help meet your business needs, improve store functionality, and drive growth.

## Browse and search

You can find apps in the Genstore app store in the following ways:

**Use the search bar**
Enter the app name in the search bar to quickly locate what you need. It's recommended to use English keywords for more accurate results.

**Browse by category**
Use the dropdown menu to explore apps by category. For example, apps in the "Marketing & Conversion" or "Sales Products" categories can help you meet specific business needs.

## Set language

You can choose a supported language in the Genstore app store to improve your browsing experience. After selecting a language from the menu in the bottom right corner, the navigation and categories will update accordingly. Keep in mind that most app descriptions are available only in English.

## App recommendations

Genstore offers personalized app recommendations to help you run and grow your business more effectively. These recommendations are based on the following factors:

- Preferences of other stores similar to yours, based on industry, location, and store attributes.
- Your Genstore usage patterns and store behavior.

You can view recommended apps in the following places:

- The **Apps** page in your Genstore admin.
- The "Popular Recommendations for Similar Stores" section on the Genstore app store homepage.
- The recommended section on the logout page.

**Note**: App recommendations are not ads. They are generated from data to help you find the best apps to improve your store.
