# Product recommendations

Product recommendations are an effective tool to enhance the customer shopping experience and increase sales. Through an intelligent recommendation system, merchants can showcase related or personalized products to customers, increasing product exposure and sales. This feature is especially effective on e-commerce platforms, significantly improving conversion rates and customer satisfaction.

- Enhance the shopping experience by offering personalized recommendations to boost conversion rates and average order value.
- Help merchants achieve precise marketing based on user behavior and product data.
- Support recommendations across multiple scenarios (e.g., homepage, shopping cart, product detail page).

## Product recommendation methods

Product recommendations offer 10 methods, each configurable with its own display settings, recommendation rules, sorting options, and data filters.

| **Recommendation method**         | **Description**                                              | **Business goal**                                            |
| ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Best-selling products**       | Displays hot-selling products based on sales data, featured on multiple pages, such as the homepage and product detail pages. | Increase exposure of best-selling products and drive higher conversion rates. |
| **Trending products**           | Shows the most popular products on the website, based on sales data, browsing behavior, and customer reviews. | Attract customers' attention to high-demand products and boost sales. |
| **Recently viewed**             | Automatically tracks and shows the products customers have recently viewed, recommending similar items based on browsing history. | Enhance the shopping experience, encourage customers to revisit products they’re interested in, and increase repeat purchases. |
| **Almost sold out**             | Alerts customers when an item is running low in stock, creating urgency and prompting quicker purchases. | Create a sense of urgency and boost immediate sales.         |
| **Smart recommendations** | Recommends the most relevant products based on customer behavior, purchase history, and browsing records. | Offer personalized recommendations, improving product relevance and conversion rates. |
| **Selected products**           | Merchants handpick items based on specific strategies (e.g., high-margin products) for display. | Increase exposure of high-margin products, boost merchant profit, and offer a personalized shopping experience. |
|**Trends** | Displays products in your store with high views, clicks, add-to-cart actions, or sales, based on recent data such as view growth rate or order growth rate.| Boost conversions, increase product visibility, and shorten decision time|
|**Top-rated**| Displays products with high star ratings and a large number of reviews, highlighting reputation and customer satisfaction.| Build customer trust|
|**New arrivals** | Displays recently added products in your store, based on publish date (e.g., items added within the last 120 days).|Increase exposure of new products and encourage returning customers to place orders|
|**Related recommendations** | Recommends similar, complementary, bundled, or stylistically related products based on the item currently being viewed or purchased. Can be set manually or generated by algorithm.| Increase average order value and reduce bounce rate|

## Install and uninstall the app

1. **Install the app**: Search for **Product Recommendations** in the Genstore admin panel or the Genstore App Store, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
2. **Pin the app**: In the apps list, find the product recommendation app and click the **Pin** button for quick access, enabling easier management and operation.
3. **Uninstall the app**: If you no longer need the product recommendation app, you can remove it by clicking the **Uninstall** button in the app list. Note that uninstalling will clear the app’s configuration and data.
4. **Reinstall the app**: If you need to reinstall the product recommendation app later, go to the **Uninstalled** section on the **Apps** page, find the app, and click to install. You’ll need to authorize user data, as the app will access store and personal information (including customer, store, and partner data) to ensure accurate and personalized recommendations.

## Create product recommendations

The settings for different recommendation strategies are generally the same. This section explains how to create a product recommendation plan using **Best-selling products** as an example.

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and navigate to **Apps**. Find the product recommendation app in the installed apps list and click **Launch app**.
2. Click **Create recommendation**, then choose the **Product recommendation method** (using best-selling products as an example).
3. Enable the recommendation type: toggle the **Status switch** to activate the current recommendation method.
4. In the theme editor, add the display configuration section and select the appropriate display location (e.g., product detail page, shopping cart). Navigate to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Themes**, click the **Customize** button. Under **Product** -> **Segment template** section, click **Add section**. In the **Apps** tab, select **Best-seller**. Preview the result and save. Once configured, the best-selling product recommendation will appear on the corresponding consumer end pages.
5. [Optional] Recommendation settings, such as selecting both specific products and intelligent recommendations. Intelligent recommendations will have higher priority than merchant-specified products.
   - **Show selected products first**: This will prioritize displaying selected products. Click **Select products** to configure recommended products.
   - **Enable smart recommendations**: Products recommended by the system’s algorithm will be prioritized.
6. [Required] Display configuration: Choose a display location, such as the product detail page, product list page, shopping cart, cart drawer page, or 404 page.
7. [Required] Recommendation rules:
   - **Set the most popular products within the past N days**: Default is 7 days; set the range (1–365 days).
   - **Product sorting method**: Sort recommended products by sales or creation date.
	 Different recommendation methods may have slightly different rules. You can configure them according to the page prompts.
	 	::: tip
	 	 Recommendation rules vary slightly by method. You can configure them based on the on-page guidance.
	 	:::
8. [Optional] Filter settings:
   - **Display sold-out products in recommended products**: Enable this option if you want to include out-of-stock products.
   - **Price**: Set a price range for recommended products, specifying the price interval. The price should reflect the product’s sales price, up to four decimal places.
   - **Tags**: Select product tags to include or exclude in the recommendations. Multiple tags can be selected.
   - **Stock quantity**: Set stock limits for recommended products, specifying minimum and maximum inventory levels.
