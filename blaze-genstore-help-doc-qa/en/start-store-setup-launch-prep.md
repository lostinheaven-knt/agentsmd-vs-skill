# Finalize store compliance and go-live readiness

After building the foundation of your Genstore store, the next step is to finalize policies, shipping, taxes, and payment settings. This ensures everything is compliant and functioning properly before launching your store.

### Set up policy pages

Create clear store policies to protect customer rights and meet platform compliance standards.

- **Create policy content**: Use Genstore templates or create custom pages for your privacy policy, return and refund policy, shipping policy, and terms of service. For detailed steps, see [Create policy pages](./operate-store-design-themes-edit-guide-policy#step-1-—-create-policy-pages-choose-one-method).
- **Add to navigation menu**: Go to **Store** -> **Online Store -> Menus** and add your policy pages to the footer menu for easier customer access. For detailed steps, see [Add policy pages to the footer menu](./operate-store-design-themes-edit-guide-policy#step-2-—-link-policy-pages-to-the-footer-menu).
- **Display policy information on your storefront**: Use the **Theme Editor** to link the footer menu and display your policy pages at the bottom of your storefront. For detailed steps, see [Display policy pages on your storefront](./operate-store-design-themes-edit-guide-policy#step-3-—-display-the-footer-menu-in-your-storefront).

### Set up shipping and tax rules

Configure your store’s fulfillment and tax settings to align with customer expectations and local regulations.

**Shipping settings:**

- **Path**: **Settings -> Shipping and delivery**
- Set warehouse and shipping origin addresses. For multiple locations, add them under **Locations**. See [Manage locations](./operate-fulfillment-location.md).
- Define shipping zones and rates. See [Set up shipping rates](./operate-fulfillment-shipping-profile-shipping-rate.md).
- Set default package size for more efficient fulfillment. See [Manage packages](./operate-fulfillment-manage-package.md).
- (Optional) Authorize logistics partners to buy labels and manage order fulfillment through Genstore. See [Genstore logistics service](./operate-fulfillment-genstore-shipping.md).

**Tax settings:**

- **Path**: **Settings -> Taxes and duties**

> **Tip**: Accurate shipping and tax settings improve the customer experience and ensure legal compliance.

### Configure payment and checkout process

Optimize your checkout experience to help customers complete purchases smoothly.

**Enable payment services:**

- **Path**: **Settings -> Payments**
- Activate [Genstore Payments](./operate-genstore-payments.md) or a supported [third-party provider](./operate-payments-third-party.md).

> **Note**: Using Genstore Payments removes third-party transaction fees and centralizes income tracking.

**Set up checkout preferences:**

- **Path**: **Settings -> [Checkout](./settings-checkout.md)**
- Choose what customer info to collect (email, phone, address).

> **Tip**: A seamless checkout experience reduces cart abandonment and improves conversions.

### Preview and debug your store

Before going live, thoroughly review your store content and functionality.

- **Page review** – Check the homepage, product pages, collection pages, cart, checkout, and blog for completeness and accuracy.
- **Navigation check** – Ensure all menu links work and there are no broken or misdirected links.
- **Responsive testing** – Confirm layout and design are optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- **SEO metadata review** – Check that each page has a clean title, meta description, and user-friendly URL.

> **Tip**: View your store from a customer’s perspective to identify and fix issues.

### Create test orders

Simulate transactions to verify key shopping flows.

- **Test successful payment** – Place an order and complete a real or test payment.
- **Test failed payment** – Simulate a failure to check if the system responds appropriately.
- **Test refund and cancellation** – Cancel or refund orders in the admin to verify functionality.
- **Test fulfillment** – Create shipments and send tracking updates.

> **Tip**: Review confirmation and notification emails to ensure accuracy.

### Launch your store

When you’re ready, remove password protection and open your store to the public.

- **Remove password protection**:
  - Go to **Store** -> **Online store -> Preferences** and disable password protection. See [Turn off password](./operate-store-design-preference#turn-off-password-protection).
- **Invite customers to activate accounts** (if migrating customers):
  - Send account activation or password reset emails so customers can access your store.
- **Add more sales channels (optional):**
  - Connect to marketplaces and social platforms to expand reach. For details:
    - [Google & YouTube sales channel](./expand-sales-channels-google.md)
    - [Facebook & Instagram](./expand-sales-channels-facebook.md)
    - [TikTok](./expand-sales-channels-tiktok.md)
- **Start your marketing campaigns:**
  - Drive your first round of traffic with:
    - [Email marketing](./expand-marketing-email.md)
    - [SMS marketing](./expand-marketing-sms.md)
    - [Social media marketing](./expand-marketing-social-media.md)
    - [Marketing automation](./expand-marketing-automated-email-overview.md)

> **Tip**: Monitor traffic, payments, and orders closely during launch. Enable [Genstore Inbox](./expand-inbox.md) to handle customer messages and order updates in real-time.

### Summary

By completing the above steps, your Genstore store will meet compliance standards, offer a smooth customer experience, and be ready for launch.

Now, focus on marketing and customer engagement to grow your brand!
