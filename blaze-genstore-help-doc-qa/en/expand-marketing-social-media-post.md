# Post on social media

Share your created content on social media platforms to attract potential customers. You can edit drafts and publish them anytime.

:::tip

You can publish up to **100** social media posts per month. Check your remaining quota in the **Monthly balance** section at the top right of the page.

:::

## Publish a post

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to **Marketing** -> **Social media marketing**.
2. On the **Social media marketing** page, click **Create**.
3. Select the **social media platform** you want to share the content on.
4. Customize and **edit** your post before publishing.

## Post content types

- **Post as**: Choose the content type (post, short video, or story).
- **Post content**: Edit the text for your post. You can:
  - Add emojis.
  - Insert links to pages on your store.
  - Include product links to promote products.
- **Publishing time**: Choose to post immediately or schedule for a later time.
