# Shipping and delivery

Before activating a market, you must ensure that all countries and regions within that market have a valid shipping profile assigned. Follow the steps below to view shipping configurations for a specific market.

## View shipping settings

1. From your Genstore admin, go to **Settings** -> **Markets**.
2. In the **Markets** overview page, click the market you want to review.
3. In the **Shipping** section, you’ll see the current shipping setup for that market.
4. Click into the shipping details to view or edit shipping profiles for each region.

For detailed instructions, see [Shipping profiles](./operate-fulfillment-shipping-profile.md).
