# Sign up reward points

This guide is designed for store managers to configure point rewards for customer registrations, fostering increased engagement and loyalty through account creation. Below, you'll find detailed steps on how to set up this specific point-earning method, along with its applications and key considerations.

## Set point rules
**Navigation**: 

1. Log in to Genstore admin and go to **Store** -> **Customers** -> **Loyalty**. 
2. Under the **Points** tab, find the **Earn points** section.
### Setup steps

Click **Add** -> **Sign up** -> **Add**.

- **Rule name**: Provide a name for this reward rule to facilitate easy management.
- **Earning rules**: Specify the number of points awarded for registration. **Adjust this number to align with your overall points system.**

## Preview and publish

Check your settings in the **Summary** section to the right. The system provides a real-time summary here. Once you confirm the details, click **Save** to activate the rule.

You can manage this rule—view, edit, enable, or disable it—via the **Earn points** list.

:::tip

Use the status switch to easily toggle this rule on or off.

:::

## FAQs

### Q1. How does the system recognize registration actions?
> A: Registration behavior includes customers actively registering for a store account or verifying their email address and logging into the store, completing the transition from "Guest" to "Member." Points are awarded once these steps are completed.

### Q2. Will customers who registered before the points plan was implemented receive points?
> A: No, this also applies to customers who register while the **Earn points on registration method** is deactivated.
> 	::: tip
> 	To enhance customer satisfaction, you may consider manually awarding points to those who previously registered.
> 	:::
