# Market preferences

In Genstore, the **market preferences** settings allow you to configure automatic redirection, currency display, price rounding, and regional pricing control to enhance the localized shopping experience across multiple markets.

These settings apply to all **non-primary markets** and can be configured separately for each one. Based on your preferences, the system automatically adjusts language, region, and currency for each visitor, ensuring a consistent and localized shopping experience.

## Overview of configurable options

| Setting                    | Description                                                  |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Country/region redirection | Automatically redirects customers to the appropriate market based on their IP location, improving entry-point relevance. |
| Language redirection       | Automatically switches to the preferred published language version based on the customer's browser settings. |
| Local currency             | When enabled, customers can view and pay for orders in their local currency (based on dynamic currency conversion). |
| Price rounding             | Rounds converted prices to avoid inconsistent decimals and improve price display consistency. |
| Compare-at price hiding    | Hides price differences between markets for customers in the European Economic Area (EEA) to comply with local regulations. |

## How to access

1. Log in to your Genstore admin and go to **Settings** -> **Markets**.
2. In the upper-right corner, click **Preferences** to open the **Preferences** page.
3. Follow the links below to configure each setting:
   - [Set up country/region redirection](./global-market-preferences-auto-redirect#country-region-redirection)
   - [Set up language redirection](./global-market-preferences-auto-redirect#language-redirection)
   - [Enable local currency display](./global-market-preferences-pricing#set-local-currency)
   - [Enable price rounding rules](./global-market-preferences-pricing#set-rounded-prices)
