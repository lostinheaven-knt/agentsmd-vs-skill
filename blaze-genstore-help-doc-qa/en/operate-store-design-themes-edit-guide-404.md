# 404 page

The 404 page informs visitors that the page they’re trying to access doesn’t exist. It helps improve user experience and guides customers back to the correct path. Genstore provides a standard 404 template that supports style customization and content updates, allowing you to maintain brand consistency and reduce bounce rates.

## How to access

1. Log in to your Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Store** -> **Online store** -> **Themes**.
3. Find your theme and click **Customize** to open the theme editor.
4. In the top navigation bar, open the page selector and choose **404 page**.

## Style and layout settings

The 404 page supports basic visual adjustments. Common options include:

- **Color mode**: Switch between light or dark mode for visual consistency.
- **Color scheme**: Apply a color palette that matches your brand.
- **Section spacing**: Adjust vertical padding (none / small / medium / large / extra large).
- **Custom CSS**: For advanced users, add custom style code.

## Add sections and apps

Beyond the default layout, you can enrich the 404 page by adding custom sections such as visuals, text, or branded media.

### Add a section

In the **Segment template** area, click **Add section** and choose from these content types:

|Section type|Description|
|---|---|
|Featured collection|Showcase selected products for campaigns or category highlights|
|Image banner|Display a large brand image or promotional banner|
|Collection list|Show multiple collections from your store|
|Video|Embed a brand story or product video|
|Slideshow|Add carousel images to highlight multiple promotions|
|Contact form|Let customers send inquiries or feedback|
|Rich text|Add brand messages or product details|
|Email signup|Add a newsletter subscription form|
|FAQ|Display frequently asked questions with solutions|
|Collage|Combine multiple images for visual impact|
|Featured product|Highlight specific products|
|Divider|Add visual separation between sections|
|Image|Insert a single image with an optional link|
|Multi-column layout|Customize text-and-image layouts|
|Blog posts|Embed articles for content marketing|
|Scrolling text|Show scrolling messages for promotions, slogans, or alerts|

::: tip  
Available sections may vary based on your theme. We regularly update content components based on user feedback. Always refer to what’s available in the editor for the most accurate options.  
:::

### Add apps

In the **Add section / Add block** pop-up, select the **Apps** tab to add functional components such as:

- Related products
- Bestsellers
- Customers also viewed
- Trending items
