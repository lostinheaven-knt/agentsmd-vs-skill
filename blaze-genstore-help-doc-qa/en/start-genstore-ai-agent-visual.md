# Visual agent

**Visual Agent** is your all-in-one expert for visual creativity and technical expertise. Whether transforming static images into dynamic videos, achieving realistic virtual try-ons, or performing complex image editing and global adaptation, Visual Agent leverages cutting-edge AI visual technology to provide a one-stop e-commerce visual solution, instantly boosting the appeal and conversion rates of your marketing materials.

## Use case 1: Dynamic visuals & interactive experiences 
**Best for**: Sellers of apparel, accessories, and trendy products who wish to break the limits of static images and increase user dwell time and purchase conversion rates through short videos or virtual experiences.

Visual Agent transforms into your **“creative motion director”** and **“virtual stylist,”** making static products "come alive."

### Image-to-video
You do not need professional video editing skills; using only existing product images, you can quickly generate high-quality short videos suitable for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or e-commerce detail pages.
* **How it works**: Enter a command in the dialogue box and upload a product image. Example: “Generate a 5-second video of a model showcasing this piece of clothing.”
* **Intelligent analysis**: AI analyzes the image content and customer requirements to generate a creative plan.
* **Execution & generation**: Based on the analysis results, it automatically adds camera movement effects, transition animations, and background music.

### Virtual try-on
Solve the problems of high costs and long cycles for model photography in the apparel category, quickly demonstrating the effect of clothing on the body.
* **How it works**: Upload a clothing image and a target model image (if no model image is uploaded, the system will automatically generate a matching model).
* **Intelligent analysis**: AI precisely identifies the pattern, material, and texture wrinkles of the clothing.
* **Execution & generation**: AI naturally "dresses" the clothing or accessories onto the model and automatically matches the lighting and shadows.

## Use case 2: Advanced image synthesis & editing
**Best for**: Operations personnel who need to quickly optimize the quality of product hero images or create a full set of marketing materials based on a single asset.

Visual Agent acts as your **“senior retoucher,”** completing complex image processing tasks with simple commands.

### Intelligent background removal & outpainting
* **Background removal**: Pixel-level identification of subject edges (even fine hair can be processed delicately), instantly separating the background.
* **Outpainting**: AI understands the content and style of the picture, intelligently generating a continuous and natural new background scene around the image.

### Product listing images generation
With just one product image, a full set of materials can be automatically created, maintaining a highly unified visual style.
* **How it works**: Upload a high-quality standard product photo and enter the command “Generate a set of multi-scene images.”
* **Intelligent analysis**: Analyzes image content and customer requirements to generate a creative plan.
* **Execution & generation**: Based on the style of the main image, AI automatically generates multi-angle close-ups and situational display images.

## Use case 3: Global visual adaptation
**Best for**: Cross-border sellers facing multi-language markets who need to localize product packaging, manuals, or marketing posters containing text.

Visual Agent acts as your **“visual translation expert,”** making your image assets “understand” global languages instantly.

* **How it works**: Upload the original image containing text and specify the target language (e.g., “Translate into Japanese”).
* **Execution & generation**: AI first accurately identifies the text areas in the image and translates them into the target language. Subsequently, the AI erases the original text and mimics the original font style, color, and perspective angle to “write” the translated text back into its original position.
