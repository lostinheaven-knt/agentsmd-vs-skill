# Genstore PartnerBoost

Genstore PartnerBoost is an all-in-one affiliate and partner management platform. Through deep integration with Genstore, you can launch a full-scale program—from **Global Influencer Recruitment** to **Automated Attribution & Payouts**—with a single click. Powered by Genstore's high-performance core, every partner-driven order is accurately synced to drive scalable brand growth.

## Prerequisites

### Collect Genstore store details
Before connecting, record the following information from your Genstore admin:
* **Store name**: Go to **Settings** -> **General**. You can find the name in the "Store Details" section.
* **Store domain**: Go to **Settings** -> **Domains** and copy your primary domain.

### Register a PartnerBoost account
Sign up and log in at the [PartnerBoost Registration Page](https://www.partnerboost.com/register). During setup, complete the following:
* **Platform**: Enter `Genstore`.
* **Store URL**: Enter your registered Genstore domain (e.g., `1a2b3c4-5.genmystore.com`).
* **Brand ID**: After registration, click the **Settings** icon on the left sidebar to retrieve your unique **Brand ID**.

## Install the PartnerBoost app

Get started by connecting the app to your Genstore admin.

1.  Visit the [Genstore App Marketplace](https://apps.genstore.ai/en-US/) and log in.
2.  Select the store you wish to integrate.
3.  Search for **Genstore PartnerBoost**.
4.  Click **Install**.
5.  You will be redirected to your Genstore Admin to complete the setup.
6.  Paste the **Brand ID** you obtained from PartnerBoost. The system will automatically link your accounts.
   
## Configure PartnerBoost settings

### Account details & branding
Log in to the PartnerBoost dashboard and navigate to **Settings** -> **Account Details**:

* **Logo**: Upload your store logo.
* **Program description**: Click **Edit** to write your brand story or upload a profile. Click **Save**.
* **Program Agreement**: Click **Edit** to define your terms (e.g., restricted media types). You can use the built-in template or upload your own. Click **Save**.
* **Supported Country**: Click **Edit** to select the regions where you ship or want to promote.
* **Keywords & Category**: Click **Edit** to tag your store for better discovery by influencers.

### Campaign & commission logic
Navigate to **Program Settings** to refine your program rules:

* **Time zone**: Set the reporting time zone for your orders.
* **Currency**: Select your payout currency and click **Save**.
* **Default Commission**: Set the base rate for partners (Percentage or Fixed Amount). Click **Save**.
* **Lock period**: The "Lock-in" time for orders (to account for returns/refunds). Defaults to the end of the month following the sale.
* **Tracking Gap**: Set the attribution window (how long after a click a sale is credited). Default is **30 Days**.
* **Authorization Setup**: Click **Start** and follow the prompts to authorize data syncing between Genstore and PartnerBoost.
* **Auto Approved Permission**: Click **Edit** to select which partner types (e.g., Content, Social) are approved automatically. *Recommendation: Enable this during the launch phase to scale quickly.*
* **Program Status**: Once settings are complete and your account is funded (minimum **$100**), you can set the program to **Active**.

### Email automation
Navigate to **Email Settings** to customize automated touchpoints for 24/7 recruitment:

* **Apply**: Sent automatically when a partner submits an application.
* **Declined**: Sent when an application does not meet your criteria.
* **Approve**: Sent when a partner is welcomed into your program.

## Go live & testing

1.  **Account review**: After finishing the setup, contact the PartnerBoost team to review and verify your account.
2.  **Launch**: Once approved, your campaign officially goes live in the marketplace.
3.  **Verification**: We highly recommend placing a **test order**. 
    > **Pro tip**: Use the tracking link provided by your Success Team. Ensure the test order uses a **different SKU or quantity** than any previous tests to confirm the tracking system is 100% accurate.

## Data flow & automation

Once integrated, data flows seamlessly across the ecosystem:

* **Member onboarding**: Partners join via your portal and receive unique tracking links/coupons.
* **Traffic conversion**: Customers click a link and complete a purchase on your Genstore site.
* **Real-time attribution**: The system identifies the partner's referral and syncs the order to PartnerBoost instantly.
* **Performance tracking**: Admins can monitor attribution, commissions, and ROI reports in real-time.
