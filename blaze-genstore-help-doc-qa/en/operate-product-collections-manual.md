# Manually create product collections

Creating product collections manually allows you to handpick products to add to a collection, giving you full control over its contents. This is especially useful for curated selections or special collections.

- Add specific products to the collection manually.
- Ideal when you need complete control over the collection, such as with curated or specially planned products.

## Operation steps

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to **Store** -> **Products** -> **Collections**.
2. Click the **Create product collection** button in the top-right corner.
3. Enter a **Title** and **Description** for your collection.
4. Under **Type**, select **Manual** to start adding products.
5. Search or filter products to select the ones you want to add. You can also delete products in bulk.
   - Sort products by **Sales**, **Alphabetical**, **Price (high to low)**, **Create Date**, or use **drag-and-drop** to manually reorder them.
1. SEO settings:
   - **Page title**: Default to the collection title. You can edit it. The recommended length is 0–70 characters.
   - **Meta description**: Default to the collection description. You can edit it. The recommended length is 0–200 characters.
   - **URL Handle:** Automatically generated with a 255-character limit. You can modify it, and the following conversion rules apply:
	 - Remove punctuation and special characters
	 - Replace consecutive spaces or underscores with `-`
	 - Replace consecutive `-` with a single `-`
	 - Convert to lowercase
7. Your collection will automatically be published on the online store.
8. In the **Cover image** section, upload an image to represent your collection. You can choose from the resource library, use a link, or upload a local image. Check the image size guidelines for more details.
9. Click **Save** to finish creating your product collection.

## Link product collections on product pages

You can also add a product to a collection when you [add a product](./operate-product-create#product-collections) or from the product details page.

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to the **Products** page.
2. In the product list, click the product you want to add to a collection, then click **Edit** in the **Actions** column.
3. In the **Collections** section on the right side of the product details page, select one or more existing collections.
4. Click **Save**, and the product will be added to the selected collections.

## Manage your product collections

Once created, your product collections will appear in the collection list on the **Collections** page. Here, you can:

1. **Edit collection**: Click **Edit** in the **Actions** column to update details like the title, description, images, filters, and SEO settings.
2. **Preview collection**: Click **Preview** in the **Actions** column to see the products included in the collection.
3. **Duplicate collection**: Click **Duplicate** in the **Actions** column to duplicate the collection. Later, you can adjust the details by editing the collection.
4. **Delete collection**: Click **Delete** in the **Actions** column to remove the collection. You can also select multiple collections for bulk deletion by checking the boxes next to them.
