# Tax forms

Drop Pay is a turnkey, KYC-free payment solution designed specifically for Genstore dropshipping businesses. In compliance with [U.S. tax laws and IRS regulations](https://www.irs.gov/), Genstore is required to collect specific tax forms to ensure transaction transparency and regulatory compliance.

> **IMPORTANT:** Payouts to your PayPal account will only be issued after your tax forms have been successfully submitted and approved by our compliance team.

## Determine which form you need

| Merchant Type | Tax Form | Official Link |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Non-U.S. Individual** | W-8BEN | [Get Form W-8BEN](https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw8ben.pdf) |
| **Non-U.S. Entity/Business** | W-8BEN-E | [Get Form W-8BEN-E](https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw8bene.pdf) |
| **U.S. Individual / Entity** | W-9 | [Get Form W-9](https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw9.pdf) |

## Execution guide

### Step 1: Complete and sign
* **Download the Form:** Obtain the correct document from the official IRS links provided above.
* **Fill & Sign:** Forms like the W-9 require a valid signature and date from an authorized representative of the business.
  * **Recommended Tools:** Use a PDF reader (e.g., Adobe Acrobat Reader or Foxit Reader).
  * **How to Sign:** Use the "Fill & Sign" or "E-Sign" tools within your PDF software to complete the document digitally.

### Step 2: Submit via Genstore admin
Once signed, follow these steps to upload your document:

1.  Log in to your **Genstore admin**.
2.  In the left sidebar, navigate to **Dropshipping** -> **[Balance](./operate-dropshipping-drop-pay-balance.md)**.
3.  Under the **Drop Pay** view, click the **Settings** button.
4.  Complete the following in the configuration window:
    * **Tax residency:** Select your applicable region (U.S. Resident or Non-U.S. Resident).
    * **Legal information:** Enter your full legal name (and optional registered business name).
    * **File upload:** Select the appropriate form type and upload your signed file (supported formats: JPEG, PNG, PDF).
5.  Click **Confirm** to submit.

## Important Notes

* **Accuracy Required:** You must provide truthful and accurate information for IRS auditing purposes. Providing false information may lead to account restrictions.
* **Review Period:** Submitted forms undergo a compliance review. Once approved, your automated payout schedule will be activated.
* **File Requirements:** Please ensure files are under **30MB**. Images or scans must be clear, and signatures must be fully visible
