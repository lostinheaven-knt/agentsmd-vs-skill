# Collaborate with AI agents

Genstore is built to be **AI-native**, changing the way you run your online store. Instead of navigating complex settings, you work with an AI team to handle everything from building your site to managing daily sales.

## How it works
We’ve automated the most time-consuming parts of e-commerce—setup, product management, design, and marketing. By collaborating with AI, you move from being a "manual operator" to a "business owner," focusing your energy on growth and strategy.

## Key terms to know
To get the most out of your AI team, here are a few basic terms we use:

| Term | What it means for you |
| :--- | :--- |
| **LUI** | You control your admin by talking to it, making the software work for you. |
| **Chat** | Where the work happens. Your instructions and the AI’s results stay here so you can always refer back to them. |
| **Prompt** | What you type into the chat. The clearer your prompt, the better the AI performs. |
| **Task** | A specific job the AI is doing, like "creating a product." You can track if a task is "in progress" or "done." |
| **Process** | Real-time feedback so you can see exactly what the AI is working on at that moment. |
| **Proposal** | The AI’s plan. It will show you a draft first; nothing goes live until you hit "confirm." |
| **Preview** | A window that lets you see design changes before they go live on your store. |

## Core AI capabilities
You can use natural language to trigger these specialized agents (we recommend starting in this order):

### [Launch agent - Ava](./start-genstore-ai-agent-store-setup.md)
**Go from zero to "open for business" in minutes.**
* **Example**: *"Help me launch my store in 3 minutes"*
* **How it helps**: The AI guides you through pre-filled settings for shipping, store name, and domains. Just confirm the details and you’re live.

### [Product agent - Sara](./start-genstore-ai-agent-product.md)
Automate your inventory, whether you stock your own goods or dropship.
- **Your own products (physical/digital products)**: Upload a photo or a quick description. The AI writes the professional details for you.
  - **Example**: *"Create a product listing for a new dress using this photo."*
  - **How it helps**: The AI creates a proposal; once you approve it, the product is live in your store instantly.
- **Smart sourcing**: For dropshippers, the AI finds trending, high-margin items from millions of options and imports them for you.
  - **Example**: *"Find some trending Christmas decor for my dropshipping store."*
  - **How it helps**: Curated best-sellers from a million-product database. Select your favorites and list them to your store with a single click.

### [Design agent - Dean](./operate-store-design-themes-ai-decorate.md)
**Professional design with zero technical skills.**
* **Example**: *"Halloween is coming up—give my store a spooky theme and add some holiday banners."*
* **How it helps**: The AI instantly updates your colors and designs custom banners to match your request.

### [Campaign agent - Olivia](./start-genstore-ai-agent-discount.md)
**Run smarter promotions without the guesswork.**
* **Example**: *"Set up a 20% discount for orders over $500 and announce it on the home page."*
* **How it helps**: The AI builds the discount logic and writes the announcement bar copy for you.

### [Visual agent](./start-genstore-ai-agent-visual.md)
**Your all-in-one expert for visual creativity.**
* **Example**: *"Generate a 5-second video of a model showcasing this piece of clothing."*
* **How it helps**: The AI transforms your static image into a dynamic video, perfect for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or e-commerce detail pages.

### Support agent - Ella
**Your 24/7 retail expert.**
* **Example**: *"How do I create a draft order?"*
* **How it helps**: Ella provides instant, step-by-step guides and direct links to the right menus.

### Analytics agent - Harvey
**Turn numbers into a growth plan.**
* **Example**: *"Show me my sales for the last week."*
* **How it helps**: The AI generates easy-to-read reports and gives you specific tips on how to improve your conversion rate.
