# Launch agent

The store launch agent Malik is your personal launch expert. We’ve designed a smart **Opening Wizard** so you can stop worrying about settings and start selling. By collaborating with AI, you can handle the essentials and get your business live in **under 3 minutes**.

## Core setup essentials

Skip the manual menus. Use the store launch agent to knock out these critical steps through simple conversation:

### Store name
Get your brand identity sorted in seconds:

**Option 1: AI-powered generation**
- **How to Start**: Type **"Help me pick a store name"** in the chat.
- **How it Works**:
    * **Smart Discovery**: AI asks about your business niche, style, and goals.
    * **Curated Picks**: Generates **3–5 tailored brand names** based on your unique input.
    * **Brand Strategy**: Provides a deep dive into the **meaning and brand associations** for every name.
    * **Unlimited Iterations**: Refine and regenerate as many times as you need until you find the "The One."   

**Option 2: Custom input**
- **How to Start**: Type **"Change my store name to [Name]"** in the chat.
- **How it Works**:
  * **Direct Entry**: Simply type in your existing brand name to get started.
  * **Real-time Availability Check**: Instantly verify if your name is available and ready for use.
  * **Expert Naming Tips (Optional)**: Access professional branding advice to further polish your name.   

### Domains
The AI offers three simple ways to set up your store's web address:

**Option 1: AI-powered generation**
- The AI recommends available domains based on your store name and industry.
- Provides a professional `.genmystore` extension.
- Automatically checks for availability in real-time.

**Option 2: Custom input**
- Enter the specific domain name you already have in mind.
- Automatically verifies if the domain is available for use.
- Provides instant setup instructions to claim your name.

**Option 3: Connect a third-party domain**
- The AI asks for your existing domain name (from providers like GoDaddy or Namecheap).
- Provides specific DNS configuration details tailored to your domain.
- Generates a step-by-step walkthrough to ensure a successful link.
- Automatically monitors the connection status until your site is live.

### Shipping, payments, and taxes
**For these more complex settings,** the agent acts as your expert navigator:
* **Shipping**: The AI pulls up the right pages and recommends shipping templates based on your location.
* **Payments**: The AI finds the payment setup screen for you and guides you through linking your payout method.
* **Taxes**: The AI leads you to the tax configuration area to help ensure your store stays compliant from day one.

## Smart opening wizard

This is your "fast track" to launching, ensuring you never miss a step before your first sale.

### How to start
* **Home page shortcut**: Click the "Launch agent" card at the bottom of your home page.
* **Just ask**: Tell the AI, *"Launch Guide"*

### How it works
Once you start, the agent takes the lead:
Once started, Malik will automatically handle the heavy lifting for you:
* **Brand your store**: The AI generates a personalized store name tailored to your business.
* **Secure your domain**: The AI pre-selects an available domain based on your new store name.
* **Configure logistics**: The AI matches and sets up the best default shipping template for your location.
* **Ready your payments**: The AI prepares the guided setup interface so you can start accepting payments immediately.

You can quickly review and fine-tune the AI's pre-configured settings:
* **Confirm or reset identity**: Apply the AI-generated store name and domain with one click, or ask Malik to generate new options.
* **Verify shipping and payments**: Quickly approve the default templates or jump in to edit specific details yourself.
