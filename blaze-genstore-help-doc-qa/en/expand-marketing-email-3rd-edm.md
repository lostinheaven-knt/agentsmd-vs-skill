# Integrate third-party email marketing tools

Genstore supports seamless integration with leading email marketing platforms, including **Klaviyo**, **Mailchimp**, and **Omnisend**. By syncing your store data and setting up automation workflows, you can build a powerful and personalized marketing system that boosts customer engagement and drives repeat purchases.

## Feature comparison

|Platform|Product data sync|Order data sync|Customer data sync|Unique strengths|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|**[Klaviyo](./expand-marketing-email-3rd-edm-klaviyo.md)**|✅|✅|✅|Supports behavior-based audience segmentation and personalized email templates|
|**[Mailchimp](./expand-marketing-email-3rd-edm-mailchimp.md)**|✅|✅|✅|Easy-to-use email builder and audience segmentation|
|**[Omnisend](./expand-marketing-email-3rd-edm-omnisend.md)**|✅|✅|✅|Supports email and SMS campaigns with behavior-based triggers|

## How it works

The integration process is similar across all platforms and typically includes the following steps:

1. **Get ready**  
    Make sure you have an account with the platform you want to integrate. Prepare any required information such as List, Audience, or store URL.
2. **Install the app**  
    Go to the Genstore App Store, search for the official integration app, and install it.
3. **Authorize your account**  
    Log in to your third-party platform account and grant the necessary permissions to complete the connection.
4. **Configure audience sync**  
    Select a target List or Audience for syncing your customer data. This will be used for future email campaigns.
5. **Sync your data**  
    Sync up to three months of your store’s product, customer, and order data. New data will continue to sync automatically after setup.
6. **Set up automation**  
    Access prebuilt campaign templates in your Genstore admin and jump directly to the external platform to finish setup.

## Notes and tips

- All data syncs are handled via authorized API calls—please ensure permissions are properly configured.
- Automation workflows must use synced events as triggers to function correctly.
- If you see a message about “unverified apps” during the authorization process, rest assured that Genstore’s official integrations are safe to use.
- We recommend regularly reviewing sync status and automation performance to ensure accurate and timely email delivery.
