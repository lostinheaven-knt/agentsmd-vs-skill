
# Genstore Marketplace after-sales policy

This document is the official after-sales policy of Genstore, applicable to all orders fulfilled through **Genstore Dropshipping**.
Please read the following terms carefully before initiating any after-sales requests. All after-sales requests must be submitted through the **Genstore platform**.

## General after-sales terms

Except for specific interpretations noted below, Genstore accepts merchants’ refund, reshipment, or return requests under any of the following circumstances:

### 1. Order delay

Orders without valid tracking information, or orders that remain *in transit* or *pending* more than **60 days** after being shipped from the supplier’s warehouse, may qualify for after-sales requests.
Exceptions and variations apply to specific countries and shipping methods:

* a. For orders shipped to **the United States**, the order is considered valid within **45 days** from the date the product leaves the supplier’s warehouse.
* b. For orders shipped to **Brazil**, the order is considered valid within **110 days** from the shipment date.
* c. In the following circumstances, Genstore **will not** accept or process any after-sales request:
  * The package is held at a local post office due to **incomplete address**, **unclaimed parcel**, or **invalid phone number**. In such cases, the customer is advised to contact the post office directly and collect the parcel.
  * In some countries, due to local customs, phone number rules, or religious practices, packages may be delivered to **self-pickup lockers**. Customers should collect the parcel proactively from the designated pickup point.
  * In some cases, logistics tracking may show the package as *delivered* while the customer reports non-receipt. Genstore will assist in verifying the situation with the supplier’s logistics provider, but the investigation may take **1–2 months** and **a positive outcome cannot be guaranteed**.

### 2. Customer did not receive the package

If the customer’s order tracking shows *delivered*, Genstore reserves the right to **decline refund or reshipment requests** under such circumstances.
* a. If the customer provides an official **“undelivered” certificate** issued by the local post office (with stamp) or if the carrier confirms a **lost package**, Genstore will coordinate with the carrier to pursue a refund or reshipment. (Success is not guaranteed.)
* b. Refund or reshipment will not be accepted if the tracking record shows one of the following alerts:
  1. Incorrect or incomplete address.
  2. Invalid tracking number.
  3. Insufficient recipient information.
  4. Package refused by recipient.
  5. Package not collected in time.
  6. No safe delivery point.
  7. Customs clearance failed.
  8. Other similar reasons.

**Important explanations:**

* a. Local couriers may attempt delivery several times. If the customer fails to collect the parcel during the delivery window, it may be returned to the local post office and held for **3–7 days**. The customer must collect it within that period. If the parcel is returned to the sender and gets **lost or damaged during the return transit**, Genstore is **not liable**.
* b. For **undelivered or destroyed packages**, if the merchant does not take timely action (e.g., fails to provide reshipment details or pay redelivery fees within the required time), Genstore **will not issue a refund or reshipment**.

### 3. Product damage

If the customer receives a **severely damaged** product, Genstore will offer either a **refund** or **replacement reshipment**.
For **minor damage** (e.g., loose threads, slight wrinkles, small scratches), Genstore may offer **partial refund** or **replacement** as appropriate.

#### Additional definitions:

1. For **fragile products**, refund is strongly recommended. If the item is damaged and the customer requests reshipment, Genstore will provide **only one reshipment opportunity**. Any further damage is not eligible for refund or reshipment.
2. For **damaged outer packaging boxes** caused by international transit, Genstore **does not provide any refund or after-sales service**.
3. For **regular electronic products**, customers must request after-sales support **within 30 days** after delivery.

### 4. Wrong or missing products

Genstore’s partner suppliers perform strict quality checks before shipment.
For incorrect or missing products, Genstore will handle the issue as follows:

1. For **incorrect products**, Genstore may offer refund or reshipment.
2. For **incorrect color or size** that does not affect product functionality, Genstore will offer refund or reshipment if you provide a **customer complaint screenshot** (including customer name, message content, and date).
3. For **missing components** that do not affect product functionality, Genstore will issue a **partial refund** or **resend the missing part**. If missing components affect the product’s function, Genstore will resend a **complete replacement**.
4. For **accessories**, Genstore will resend the missing or incorrect accessory.

### 5. Order cancellation

* If the order has **not yet been processed** by the supplier’s warehouse, Genstore will issue a refund.

## Additional important explanations

### 1. After-sales request period

If the order status is **Closed**, Genstore cannot process any after-sales request.
According to supplier cooperation agreements, after-sales requests must be submitted **within 7 days after delivery**.
After 7 days, the after-sales request entry will be closed.
For some **electronic products**, the request window may be extended to **30 days** after delivery if the product is damaged. Final confirmation is subject to Genstore’s after-sales support team.

### 2. Force majeure

Genstore shall not be held liable for any product damage or shipping delay caused by **force majeure** events, including but not limited to: pandemics, international conflicts, strikes, wars, earthquakes, floods, viruses, storms, snow, or customs inspections.

### 4. Returns

Due to the high cost and long transit time of returning products to supplier warehouses (which may take several months), and the high likelihood of damage or loss during transport, **returns are not recommended** unless strictly necessary.
If a return is required, the customer must send the product back **within 30 days of receipt**.

## Non-eligible after-sales cases

Genstore does not accept after-sales requests under the following circumstances:

* The buyer dislikes the product due to personal preference.
* The product description differs from the buyer’s subjective expectation.
* The product has an odor.
* The buyer ordered the wrong product or SKU.
* The buyer provided an incorrect shipping address.
* Product differences were confirmed and agreed upon prior to purchase.
* The tracking information was deleted by the logistics company or local post office.
* The recipient failed to complete customs clearance procedures according to the destination country’s regulations, causing the package to be returned, discarded, or held.
