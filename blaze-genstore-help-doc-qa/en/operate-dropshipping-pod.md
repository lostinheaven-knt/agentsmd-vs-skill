# Genstore POD

Genstore offers two distinct Dropshipping models to suit your business needs:
- [Marketplace (Standard Dropshipping)](./operate-dropshipping-product-sourcing.md): Best for merchants looking to sell pre-existing, ready-to-ship products. This model features a one-click sync of trending global products with no design work required.
- **Print on Demand (POD)**: Best for merchants looking to build a brand IP or offer custom products. This model focuses on creative customization and made-to-order production. And this page focuses on the Genstore POD model.   

Genstore POD is an all-in-one solution integrating product sourcing, custom design tools, automated or manual fulfillment, order tracking, and unified settlement.

## Business scenarios
- **Scenario 1**: Creators / KOLs / IP Owners: Create branded merchandise (e.g., T-shirts, hoodies, stickers) using your original artwork, logos, or viral content with zero upfront inventory costs.
- **Scenario 2**: Brand Entrepreneurs: Start your own apparel line (e.g., streetwear, fitness, or lifestyle brands) without owning a factory, leveraging high-quality printing techniques like DTG or embroidery.
- **Scenario 3**: Group Customization: Provide small-batch custom apparel for local events or organizations, ensuring fast delivery and consistent quality.

## Core features: sourcing & design
Before launching your products, use these tools to create and push your designs:

- **Product catalog**: Access a massive pool of reliable suppliers offering various high-quality "blank" products ready for customization.
- **[Express design](./operate-dropshipping-pod-designer.md)**: Designed for merchants without professional design experience; simply upload images or add text to generate products instantly.
- **[Professional design](./operate-dropshipping-pod-designer-advanced.md)**: Features multi-layer management and precise print-area positioning. Use detailed mockups to satisfy high-end design needs for brand building or IP collaborations.

## Design mode

To help you choose the most suitable tool for your current business needs, please refer to the table below:

| Dimension | [Express design](./operate-dropshipping-pod-designer.md) | [Professional design](./operate-dropshipping-pod-designer-advanced.md) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Positioning** | Easy to start; ideal for simple graphic and text combinations | Professional customization; ideal for brand building and complex designs |
| **Core Advantages** | **One-click rendering for 400+ products**, bulk publishing | **Multi-layer management**, high-precision printing for multiple positions |
| **Design Flexibility** | Basic image uploads and text editing | Supports asset libraries, premium assets, and pattern/text fills |
| **Preview Effect** | Real-time preview in default positions | **Custom Mockup** configuration and management |
| **Use Cases** | Rapid market testing, simple merchandise for KOLs | Flagship streetwear, IP collaborations, complex All-Over Print (AOP) products |
