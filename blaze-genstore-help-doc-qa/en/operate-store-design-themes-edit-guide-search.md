# Search page

The search results page displays products that match a customer’s keywords. It supports advanced filtering and sorting options to help customers quickly find what they need. Flexible layout and interaction settings can significantly improve the search experience and conversion rate.

## How to access

1. Log in to your Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Themes**.
3. Find your theme and click **Customize** to open the theme editor.
4. In the top navigation bar, open the page selector and choose **Search**.

You can configure the **Search** page under **Segment template** in the left panel and adjust display styles, filters, and sorting options in the right panel.

## Style and layout settings

The search results page offers multiple visual and layout options to optimize product presentation:

- **Display style**: Choose “small” or “large” mode for different visual styles and information density.
- **Product count**: Set the number of products per page and define the number of columns for desktop and mobile.
- **Spacing and color**: Adjust card spacing (small / medium / large) and apply light or dark mode to match your brand theme.
- **Product cards**:
    - **Image ratio**: Options include “fit,” 3:4, and 1:1 to accommodate various product categories.
    - **Product info**: Choose whether to show vendor details, price, or enable a hover image for alternate views.
    - **Quick actions**: Enable “Quick add to cart” to boost conversions.
## Filter and sort settings

To help customers find products faster, the search page supports:

- **Filters**: Allow filtering by price, inventory, and more. Merchants can customize filter options through the [Search & Discovery app](./operate-store-design-search-discovery.md).
- **Filter layout**: On desktop, choose horizontal, vertical, or drawer-style layouts.
- **Sorting**: Enable sorting by date, price, or popularity.
