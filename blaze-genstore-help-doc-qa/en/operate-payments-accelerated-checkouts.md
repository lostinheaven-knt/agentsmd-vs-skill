# Accelerated checkout

You can enable the accelerated checkout feature on Genstore when using **Genstore Payments** or **PayPal**. This feature makes the checkout process faster and more convenient for your customers.

Accelerated checkout improves the online shopping experience by allowing customers to complete their purchases quickly—without having to re-enter payment or shipping details. It’s especially beneficial for customers who have already saved their information, such as repeat buyers. Here are the key features and benefits:

## Key features

- **Simplified process**: Reduces checkout steps with pre-filled or one-click payment options.
- **Supports multiple payment methods**: Includes popular options like **Apple Pay**, **Google Pay**, and **PayPal**.
- **Auto-fills user data**: Automatically fills in necessary fields based on saved payment, billing, or shipping information.
- **Faster checkout**: Customers can complete their purchase more quickly, which can increase conversion rates.

## Best use cases

- **Mobile shopping**: The quick checkout feature is especially useful on mobile devices.
- **Repeat customers**: Streamline the buying process for regular customers with saved information.
- **Impulse purchases**: Make it easier for customers to complete a purchase quickly, reducing barriers for spontaneous buys.

### Advantages

- **Higher conversion rates**: By reducing checkout obstacles, you’ll lower cart abandonment rates.
- **Improved user experience**: A fast, convenient checkout process boosts customer satisfaction.
- **Increased trust**: Using trusted payment methods like PayPal and Apple Pay helps customers feel secure.

## Payment options and platform support

### Genstore Payments

If you use **Genstore Payments**, you can enable **Apple Pay** or **Google Pay** to offer an accelerated checkout. Simply activate these options in the **Genstore Payments** settings to give your customers a quick and easy checkout experience.

### PayPal Express Checkout

To use **PayPal Express Checkout**, ensure that:

- Your PayPal account is a business account.
- Your Genstore store is authorized with PayPal.

Once set up, customers won’t need to enter checkout details again. They can pay using the information saved in their PayPal account.

## Activate accelerated checkout button

The accelerated checkout button lets customers complete their purchase directly from the product page, cart drawer, or shopping cart page, using saved payment details like **Apple Pay**, **Google Pay**, or **PayPal**. For detailed setup instructions, check out [Activate accelerated checkout button](./operate-payments-accelerated-checkouts-button.md).
