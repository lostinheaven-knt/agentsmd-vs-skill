# Create a knowledge base from template

To help you build an effective AI Agent knowledge base faster, Genstore provides a **Create from template** feature within the Knowledge base setup page. These templates are built around industry best practices and give you a clear, structured foundation that helps the AI better understand your business and deliver high-quality responses.

## Key features

- **Built-in best-practice templates**: All templates follow proven industry guidelines for structuring knowledge documents, including standardized formatting, tone, and sample content.
- **Language selection supported**: When creating a template, you can choose your preferred language. We recommend using **English** for optimal AI understanding and response accuracy.
- **Personalized placeholders**: Editable fields within the template are marked using `double curly brackets`, so you can quickly replace them with your store-specific information without starting from scratch.

## How to use

1. Log in to your Genstore Admin and go to **Sales channels -> Inbox**
2. In the **AI Agent** section, click **Knowledge base**
3. Click **Create from template** in the top-right corner
4. In the pop-up window, select a language version (we recommend **English**)
5. The system will generate a standard knowledge base template. You can now edit it using either of the following approaches:
   - **Quick replace mode**: Simply fill in the `double curly brackets` placeholders with your own content
   - **Custom rewrite mode**: Keep the template’s structure but rewrite the content to better match your brand tone
6. When finished, click **Save**. The AI Agent will begin referencing the new content immediately.

## Tips for configuration

- **Use English if possible**: AI performs best when trained on English content. Even if your customer interface is in another language, the AI can translate before responding.
- **Review and update regularly**: Whenever your business or policies change, make sure to update the knowledge base content accordingly.
- **Keep the structure intact**: If you rewrite the content, we recommend retaining the section headings and format from the template. This helps the AI parse and understand your information more effectively.
