# Customer management

This section provides a complete guide to managing customer data within Genstore. You'll learn how to handle customer accounts, data, marketing subscriptions, tax exemptions, tags, account merging, deletion, and how to erase personal data.

## Topics of this chapter

- [Customer accounts](./operate-customers-manage-account.md): This section defines customer accounts, explains account statuses, and covers customer invitations. It gives you a full overview of customer account management.
- [Add/Edit customers](./operate-customers-manage-add-modify.md): Here, you'll find step-by-step instructions on how to add or update customer information on Genstore, ensuring your customer records stay accurate and up-to-date.
- [Delete customers](./operate-customers-manage-delete.md): Learn when and how to delete customer data securely, in compliance with your business policies.
- [Import/Export customers](./operate-customers-manage-import-export.md): This section covers how to bulk import or export customer data, essential for tasks like data migration and backup.
- [Merge customers](./operate-customers-manage-merge.md): Find out how to merge customer accounts to clean up duplicate data and streamline your customer management process.
- [Delete customer personal data](./operate-customers-manage-erase-personal-data.md): In accordance with privacy regulations, this guide explains how to permanently delete customer personal data from your system.
- [Export customer personal data](./operate-customers-manage-request-personal-data.md): Provides detailed instructions on how to access customer personal data in accordance with privacy policy requirements.
- [Manage customer marketing subscriptions](./operate-customers-manage-marketing-subscription.md): Learn how to manage customer subscriptions to email and SMS marketing, including how to subscribe, unsubscribe, and track their status.
- [Set customer tax exemption](./operate-customers-manage-tax-exemption.md): This section shows you how to set up tax-exempt status for certain customers, either globally or for specific regions.
- [Manage customer tags](./operate-customers-manage-tags.md): Understand how to apply and manage customer tags, helping you group and identify customers based on specific criteria.
