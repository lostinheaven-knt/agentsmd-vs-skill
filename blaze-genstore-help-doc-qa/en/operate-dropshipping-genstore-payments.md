# Genstore Payments

If you are looking to further boost your checkout conversion rates and establish stronger brand authority, you can choose **Genstore Payments** as the primary payment method for your Dropshipping business.

## Key advantages

* **Superior Conversion Power:** Native support for **Accelerated Checkout** (Apple Pay, Google Pay, etc.) provides customers with a frictionless shopping experience.
* **Professional Brand Authority:** A more formal checkout interface and multi-currency support build trust and buyer confidence.
* **Full Business Coverage:** Beyond Dropshipping and POD, this solution supports all self-managed/custom inventory in your store.

## How to activate

1.  Log in to your **Genstore admin**.
2.  In the left sidebar, navigate to **Dropshipping** -> **Balance**.
3.  Use the view toggle in the top right corner of the page to switch to **Genstore Payments**.
4.  Within the Genstore Payments view, click the **Activate Genstore Payments** button.
5.  The system will guide you to the onboarding page. Please fill out the application based on your legal entity (e.g., **US** or **HK**) and submit it for review.
6.  Once approved, return to this page and click **Activate**. The system will automatically disable Drop Pay at this time.

> **TIP:** Once activation is successful, all subsequent transactions will be processed through Genstore Payments. Please ensure your settlement bank account information is accurate to avoid payout delays.
