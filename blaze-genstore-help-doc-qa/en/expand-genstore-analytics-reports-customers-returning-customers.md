# Returning customers

**The Returning customers report** displays data about all your customers whose order history includes **two or more orders**.

## Use this report to

- **Identify high-value customers**  
	Repeat customers typically have higher lifetime value and stronger purchase intent. Use metrics like order count and total spend to evaluate their contribution and develop exclusive rewards or loyalty programs.
- **Analyze repurchase behavior and timing**  
	Look at the gap between a customer’s first and most recent order to understand their buying cycle. Use this insight to improve the timing of product recommendations and customer communications.

## Report data fields

You can find the following details for each customer:
- their name
- their email address
- whether they agreed to accept marketing when they placed their most recent order
- the date of their first order
- the number of orders that they have placed
- the average amount that they have spent per order
- the order amount, **excluding discounts**, includes taxes, shipping fees, and all refunds.
