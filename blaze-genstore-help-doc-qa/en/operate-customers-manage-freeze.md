# Freeze/Unfreeze customers

When managing customers, you may need to freeze certain accounts due to fraudulent behavior or suspicious activity. Genstore provides tools for freezing and unfreezing customer accounts.

### Freeze a customer

::: tip

- Only **activated** accounts can be frozen. The freeze option is not available for inactive accounts.
- Frozen customers will no longer be able to log in to your store. Please proceed with caution.
:::

To freeze a customer:

1. Log in to your **Genstore** admin.
2. Go to the **Store -> Customers** section and open the **customer detail page**.
3. Click **More actions** and select **Freeze account**.
4. Confirm the action in the pop-up by clicking **Freeze account**.

Once successful, the customer status will change to **Freeze**.

:::tip
Freezing only affects the current account. Customers can still register a new account using a different email address.
:::

### Unfreeze a customer

To unfreeze a customer, you can resend an account activation invitation:

1. Log in to your **Genstore** admin.
2. Go to the **Store -> Customers** section and open the **customer detail page**.
3. Click **Send invitation**.

Once the customer receives the email and completes password setup via the activation link, the account will be reactivated and login access will be restored.
