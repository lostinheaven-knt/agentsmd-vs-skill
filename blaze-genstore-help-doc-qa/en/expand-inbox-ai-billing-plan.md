# Inbox AI pricing plan

Each store gets 200 AI Agent replies for free. Once the free quota is used up, you'll be charged based on actual AI replies.

## What counts as one AI Agent reply?

An AI Agent reply is counted each time the AI sends a complete automated response to a customer message.

- **What’s included**:
    - Any AI-generated text, links, or suggested replies
    - Every AI response in a multi-turn conversation is counted separately
- **What’s not included**:
    - Messages where the AI doesn’t provide a response
    - System prompts when transferring to a live agent
    - Standard non-personalized phrases like “Thank you for your message”

> Example: If a customer asks three different questions and receives three AI-generated responses, it counts as **3 replies**.

## Free usage

- **Free quota**: Each store receives a one-time lifetime allowance of 200 AI Agent replies
- **Availability**: Available to all stores, no paid plan required
- **Validity**: Free quota doesn’t expire and stays available until fully used

## Pricing after free quota

- **Billing method**: Charged per AI Agent reply
- **Rate**: $0.05 per reply

## Example calculation

Let’s say your store used 1,528 AI replies in a given billing cycle. If you still had your free 200 replies available, your billable usage would be:

```
1,328 × $0.05 = $66.40
```

If your free quota was already used up, the full usage would be billed:

```
1,528 × $0.05 = $76.40
```

## View charges

Inbox AI charges will appear on your Genstore bill. You can review your billing details under **Settings** -> **Billing**.
