# **Settings**

This chapter covers **Genstore’s** notification settings, policy management, customer privacy, and content asset management.

- [Notification management](./settings-notifications.md): Set up notifications in **Settings -> Notifications**, verify sender email, and enable or disable email, SMS, and backend notifications.
- [Content management](./settings-media.md): Upload and manage images, videos, and files in **Settings -> Content**. Supports multiple formats with storage limits from **100GB to 300GB**, depending on the plan.
- [Tax management](./taxes-overview.md): Manage the tax settings for your sales regions in **Settings -> Taxes and duties**, including the taxes on products sold and the related transaction fees.
- [Markets](./global-set-product-prices-by-country.md):
  - [Set product prices by country/region](./global-set-product-prices-by-country.md): Set product prices for different countries/regions to better manage your e-commerce store targeting international or multi-regional markets.
  - [View product prices by country/region](./global-set-product-prices-by-country.md): View product price details for different markets to ensure pricing accuracy and consistency.
- [**Domains**](./global-domains.md): Manage the domain settings for customer access to your store in **Settings -> Domains**.
- [Policy management](./settings-policy.md): Create and manage return, privacy, and terms of service policies in **Settings -> Policies**. Use templates or customize content. Policy links automatically appear in the checkout footer.
- [Customer privacy management](./settings-policy.md): Protect customer privacy and comply with privacy laws by configuring **Customer privacy** settings in the Genstore admin.
