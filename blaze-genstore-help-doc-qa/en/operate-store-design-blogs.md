# Blogs

A blog is a powerful content marketing tool for online stores. By regularly posting articles about products, industry trends, and helpful information, a blog not only helps build trust and communication with customers but also boosts your store’s search engine ranking, attracting more potential customers.

## Key Benefits

- **Improve SEO**: Optimize articles with targeted keywords to improve your search engine ranking.
- **Enhance brand authority**: Publish high-quality content to showcase your expertise and build brand trust.
- **Attract potential customers**: Share valuable content to engage and retain your target audience.
- **Strengthen customer relationships**: Use comments and interactions to deepen engagement with customers.
- **Support product sales**: Promote products through your blog content to drive sales indirectly.

## Create blog post

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to **Store** -> **Online Store -> Blog posts**.
2. Click **Create blog post** in the top-right corner.
3. In the pop-up window, set your blog title, summary, body, SEO settings, publish date, cover image, author details, and more, then click **Save**. The new blog will automatically appear in the **Blog articles** list. For more details, see [Create blog post](./operate-store-design-blogs-create.md).

## Preview your blog post

After saving your blog posts, you can preview how it will appear in your online store in real time.

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin** and go to **Store** -> **Online Store -> Blog posts**.
2. In the blog post list, click the **Preview** icon in the action column to open a new window and view the blog’s display in your **online store**.
3. At the bottom status bar, you can check the blog post status (such as hidden or visible), view the current theme name, and either **exit preview** or click **Customize theme** to open the theme editor for customization.

## Blog collections

Genstore allows you to organize your blogs into collections, making it easier to group related posts together.

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and go to **Store** -> **Online Store -> Blog posts**.
2. Click **Manage blog collection** in the top-right corner.
3. Here, you can perform the following actions:
   - **Edit**: Click **Edit** next to a blog entry to modify its content.
   - **Delete**: Click **Delete** to remove a blog. Note: Once deleted, all associated articles will also be removed and cannot be restored.
   - **Preview**: Click **Preview** to see how the blog will appear to visitors, ensuring the content displays as expected.
4. To create a new blog collection, click **Create blog collection**, where you can
   - Edit the blog collection title
   - Optimize SEO settings for better search engine visibility
   - Manage comments: Choose to disable comments, enable them, or allow comments with approval required before they appear online.

## Preview your blog collection

After saving your blog collection, you can preview how it will appear in your online store in real time.

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin** and go to **Store** -> **Online Store -> Blog posts**.
2. Click **Manage blog collection**.
3. In the blog collection list, click **Preview** in the action column to open a new window and view the collection’s display in your **online store**.
4. At the bottom status bar, you can view the current theme name, and either **exit preview** or click **Customize theme** to open the theme editor for customization.

## Manage blog comments

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and navigate to **Store** -> **Online Store -> Blog posts** -> **Manage comments**.
2. View the comments for your blog collections. You can take the following actions:
   - Mark as **Spam**: Flag comments as spam if they are inappropriate or irrelevant.
   - **Delete**: Remove comments that are no longer needed.

## Add blog navigation menu

To make it easier for customers to access your blog, you can add a link to the blog page in your store's navigation:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store -> Menus**.
3. Click **Create menu**, then fill in the navigation title and URL handle.
4. In **Menu items**, click **Add menu item**, name it (e.g., "Product Blog"), and link it to the blog page, then click **Save**.
5. Click **Save** to finalize and save your blog navigation settings.
