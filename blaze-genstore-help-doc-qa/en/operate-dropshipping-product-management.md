# Manage dropshipping products

After sourcing products, merchants can **push them directly to their connected store** with one click.
When products are pushed, the system automatically syncs key information such as titles, descriptions, images, and prices.
You can then review and customize each product to match your brand tone and merchandising strategy.

## Push products to your store

When a product is pushed to your store, the system automatically syncs the following information:

| Field                   | Description                                                                   | Editable after push   |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| **Product title**       | Synced from the sourced product. You can modify it to match your brand style. | ✅ Editable            |
| **Key features**  | Synced from the sourced product. You can add or revise content.               | ✅ Editable            |
| **Images / videos**     | Synced from the sourced product. You can replace or upload new media.         | ✅ Editable            |
| **Product description** | Synced from the sourced product. You can refine and optimize the copy.        | ✅ Editable            |
| **Category**            | Automatically mapped to the corresponding product category.                   | ❌ Not editable        |
| **Variants**            | Only price and compare-at price are editable.                                 | ⚠️ Partially editable |
| **Status**              | Default status is **Active** after push.                                       | ✅ Editable        |
| **Sales channels**      | Default channel is **Online store**.                                          | ✅ Editable            |
| **Vendor**              | Synced from the supplier. You can adjust as needed.                           | ✅ Editable            |
| **Tags**                | Synced from the sourced product. You can add or adjust tags.                  | ✅ Editable            |
| **Collections**         | Automatically or manually assigned. You can update the placement.             | ✅ Editable            |

## Product status and quantity limits

Pushed products are set to **Active** status by default. You can adjust the product status when pushing it to your store:

1. Navigate to **Dropshipping** -> **Settings**.
2. Under **Product status**, choose the desired status: 
   - **Push as draft for editing**: Products are saved as drafts for review and adjustments before going live.
   - **Push as active and edit later**: Products will go live immediately with supplier content, and you can edit them later in the **Product**. For more details, please refer to [Add product](./operate-product-create.md).

Each subscription plan includes a fixed limit on the number of products you can push:
  * When the limit is reached, new products cannot be pushed.
  * If your plan is downgraded, the system will prompt you to unpublish or remove excess products.


## Best practices

* Review and adjust product titles, descriptions, and media to match your brand identity.
* Update prices or sort order manually based on your sales strategy.
* Use tags and collections to organize products and improve visibility across your storefront.

## Quickly identify pushed dropshipping products

After pushing, Dropshipping products will appear in the product list. You can quickly distinguish Dropshipping products from self-built products using the Source column:

1. In the Genstore merchant admin, navigate to the **Store** -> **Products** page.
2. If the **Source** column shows **Dropshipping**, it means the product was pushed to the store via Dropshipping. Conversely, if it’s empty, the product is self-built by the merchant.
3. If the **Source** column is not displayed on the product list page, click the column icon to add it.

## Showcase your dropshipping products in your store

Products pushed through the Dropshipping channel will automatically be assigned the Dropshipping product template.
- **PC display rules**: By default, the product description area is on the left side of the page with a "hover to expand" interaction. Merchants can also customize the template style for Dropshipping products.

### Customize the template

1. In Genstore merchant admin, click **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Themes**.
2. Click **Customize** of your live theme to open the theme editor.
3. Switch to **Products**.
4. Select the Dropshipping product template, preview it, and customize the page template as needed.

For more details on customizing the template, please refer to [Customize with the theme editor](./operate-store-design-themes-edit-guide.md).
