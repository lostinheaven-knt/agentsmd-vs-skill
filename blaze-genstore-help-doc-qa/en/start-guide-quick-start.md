# Quick start

Welcome to Genstore! We’ve streamlined the e-commerce journey using AI. At Genstore, you don't need to be a developer or a designer. Simply describe your vision to our AI assistant, and you'll have a professional storefront ready for the global market in minutes.

## Step 1: Start the conversation
After signing up, you’ll be greeted by the **AI chat interface**. This is your command center.
* **How it works**: Simply describe what you want to sell in plain English. No technical jargon required.
* **Need inspiration?**: If you're stuck, click the **"Prompt inspiration"** cards (e.g., *"Body-positive vintage store"* or *"Playful makeup brand"*) to kickstart the process instantly.

## Step 2: Confirm category and style
Our AI analyzes your description to build a custom brand identity based on two pillars:
* **Niche refinement**: The AI narrows down broad ideas into profitable categories (e.g., turning "Apparel" into "Curated womenswear" or "Sustainable streetwear").
* **Visual branding**: It recommends a high-converting aesthetic, such as **"Modern minimalist"** or **"Vintage chic."**

::: tip
Not feeling the vibe? Just tell the AI *"Change the style"* or *"Try another category."* It will pivot and offer new options immediately.
:::

## Step 3: Add your products
Genstore offers two flexible ways to stock your digital shelves, depending on your business model:

### Option A: Dropshipping (The "hands-off" model)
* **Best for**: Entrepreneurs who want to launch without upfront inventory costs or warehouse overhead.
* **How it works**: Choose between reselling curated items from our Marketplace or launching your own brand with our POD custom design tools.
* **The edge**: Zero inventory risk. When a customer orders, the supplier ships directly to them. You focus on marketing; we handle the logistics.

### Option B: Upload your own (The "brand-owner" model)
* **Best for**: Artisans, creators, or merchants with existing physical stock.
* **How it works**: Click **"Upload images"** to add your product photos.
* **The edge**: Our AI "scans" your product photos to automatically match the website’s UI colors and tone-of-voice to your specific items.
* **Note**: If your professional photos aren't ready yet, click **"Skip upload"** and add them later from your admin.

## Step 4: Initialization and preview
Once your products are added, Genstore’s engine builds your backend and frontend infrastructure.
* **Speed**: Setup is lightning-fast, usually taking just a few minutes.
* **Privacy**: Your site is initially password-protected. Use your unique **access password** to review and fine-tune your store before the public sees it.

## Step 5: Go live
When your store looks exactly how you want it, hit **"Publish"** to open your doors to the world.
* **Unlock features**: Publishing activates your full suite of marketing tools, payment gateways, and analytics.
* **AI assistance**: Your dedicated **AI Agent** will remain available to help you navigate your dashboard and grow your sales.

## FAQ

### Q1. Can I create more than one store?
> **A**: Yes. Click your **Store logo** in the top-left corner of the Genstore admin, hit the dropdown arrow, and select **"Create store"** at the bottom.

### Q2. Are the dropshipping suppliers reliable?
> **A**: We vet every supplier for quality and shipping speed. The sourcing library provides transparent wholesale pricing, high-res imagery, and real-time inventory tracking. Once a sale is made, the fulfillment process is automated to ensure a seamless customer experience.
