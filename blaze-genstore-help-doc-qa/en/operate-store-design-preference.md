# Preferences settings

The **Preferences** section allows you to manage your store's accessibility and customize how your brand appears on search engines and social media platforms.

## Store access permissions
This section controls who can view your online store and manages your brand's presence on the storefront.

- **Remove branding**: Toggle this switch to enable or disable Genstore branding on your online store storefront. For more information, please refer to the [Genstore Branding](./operate-store-design-preference-branding.md) section.
- **Password protection**: Restrict access to your online store by setting a password. Visitors will be required to enter this password to view your site.
  - **Note**: You may need to upgrade your plan to automatically remove password protection.

## Social sharing image and SEO
Configure your store's digital "business card" for social media and search engines. When you share your store link, these settings determine the preview image and text shown.

### Social sharing image
A high-quality image improves click-through rates on platforms like Facebook or WhatsApp.

- **Default behavior**: If no custom image is added, your store theme logo is usually displayed.
- **How to add**: Click the Add image box to upload a specific image from your device.
- **Suggested dimensions**: Use 1200 * 628 pixels for the best display quality across different platforms.

### Search engine optimization (SEO)
Customize how your homepage is presented in search engine results.

- **Homepage title**: Enter a concise title for your store (up to 70 characters).
- **Meta description**: Write an engaging summary of your business (up to 320 characters) to encourage potential customers to click.

## Saving your changes
After making any adjustments to your store preferences, ensure you click the Save button at the top right of the web page to apply your updates.
