# Discounts

Genstore offers a variety of discount types designed to help merchants attract customers, boost sales, and improve customer satisfaction based on different promotional needs. With customizable discount strategies, merchants can apply the most suitable discounts for various scenarios, encouraging purchases and increasing the average order value. Genstore’s discount types include product discounts, order discounts, and shipping discounts, catering to the needs of different merchants.

### Product discounts

Applicable for promoting new products or clearing inventory, product-specific or product-series discounts include:

- **[Amount off products](./expand-sales-discounts-product)**: Directly reduce the product price.
- **[Buy x get y](./expand-sales-discounts-buy-x-get-y-free)**: Offer discounts when a certain quantity or amount is reached.
- **[Fixed bundle](./expand-sales-discounts-fixed-bundle)**: Sell specific product combinations at a discounted price.

### Order discounts

This promotion aims to increase the average order value by offering [amount off the total order](./expand-sales-discounts-order). When the order amount meets a certain threshold, the system will automatically apply a fixed amount or percentage discount.

### Shipping discounts

Provide [free shipping services](./expand-sales-discounts-shipping) for eligible orders.

### Discount code

Genstore offers both automatic and manual options for each discount type. Manual discounts utilize [discount codes](./expand-sales-discounts-discount-codes.md), which are either system-generated or custom codes, suitable for distribution across various marketing channels, including email.
