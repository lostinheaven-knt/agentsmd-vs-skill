# Physical products

A physical product refers to a tangible item that can be touched and stored, such as clothing, home goods, or electronics. These products typically require inventory management and delivery through shipping or logistics.

## Add a physical product

### Steps

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin**, navigate to **Store** -> **Products** -> **Add product** -> **Physical products**.
2. Set product title, key features, description, and images:
   - **Images**: Add product images by dragging them into the **Media** section or clicking to upload. Multiple images are supported, and you can rearrange them.
   - **Description**: Supports **rich text formatting** for detailed product descriptions.
4. **Product category**: Select the appropriate category for the product.
5. **Product variants**: Supports multiple variants such as color, size, and style, which can be customized as needed.
6. **Pricing & inventory**: Set the product’s price and inventory quantity. The system will automatically track inventory changes.
7. **SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)**: Assign a unique SKU to each product variant for accurate inventory tracking. Click **Set the product SKU or barcode** to expand and fill in the details.
8. **Shipping & customs info**: Enter the product’s weight to calculate shipping rates. For international shipping, customs information can also be added. By default, customs info is not required. Once you check **Add customs details**, fields for **Country/Region of origin** and **HS code** will appear for you to complete.

For additional settings, refer to the [Add product](./operate-product-create.md) page. Once setup is complete, click **Save** to publish the product.

## Manage physical products

### Edit a physical product

1. In the **Product list** page, locate the product you want to edit and click **Edit**.
2. Modify the necessary fields, such as product description, inventory, and pricing.
3. Click **Save** to apply the changes.

### Remove a physical product

1. In the **Product list** page, find the product you want to delete and click **Delete**.
2. You can also **select multiple products for bulk deletion**.
3. Click **Confirm** in the popup to proceed, and the product will be removed from the store.
