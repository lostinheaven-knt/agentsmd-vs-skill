# App types

You can enhance your Genstore admin or add new features to your store by installing apps. This section explains the different types of apps you can add to your Genstore store.

## Public apps

Public apps have passed Genstore's review process and are available for installation through the Genstore app store.

- **Genstore apps**: These apps are developed and supported by Genstore. They are typically free and highly compatible with the platform, providing an optimal user experience.
- **Third-party apps**: These apps are developed by Genstore partners. After passing review, they are available in the app store. Support for these apps is provided by the app developers.

## Custom apps

[Custom apps](./apps-type-custom.md) can extend the functionality of your Genstore admin or expand your online store to other platforms through custom storefronts.

- **Exclusive development**: Custom apps are developed specifically for your Genstore store and are not listed in the Genstore App Store. They are designed to meet your unique business needs.
- **External development support**: External developers can create custom apps for you without requiring access to your Genstore admin or store API permissions.
- **Installation method**: You can easily add custom apps to your Genstore store through a dedicated installation link provided by the app developer.
