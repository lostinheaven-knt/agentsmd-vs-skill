# Conversion by product

**The conversion by product report** offers insights into **product performance through sales and conversion data**, helping you identify which products effectively drive revenue and customer interest.

## Use this report to

- **Product detail page trends**: Visual representation of how product page visits and conversions change over time. This helps you identify traffic fluctuations and their correlation with sales performance.
- **Product detail page details**: In-depth information for each product, including the metrics listed above. This data helps you analyze and compare product performance more effectively.

## Report data fields

| **Field**                    | **Description**                                                |
|-----------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Product title**           | Name of the product.                                           |
| **Product ID**              | Unique identifier for the product.                             |
| **Product image**           | Visual representation of the product.                          |
| **Sessions**                | Total sessions to the product detail page.                     |
| **Visitors**                | Total number of unique visitors to the product page.           |
| **Sessions with add-to-carts** | Total sessions where customers added the product to their cart. |
| **Reached checkout**        | Total sessions during which customers proceeded to checkout.   |
| **Sessions converted**      | Total sessions that resulted in a purchase.                    |
| **Conversion rate**         | Percentage of sessions that resulted in a purchase.            |
| **Quantity**                | Total number of units sold.                                    |
| **Product gross sales**     | Total revenue from the product.                                |
| **Add-to-cart rate**        | Percentage of sessions where the product was added to the cart.|
