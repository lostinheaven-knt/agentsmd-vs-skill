# Deactivate your store

As the **store owner**, if your store meets the required conditions, you can cancel your subscription and deactivate your store at any time via the Genstore admin.

Before deactivating your store, please make sure to read and understand the [Terms of Service](https://www.genstore.ai/legal/terms) to ensure you're aware of the impact.

## Cancel your subscription and deactivate your store

::: tip

Deactivating your store will immediately cancel your subscription. You will lose access to the admin. To reactivate the store later, you’ll need to re-subscribe to a paid plan.

:::

### Steps to deactivate your store

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Settings -> Plan and credits.**
3. In the **Subscription** section, click **Deactivate store.**
4. Select a reason for closing, and optionally provide additional comments.
5. Click **Continue** to confirm the deactivation.

After the store is deactivated, you can still access and download past invoices.
 Your store data—including sales, product, and customer information—will be retained for **two years**, allowing you to reactivate your store at any time.

## Reactivate your store

If you decide to reopen your store, you can log in at any time and choose a plan to reactivate.

### Steps to reactivate

1. Log in to the Genstore admin and select your deactivated store.
2. Click **Continue subscription** or **Change plan.**
3. Complete the payment to reactivate your store.

## Store closure checklist

### Account access and visibility

|Item|Description|
|---|---|
|Admin access|The store will be marked as "Inactive" and the store admin will no longer be accessible.|
|Linked account|Your account remains active and can be used to reactivate the store|
|Data retention|Store data will be retained for 2 years; data will be automatically deleted after that period|
|Storefront view|Visitors will see a "Store Closed" message; recommend linking to new store or contact info|
|Domain handling|Both the default and custom `genmystore.com` domains cannot be reused.  <br />For third-party domains, please remove bindings in advance.  <br />Path: **Genstore admin -> Settings -> Domains**|

::: tip

**Unique domain rule**

When creating a store, the system assigns a default `xxx.genmystore.com` domain. You can customize it once in Genstore admin. However, whether it’s the default or a modified version, **once the store is closed, the domain can no longer be used again**.

**Example:**  
If you close a store named **Very Good Things** with the URL  
`very-good-things.genmystore.com`, you can still reuse the brand name when creating a new store, but **you won’t be able to use the same domain**.

:::

### Payments and billing

#### For **Genstore Payments** users

|Item|Restriction|
|---|---|
|New payments|Customers can no longer initiate payments after the store is closed|
|Payouts|Pending settlement funds will be processed as usual and deposited into the merchant's bank account according to the payment settings configured prior to store closure|
|Refunds|Refunds and manual captures cannot be processed from the admin|
|Chargebacks|Merchants cannot dispute chargebacks—**all disputes will be automatically accepted**|

#### For **third-party payment** users

|Item|Restriction|
|---|---|
|New payments|Payments cannot be initiated once the store is closed|
|Refunds & disputes|Cannot be handled via Genstore—**must be processed directly through the third-party payment provider**|

#### For **manual payment** users

|Item|Restriction|
|---|---|
|New orders|Manual payment methods will be disabled after closure|
|Post-sale|Refund and return flows cannot be performed|

### Orders and fulfillment

|Status|Restriction|
|---|---|
|Incomplete orders|Cannot proceed with payment, fulfillment, export, etc.|
|After-sales flows|Refund, return, and exchange processes will be disabled|
|Purchased labels only|Strongly recommend shipping or voiding labels in time to avoid waste|
|Shipped orders|Tracking updates continue, but after-sales support will no longer be available|

### Marketing and sales channels

|Item|Status|
|---|---|
|Email campaigns|All email tasks that have not yet entered the sending queue will be paused and converted to drafts. Emails that are already in the sending process will be sent as scheduled and billed accordingly.|
|Automation flows|All flows will be paused|
|Scheduled posts|All scheduled social posts will be cancelled and saved as drafts|
|Popups|All active popup campaigns will be taken offline|
|SEO functions|All SEO scans and robots.txt rules will be disabled|
|Channel sync|All sales channel authorizations will be disconnected; product sync will stop|

### Export important assets

|Item|Details|
|---|---|
|Gift cards|Please export gift card data before closing the store.  <br />Path: **Store -> Products -> Gift cards -> Export gift cards**|
|Themes|You can export theme files for use in another store.  <br />Path: **Store -> Online Store -> Themes**, click `...` -> **Download theme file**|
