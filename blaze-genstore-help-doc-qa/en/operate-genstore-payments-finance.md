# Finance

For merchants who have activated Genstore Payments, Genstore offers powerful financial management features to help users efficiently monitor and manage their financial activities.

## How to access

To access financial functions:

1. Log in to the Genstore admin.
2. In the left navigation bar, click **Finance**.

From the **Finance** page, you can manage your account balance and payment operations in detail.

::: tip
You must first activate **Genstore Payments** to use the **Finance** feature.
:::
## Process payments

[Payments](./operate-genstore-payments-finance-payments.md) are essential for processing order transactions through Genstore Payments. The **payment processing** section covers:

- Setting up payment methods and related account information.
- Viewing and managing transaction statuses to track payments for each order.
- Using the dashboard and payment details to monitor transactions and payment statuses.
- Understanding the impact of payment risk scores, refunds, and chargebacks on your account.

## Manage balance

[Balance](./operate-genstore-payments-finance-balance.md) represents the total funds in your account. The **balance management** section explains how to:

- View and track your account balance.
- Interpret key indicators and data points shown on the balance dashboard.
- Effectively handle adjustments and resolve discrepancies.

## Process payouts

[Payouts](./operate-genstore-payments-finance-payout.md) are the process of transferring funds from your Genstore account to a designated account. The **payout processing** section covers:

- Setting up payout methods and qualification requirements.
- Applying for and tracking payouts.
- Understanding payout schedules, fees, and potential delays.
