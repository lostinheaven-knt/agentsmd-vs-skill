# Automatically create product collections

The **Genstore** platform allows you to automatically add products that meet specific conditions to a designated collection. You can set conditions based on product type, price, tags, inventory status, and more, simplifying product management—especially for large product volumes and dynamic inventory changes.

When creating an automatic product collection, you’ll define the products that meet your conditions. You can create up to 5000 automatic product collections:

- **Configurable conditions:** Conditions include product type, price range, tags, inventory status, title keywords, etc.
- **Condition type:** Choose whether products must meet **all conditions** or **any condition** to be included in the collection.
- **Maximum conditions:** Each collection can have up to 60 filter conditions.

## Create a product collection

1. Log in to the **Genstore admin** and go to **Store** -> **Products** -> **Collections**.
2. Click **Create product collection** in the top-right corner.
3. Enter the collection’s **title** and **description**.
4. Under **Type**, select **Automatic** and set the automatic conditions:
	- Choose whether products must meet **all conditions** or **any condition** to be included in the collection.
	- Click **Add conditions**.
	- Use the dropdown menu to set collection conditions. For more details on filter conditions, refer to [**Automatic filtering conditions**](./operate-product-collections-condition.md).
5. Set the display order for products in an automated collection:
	- By default, products are sorted by create date.
	- You can also sort by price, title, best-selling, and other options.
    - If you choose **manual sorting**, you can rearrange products manually.  **Note:** If you later update the collection’s filter conditions, the manual sort order will be reset and needs to be reconfigured.
6. SEO settings:
	- **Page title**: By default, it uses the collection title, but you can edit it. Recommended length: 0-70 characters.
	   - **Meta description**: By default, it uses the collection description, but you can edit it. Recommended length: 0-200 characters.
	   - **URL handle**: Generated by default, up to 255 characters. You can modify it. Conversion rules:
		 - Remove punctuation and special characters
		 - Replace consecutive spaces/underscores with `-`
		 - Replace consecutive `-` with a single `-`
		 - Convert uppercase to lowercase
7. The product collection will be automatically published to your online store.
8. Under **Cover image**, add a cover image for the collection. You can choose one from the resource library, upload via link, or upload from your computer.
9. Click **Save** to finalize the product collection creation.

## Manage product collections

All created product collections will appear on the **Product collections** page list, where you can:

1. **Edit collection:** Click **Edit** in the **Actions** column to update the collection’s title, description, image, filter conditions, and SEO settings.
2. **Preview collection:** Click **Preview** in the **Actions** column to see the products included in the collection.
3. **Duplicate collection:** Click **Duplicate** in the **Actions** column to quickly duplicate the current collection. You can later adjust settings from the collection details page or by clicking the edit button in the list.
4. **Delete collection:** Click **Delete** in the **Actions** column to remove the collection. You can also select multiple collections using checkboxes to delete them in bulk.

## FAQs

### Q1: Can I convert a manually created product collection to an automatic one?
> A: This feature is not currently supported, but you can create a new collection as automatic.
