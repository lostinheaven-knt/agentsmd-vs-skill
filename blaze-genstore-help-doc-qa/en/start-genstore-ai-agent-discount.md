# Campaign agent
Campaign agent Olivia is your chief marketing strategist. Through AI collaboration, you can quickly create high-converting discount campaigns that automatically sync to your store’s announcement bar. Whether you’re clearing out inventory or aiming to increase your average order value (AOV), the AI provides a fully automated marketing experience.

With the campaign agent, you can skip complex rule settings and simply tell the AI to build a high-performance promotion for you.

## Competitive campaign creation
- **Best for**: Matching industry trends or benchmarking against top market players.
- **Example prompt**: *"Create a discount campaign based on a market competitor analysis."*
- **AI execution**: The AI analyzes top competitors in your category and combines those insights with your store's sales trends to generate a highly competitive discount proposal.
  - **Visual sync**: The AI designs your homepage announcement bar to match the campaign, instantly creating a sense of urgency for shoppers.
  - **Auto-scheduling**: Your sale goes live and expires automatically on your set schedule, keeping your store's information accurate without manual updates.

## Direct smart creation
- **Best for**: Specific sales goals like Black Friday, new arrivals, or end-of-season clearance.
- **Example prompt**: *"Help me set up a Black Friday sale: 10% off orders over $500."*
- **AI execution**: The AI builds the campaign framework based on your goal, including setting the threshold and discount amount.
  - **Visual sync**: The AI designs your homepage announcement bar to match the campaign, instantly creating a sense of urgency for shoppers.
  - **Auto-scheduling**: Your sale goes live and expires automatically on your set schedule, keeping your store's information accurate without manual updates.
