# Marketing

You can view key marketing data to help plan your marketing strategies, and leverage Genstore's extensive marketing tools to execute your plans. The app store also offers a variety of applications such as countdowns, badges, and pop-up marketing, which can assist in driving conversions.

## Topics of this chapter

- [View marketing campaign performance](./expand-marketing-analysis.md):
	- In Genstore reports, you can analyze traffic, orders, revenue, and conversion rates from email marketing, social media marketing, automated marketing, and more, helping you assess the effectiveness of your campaigns.
	- If you've integrated **Facebook or Google**, you can also access detailed analytics on ad clicks, audience insights, and conversion paths from their respective platforms. To set up data sharing with Facebook or Google, visit:
		- [Set up Facebook data sharing](./expand-marketing-meta-data-sharing.md)
		- [Set up Google data sharing](./expand-marketing-google-data-sharing.md)
- Marketing tools
  - [Email marketing](./expand-marketing-email.md): Use email to promote products, services, or your brand to specific customer segments.
  - [SMS marketing](./expand-marketing-sms.md): Genstore SMS marketing offers a free trial and transparent pricing based on country and message volume — no hidden fees.
  - [Social media marketing](./expand-marketing-social-media.md): Connect to various social media platforms and create marketing content directly in the Genstore admin for one-click publishing.
  - [Marketing automation](./expand-marketing-automated-email-overview.md): Set trigger rules and workflows, and the system will automatically execute your marketing tasks.
  - [Integrate third-party email marketing tools](./expand-marketing-email-3rd-edm.md): Genstore supports seamless integration with leading email marketing platforms, including **Klaviyo**, **Mailchimp**, and **Omnisend**.
  - [Affiliate marketing](./expand-marketing-affiliate-goaffpro.md): Earn commission from your customers' referrals with Genstore GOAFFPRO.
