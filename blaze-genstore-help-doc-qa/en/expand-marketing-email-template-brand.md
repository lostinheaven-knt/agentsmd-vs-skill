# Set email visual styles

With Genstore’s brand styling tools, you can define your brand’s colors and fonts once and apply them consistently across all your email campaigns. From primary tones to typography, every message stays visually aligned with your brand—no matter the layout or marketing goal.

- **Strengthen your brand identity with consistent visuals**: Predefined light and dark modes keep your emails visually aligned with your brand tone.
- **Apply your brand colors instantly**: Set them once and use them across all email templates.
- **Customize flexibly for different use cases**: Beyond global branding, you can fine-tune styles for individual campaigns or components like buttons and text blocks.
## Key features

|Level|Description|
|---|---|
|**Brand level**|Set a unified color scheme for all templates, with support for light and dark modes.|
|**Campaign level**|Customize colors for specific campaigns to better match their themes.|
|**Component level**|Apply different color settings to specific components for more creative control.|

## How to set it up

### Set up a unified brand template

1. In the Genstore admin, go to **Marketing -> Email marketing**.
2. Click **Create email campaign** in the top right.
3. On the **Email template** page, select **Settings**.
4. Click the **Custom** button next to **Set brand style**, then you’ll be able to:
    - Upload or update your **logo**.
    - Choose a **font** (web-safe fonts supported)
    - Define **primary, secondary, accent**, and **neutral** colors for both **light** and **dark** modes
        > Note: The system will use the brand colors you've saved in [Brand assets](./start-genstore-account-brand-assets.md) as the default.
5. Use the live preview on the right to see changes instantly:
    - Logo placement
    - Text and heading colors
    - Font style
6. When finished, click **Save**.

### Optional: Customize individual emails or components

For special promotions or seasonal campaigns, you can go beyond the base template:

- Adjust the color scheme for a **specific campaign**
- Set unique styles for **individual components**, like a banner or CTA button

## Tips

- Define your core brand colors in [Brand assets](./start-genstore-account-brand-assets.md) before customizing.
- Make sure your color choices are accessible and visually balanced to ensure a great reading experience.
