# Gift card

A **gift card** is a prepaid voucher that can be redeemed in your online store or at physical locations. Merchants can create gift cards in the admin and send them to customers by email. Customers can view their balance, copy the redemption code, or use a QR code for in-store redemption.

## How to access

1. Log in to your Genstore admin.
2. Go to **Store** -> **Online Store** -> **Themes**.
3. Find your theme and click **Customize** to open the theme editor.
4. In the top navigation bar, open the page selector and choose **Gift card**.
## Features

- Displays brand name, gift card value, expiration date, redemption code, and QR code.
- Supports online use and in-store scanning for redemption.
## Current limitations

The gift card page does not currently support customization such as style changes, layout modifications, or adding/removing sections. Additional customization options may be available in future updates.
